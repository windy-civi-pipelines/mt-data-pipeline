4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE TRANSFER OF TITLE OF OPEN SPACES
5 IN A RECORDED FINAL PLAT TO A HOMEOWNERS' ASSOCIATION; REQUIRING A PUBLIC HEARING;
6 PROVIDING A DEFINITION; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 76-3-103, 76-8-101, 76-25-103, AND 76-25-411,
7 MCA.”

9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Transfer of title of open spaces in recorded final plat. (1) After a final
12 subdivision plat has been filed for record with the county clerk and recorder, a homeowners' association may
13 file a petition with the governing body to transfer title to the homeowners' association of any open spaces,
14 parks, or similar common-use areas set aside in the final plat if the following conditions are met:
15 (a) the homeowners' association is organized in accordance with the covenants;
16 (b) the covenants require the homeowners' association to maintain, repair, or insure the open
17 spaces, parks, or similar common-use areas; and
18 (c) the governing body finds that it was the intent of the subdivider that the open spaces, parks, or
19 similar common-use areas be owned by the homeowners' association; AND
20 (D) THE PROPERTY TO BE TRANSFERRED IS NOT OWNED BY A PERSON.
21 (2) The petition under subsection (1) must be signed by an authorized representative of the
22 homeowners' association.
23 (3) After a public hearing on the petition, the governing body shall issue written findings of fact and
24 a decision based on the record. If the requirements of subsection (1) have been met, the governing body shall
25 approve the transfer. If the governing body approves the transfer, the approval must be recorded with the clerk
26 and recorder.
