- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0368.2
2 INTRODUCED BY G. PARRY
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING A COAL-FIRED GENERATING UNIT OPERATOR TO
5 PROVIDE PERPETUAL PROVIDING ACCESS TO WATER FOR AN ATTENDANT CITY OR TOWN;
6 CREATING THE TOWN OF COLSTRIP WATER INFRASTRUCTURE MITIGATION ACCOUNT; AND
7 AMENDING SECTION 15-35-108, MCA; CLARIFYING THE FINANCIAL ASSURANCE NECESSARY TO
8 PROVIDE WATER TO AN ATTENDANT CITY OR TOWN; REVISING A DEFINITION; AND AMENDING
9 SECTIONS 75-8-103 AND 75-10-721, MCA.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 (Refer to Introduced Bill)
14 Strike everything after the enacting clause and insert:
15
16 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Town of Colstrip water infrastructure mitigation account. (1) There is
17 a town of Colstrip water infrastructure mitigation account within the state special revenue fund established in
18 17-2-102.
19 (2) There must be deposited in the account money allocated pursuant to 15-35-108(11).
20 (3) Money deposited in the account must be transferred to the town of Colstrip and may be used to
21 pay for costs to ensure a water supply is available to the town if the proximate coal-fired generating unit closes
22 or retires, and the town is unable to access a water supply.
23 (4) The department shall review this account every 10 years to confirm the funding level remains
24 sufficient to meet the purposes of this section. The department shall report its finding to the economic affairs
25 interim committee in accordance with 5-11-210.
26
27 Section 2. Section 15-35-108, MCA, is amended to read:
28 "15-35-108. (Temporary) Disposal of severance taxes. Severance taxes collected under this

1 chapter must, in accordance with the provisions of 17-2-124, be allocated as follows:
2 (1) Fifty percent of total coal severance tax collections is allocated to the trust fund created by
3 Article IX, section 5, of the Montana constitution. The trust fund money must be deposited in the fund
4 established under 17-6-203(6) and invested by the board of investments as provided by law.

6 building program account established in 17-7-221.

8 thereafter must be allocated for provision of basic library services for the residents of all counties through library
9 federations and for payment of the costs of participating in regional and national networking and must be
10 deposited in the basic library services account established in 22-1-202.
11 (4) The amount of 3.77% in fiscal year 2020 and 3.71% in fiscal year 2021 and in each fiscal year
12 thereafter must be allocated to the department of natural resources and conservation for conservation districts
13 and deposited in the conservation district account established in 76-15-106.
14 (5) The amount of 0.79% in fiscal year 2020 and 0.82% in fiscal year 2021 and in each fiscal year
15 thereafter must be allocated to the Montana Growth Through Agriculture Act and deposited in the growth
16 through agriculture account established in 90-9-104.
17 (6) The amount of 1.27% must be allocated to a permanent fund account for the purpose of parks
18 acquisition or management. Income from this permanent fund account, excluding unrealized gains and losses,
19 must be appropriated for the acquisition, development, operation, and maintenance of any sites and areas
20 described in 23-1-102.
21 (7) The amount of 0.95% must be allocated to the debt service fund type to the credit of the
22 renewable resource loan debt service fund.
23 (8) The amount of 0.63% must be allocated to a trust fund for the purpose of protection of works of
24 art in the capitol and for other cultural and aesthetic projects. Income from this trust fund account, excluding
25 unrealized gains and losses, must be appropriated for protection of works of art in the state capitol and for other
26 cultural and aesthetic projects.
27 (9) The amount of 5.8% must be credited to the coal natural resource account established in 90-6-
28 1001(2).
