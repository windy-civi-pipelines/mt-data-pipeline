69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Julie Johnson, HB0234.001.001
1 HOUSE BILL NO. 234
2 INTRODUCED BY P. TUSS, J. ELLIS, L. DEMING, B. BARKER, C. KEOGH, G. NIKOLAKAKOS, M.
3 ROMANO, M. THANE
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT AUTHORIZING REAPPROPRIATIONS FOR PROJECTS;
6 AMENDING SECTION 5, CHAPTER 763, LAWS OF 2023; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE
7 DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 5, Chapter 763, Laws of 2023, is amended to include a new subsection (11) as
12 follows:
13 "Section 5. Capital improvement projects.
14 (11) The For the biennium ending June 30, 2027, the appropriations in subsection (10) are
15 reappropriated for the purposes of the original appropriation until the funds are fully expended or the project is
16 completed."
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
19 - END -
