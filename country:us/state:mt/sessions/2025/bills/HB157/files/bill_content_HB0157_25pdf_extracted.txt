- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0157.2
2 INTRODUCED BY K. KORTUM, E. BUTTREY
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING ALCOHOL LAWS RELATING TO THE RESPONSIBLE
5 SERVER AND SALES TRAINING PROGRAM; REQUIRING TWO TRAINING PROGRAMS RELATED TO
6 WHETHER THE SERVER IS EMPLOYED AT A LICENSED LOCATION WHERE ON-PREMISES
7 CONSUMPTION OF ALCOHOL IS ALLOWED; REQUIRING THAT A PROGRAM FOR EMPLOYEES OF
8 LICENSEES ONLY PROVIDING OFF-PREMISES CONSUMPTION MUST TAKE LESS TIME; AND
9 AMENDING SECTION 16-4-1006, MCA; AND PROVIDING A DELAYED EFFECTIVE DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 16-4-1006, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "16-4-1006. Responsible server and sales training program. (1) The Subject to subsection (2), the
15 department shall certify all server and sales training programs that include the following:
16 (a) effects of alcohol on the human body;
17 (b) information, including criminal, civil, and administrative penalties, related to 27-1-710 and this
18 code;
19 (c) procedures for checking identification;
20 (d) procedures for gathering proper documentation that may affect the licensee's liability;
21 (e) training for skills to handle difficult situations and to learn evaluation techniques regarding
22 intoxicated persons or others that pose potential liability;
23 (f) a final test; and
24 (g) a certificate of completion, which must be provided to participants who pass the final test.
25 (2) The department shall certify two classes of training programs based on the nature of
26 employment of the individual as follows:
27 (a) programs for employees of licensed locations that provide for on-premises consumption; and
28 (b) programs for employees of licensees that only provide for off-premises consumption. A training
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 157
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0157.2
1 program under this subsection (2)(b) must be completed within at least half of the time for programs under
2 subsection (2)(a).
3 (2)(3) The department may not provide a responsible server and sales training program."
4
5 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. [THIS ACT] IS EFFECTIVE JANUARY 1, 2026.
6 - END -
