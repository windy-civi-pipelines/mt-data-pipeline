13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Exemption from environmental review. The department is exempt
14 from the provisions of Title 75, chapter 1, parts 1 and 2 when reviewing water or sewer system plans or
15 specifications under this part.

9 Section 2. Section 76-4-104, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "76-4-104. Rules for administration and enforcement. (1) The department shall, subject to the
11 provisions of 76-4-135, adopt reasonable rules, including adoption of sanitary standards, necessary for
12 administration and enforcement of this part.
13 (2) The rules and standards must provide the basis for approving subdivisions for various types of
14 public and private water supplies, sewage disposal facilities, storm water drainage ways, and solid waste
15 disposal. The rules and standards must be related to:
16 (a) size of lots;
17 (b) contour of land;
18 (c) porosity of soil;
19 (d) ground water level;
20 (e) distance from lakes, streams, and wells;
21 (f) type and construction of private water and sewage facilities; and
22 (g) other factors affecting public health and the quality of water for uses relating to agriculture,
23 industry, recreation, and wildlife.

25 land that are exempt from review under 76-3-207(1)(a), (1)(d), (1)(e), or (1)(f) that:
26 (a) are used for a single-family residential purpose; and
27 (b) include no more than 25% that is impervious.
