24 (i) the name, voice, or likeness of an individual remains in use for commercial purposes by all
25 executors, heirs, transferees, or devisees; or
26 (ii) all executors, heirs, transferees, or devisees of the individual exercising the individual's
27 property rights are deceased; and

12 may believe the depiction is genuine but in a manner that the individual did not actually perform or appear, or, if

14 performance or appearance.

16 rendering of an individual's voice that is created or altered in whole or in part using digital technology and is

4 (e) "Person" means an individual, firm, association, partnership, corporation, joint stock company,

16 simulation of the voice.
