12 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO INACTIVE VOTER STATUS;
13 CLARIFYING THAT A VOTER ON THE INACTIVE LIST IS NOT MOVED TO THE ACTIVE LIST BY SIGNING
14 A PETITION FOR A STATEWIDE BALLOT ISSUE; AMENDING SECTION 13-2-222, MCA; AND PROVIDING
15 AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
18
19 Section 1. Section 13-2-222, MCA, is amended to read:
20 "13-2-222. Reactivation of elector. (1) The name of an elector must be moved by an election
21 administrator from the inactive list to the active list of a county if an elector meets the requirements for
22 registration provided in this chapter and:
23 (a) appears at a polling place in order to vote, submits an application to vote by absentee ballot in
24 a polling place election or mail ballot election, or votes in a mail ballot election conducted under Title 13,
25 chapter 19;
26 (b) notifies the county election administrator in writing of the elector's current residence, which
27 must be in that county; or
28 (c) completes a reactivation form provided by the county election administrator that provides
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 179
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 179.1
1 current address information in that county.
2 (2) The name of an elector may not be moved from the inactive list to the active list of a county by
3 the elector signing a petition for a statewide ballot issue.
4 (2)(3) After an elector has complied with subsection (1)(a), (1)(b), or (1)(c), the county election
5 administrator shall place the elector's name on the active voting list for that county.
6 (3)(4) An elector reactivated pursuant to subsection (1)(a) is a legally registered elector for purposes
7 of the election in which the elector voted."
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
10 - END -
