4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: "AN ACT PROVIDING AN INCOME TAX CREDIT FOR A PROPERTY
5 OWNER THAT RENTS A DWELLING BELOW MARKET RATE; PROVIDING THAT THE CREDIT IS
6 AVAILABLE FOR INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAXPAYERS AND CORPORATE INCOME TAXPAYERS;
7 PROVIDING THAT THE CREDIT MAY BE CARRIED FORWARD; PROVIDING DEFINITIONS; AMENDING
8 SECTION 15-30-2303, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE."
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Credit for renting dwelling below market rate. (1) A taxpayer or
13 corporation is allowed a credit against the tax imposed by chapter 30 or 31 for property owners who rent a
14 dwelling in Montana below market rate. The credit is available to a taxpayer or corporation that charges rent on
15 a dwelling of less than 110% of fair market rent for the county in which the property is located.
16 (2) The amount of the credit is equal to $200 for each $100 by which 110% of fair market rent
17 exceeds monthly rent.
18 (3) The credit allowed under this section may not exceed the taxpayer's income tax liability but
19 may be carried forward 3 years. The entire amount of the tax credit not used in the year earned must be carried
20 first to the earliest tax year in which the credit may be applied and then to each succeeding tax year.
21 (4) A dwelling for which the credit is claimed must:
22 (a) be subject to a lease agreement of not less than 1 year; and
23 (b) meet the housing quality standards for the housing choice voucher program provided for in 24
24 CFR, part 982.401.
25 (5) If the credit allowed under this section is claimed by a small business corporation, a pass-
26 through entity, or a partnership, the credit must be attributed to shareholders, owners, or partners using the
27 same proportion as used to report the entity's income or loss.
28 (6) The credit may not be claimed for a dwelling for which the rent is limited due to participation in
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – ****
****
69th Legislature 2025 LC3243.1
1 any other program that provides a benefit to the property owner for limiting rent.
2 (7) The department may disallow the credit when the landlord and tenant have not dealt at arm's
3 length.
4 (8) The taxpayer or corporation shall file with each claim for the credit a receipt or lease agreement
5 showing rent paid on the dwelling and, if rent includes utilities, information on the annual cost of the included
6 utilities. In addition, a taxpayer or corporation shall, at the request of the department, supply all additional
7 information necessary to support a claim for the credit.
8 (9) Pursuant to 5-4-104, the purpose of this credit is to incentivize landlords to provide affordable
9 rental housing.
10 (10) For the purposes of [section 2] and this section, the following definitions apply:
11 (a) "Dwelling" means a residential dwelling, manufactured home, trailer, mobile home, or unit of a
12 multiple-unit dwelling and as much of the surrounding land, but not in excess of 1 acre, as is reasonably
13 necessary for its use as a dwelling.
14 (b) (i) "Fair market rent" means the fair market rent based on the size of the dwelling as published
15 annually by the U.S. department of housing and urban development.
16 (ii) The term excludes any charges for utilities.
17 (c) "Utility" means a service provided to a dwelling that may be included in a rent payment,
18 including but not limited to energy, water and wastewater, and trash removal.
19
20 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Credit for renting dwelling below market rate. There is a credit against
21 the tax liability under this chapter for renting a dwelling below market rate as provided in [section 1].
22

3 or termination date that is not listed in this section in the biennium before the credit is scheduled to expire or
4 terminate.

18 integral part of Title 15, chapter 30, and the provisions of Title 15, chapter 30, apply to [section 1].
19 (2) [Section 2] is intended to be codified as an integral part of Title 15, chapter 31, part 1, and the
20 provisions of Title 15, chapter 31, part 1, apply to [section 2].
21
22 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Applicability. [This act] applies to income tax years beginning after
23 December 31, 2025.
