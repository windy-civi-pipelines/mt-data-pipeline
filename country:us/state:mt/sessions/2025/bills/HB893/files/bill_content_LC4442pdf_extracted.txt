69th Legislature 2025 LC 4442
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT CREATING A LOTTERY GAME TO PROVIDE PROPERTY TAX
5 ASSISTANCE; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION; PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AND
6 PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Montana millions lottery game -- proceeds to state property tax
11 assistance account. (1) The commission shall by rule determine a Montana millions lottery game to maximize
12 the net revenue paid to the state property tax assistance account provided for in [section 2 of Senate Bill No.
13 90].
14 (2) The Montana millions lottery game must:
15 (a) be held annually on July 4;
16 (b) result in four prizes of $1 million each; and
17 (c) allow for the sale of 500,000 tickets sold for $20 each.
18 (3) After paying for costs by the commission and prizes, the commission shall transfer net revenue
19 from the Montana millions lottery game tickets to the state property tax assistance account.
20 (4) The Montana millions lottery game is subject to the provisions of this chapter.
21
22 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Appropriation. There is appropriated $50,000 from the general fund to
23 the department of revenue for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, to implement the provisions of Senate Bill
24 No. 90.
25
26 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
27 integral part of Title 23, chapter 7, part 3, and the provisions of Title 23, chapter 7, part 3, apply to [section 1].
28
