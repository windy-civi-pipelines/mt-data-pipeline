5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT CREATING A JUDICIAL BRANCH INDEPENDENCE
6 INVESTIGATION COMMITTEE; PROVIDING DUTIES AND MEMBERS; TEMPORARILY SUSPENDING THE
7 TRANSPORTATION INTERIM COMMITTEE FOR THE 2027 AND 2029 BIENNIUMS; TEMPORARILY
8 TRANSFERRING THE TRANSPORTATION INTERIM COMMITTEE'S DUTIES TO THE REVENUE INTERIM
9 COMMITTEE; AMENDING SECTIONS 5-5-227 AND 5-5-223, MCA; PROVIDING APPROPRIATIONS;
10 PROVIDING FOR CONTINGENT VOIDNESS; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE AND A
11 TERMINATION DATE.”
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Judicial branch independence investigation committee. (1) (a) There
16 is a committee to investigate district court judges and supreme court justices to determine their independence
17 and the quality of their rulings and to disseminate this information. The committee may establish standards to
18 determine the sufficiency of rulings and measurements as needed.
19 (2) The committee consists of nine legislators appointed as follows:
20 (a) five members of the house of representatives appointed by the speaker of the house; and
21 (b) four members of the senate appointed by the president of the senate.
22 (3) The appointments must be made within 30 days of [the effective date of this act]. A vacancy on
23 the committee must be filled in the same manner as the original appointment.
24 (4) The committee shall elect a presiding officer and a vice presiding officer.
25 (5) The committee shall report its findings to the law and justice interim committee and disseminate
26 information broadly by any means the committee determines effective and within the limits of its budget.
27 (6) The committee may contract to perform the investigation and evaluation processes required by

6 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Temporary suspension of transportation interim committee. (1) The
7 transportation interim committee established in 5-5-223 is suspended, and may not meet, from July 1, 2025,
8 through June 30, 2029.
9 (2) The revenue interim committee shall perform the statutory duties listed in 5-5-215 for the

11 required by statute to be delivered to the transportation interim committee.

13 Section 3. Section 5-5-227, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "5-5-227. Revenue interim committee -- powers and duties -- revenue estimating and use of
15 estimates. (1) The revenue interim committee has administrative rule review, draft legislation review, program
16 evaluation, and monitoring functions for the Montana tax appeal board established in 2-15-1015 and for the
17 department of revenue and the entities attached to the department for administrative purposes, except the
18 divisions of the department that administer the Montana Alcoholic Beverage Code and the Montana Marijuana
19 Regulation and Taxation Act.
20 (2) (a) The committee must have prepared by December 1 for introduction during each regular
21 session of the legislature in which a revenue bill is under consideration an estimate of the amount of revenue
22 projected to be available for legislative appropriation.
23 (b) The committee may prepare for introduction during a special session of the legislature in which
24 a revenue bill or an appropriation bill is under consideration an estimate of the amount of projected revenue.
25 The revenue estimate is considered a subject specified in the call of a special session under 5-3-101.
26 (3) The committee's estimate, as introduced in the legislature, constitutes the legislature's current
27 revenue estimate until amended or until final adoption of the estimate by both houses. It is intended that the

18 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Appropriation. (1) There is appropriated from the general fund to the

23 as provided in [section 1(7)].
24 (2) The appropriations in subsection (1) are authorized to continue in the biennium beginning July
