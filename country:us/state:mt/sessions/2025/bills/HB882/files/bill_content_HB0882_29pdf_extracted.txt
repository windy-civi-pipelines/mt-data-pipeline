25 (3) It is lawful to consume alcoholic beverages sold as provided in subsection (2) at any place
26 within the boundaries of the golf course, whether inside or outside of a building or other structure.

15 Section 4. Section 16-6-303, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "16-6-303. Sale of liquor not purchased or transferred from agency liquor store forbidden --
17 penalty. It Except as otherwise allowed under 16-3-302(5) and 16-4-213(4)(a)(iii), it is unlawful for any licensee
18 to sell or keep for sale or have on the licensee's premises for any purpose any liquor except that purchased
19 from an agency liquor store or transferred as allowed in 16-4-404(7). Any licensee found in possession of or
20 selling and keeping for sale any liquor that was not purchased from an agency liquor store or transferred as
21 allowed in 16-4-404(7) shall, upon conviction, be punished by a fine of not less than $500 or more than $1,500,
22 by imprisonment for not less than 3 months or more than 1 year, or by both fine and imprisonment. If the
23 department is satisfied that the liquor was knowingly sold or kept for sale within the licensed premises by the
24 licensee or by the licensee's agents, servants, or employees, the department shall immediately revoke the
25 license."

15 Section 4. Section 16-6-303, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "16-6-303. Sale of liquor not purchased or transferred from agency liquor store forbidden --
17 penalty. It Except as otherwise allowed under 16-3-302(5) and 16-4-213(4)(a)(iii), it is unlawful for any licensee
18 to sell or keep for sale or have on the licensee's premises for any purpose any liquor except that purchased
19 from an agency liquor store or transferred as allowed in 16-4-404(7). Any licensee found in possession of or
20 selling and keeping for sale any liquor that was not purchased from an agency liquor store or transferred as
21 allowed in 16-4-404(7) shall, upon conviction, be punished by a fine of not less than $500 or more than $1,500,
22 by imprisonment for not less than 3 months or more than 1 year, or by both fine and imprisonment. If the
23 department is satisfied that the liquor was knowingly sold or kept for sale within the licensed premises by the
24 licensee or by the licensee's agents, servants, or employees, the department shall immediately revoke the
25 license."
