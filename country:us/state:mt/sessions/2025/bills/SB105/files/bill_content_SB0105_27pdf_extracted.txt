- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 SB0105.2
2 INTRODUCED BY J. WINDY BOY, S. WEBBER, M. YAKAWICH
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROHIBITING ELECTIONEERING AT ALL POLLING LOCATIONS
5 FOR ALL ELECTIONS FROM THE TIME ABSENTEE BALLOTS ARE AVAILABLE UNTIL ALL VOTING IS
6 COMPLETED; AMENDING SECTION 13-35-211, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE
7 DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 13-35-211, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "13-35-211. Electioneering -- soliciting information from electors. (1) A From the start of in-
13 person absentee voting as provided in 13-1-404, 13-1-503, 13-13-205, and 20-20-401, and until the last person
14 in line at a polling location on election day has voted or the polls close, whichever is later, a A person may not
15 do any electioneering on election day within any polling place or any building in which an election is being held
16 AT ANY LOCATION WHERE AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, or within 100 feet of any entrance to the A
17 building in which the polling place is located AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, DURING THE HOURS THE
18 LOCATION IS OPEN TO OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, that aids or promotes the success or defeat of any candidate or
19 ballot issue to be voted upon on at the election.
20 (2) On election day, During the timeframe prescribed in subsection (1), a A candidate, a family
21 member of a candidate, or a worker or volunteer for the candidate's campaign may not distribute alcohol,
22 tobacco, food, drink, or anything of value to a voter within a polling place or a building in which an election is
23 being held IN ANY LOCATION WHERE AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, or within 100 feet of an entrance to
24 the A building in which the polling place is located AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, DURING THE HOURS
25 THE LOCATION IS OPEN TO ELECTORS TO OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT.
26 (3) A person may not buy, sell, give, wear, or display at or about the polls on a n election day
27 during the timeframe prescribed in subsection (1) ANY LOCATION WHERE AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A
28 BALLOT, DURING THE HOURS THE LOCATION IS OPEN TO ELECTORS TO OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, any badge, button,
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – SB 105

1 or other insignia that is designed or tends to aid or promote the success or defeat of any candidate or ballot
2 issue to be voted upon on at the election.
3 (4) A person within a polling place or any building in which an election is being held A LOCATION
4 WHERE AN ELECTOR MAY OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, DURING THE HOURS THE LOCATION IS OPEN TO ELECTORS TO
5 OBTAIN OR VOTE A BALLOT, may not solicit from an elector, before or after the elector has marked a ballot and
6 returned it to an election judge, information as to whether the elector intends to vote or has voted for or against
7 a candidate or ballot issue."
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Notification to tribal governments. The secretary of state shall send a
10 copy of [this act] to each federally recognized tribal government in Montana.
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval JULY 1,
13 2025.
14 - END -
