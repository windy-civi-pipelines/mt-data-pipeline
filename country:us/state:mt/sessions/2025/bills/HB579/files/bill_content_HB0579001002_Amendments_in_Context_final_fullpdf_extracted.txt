17 establish and continually fund a capital reserve account meeting the requirements of subsection (3) for

23 potential funding.

25 (a) except as provided in subsection (4), an amount equal to 10% of the revenues received by the
26 local government from water supply fees, wastewater system usage fees, or water delivery fees assessed by
27 the local government; or
