69th Legislature 2025 HB 22.1
2 INTRODUCED BY M. ROMANO
3 BY REQUEST OF THE EDUCATION INTERIM COMMITTEE
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ALIGNING THE HOME VALUE AMOUNTS FOR SCHOOL
6 DISTRICT PROPERTY TAX IMPACT STATEMENTS WITH OTHERS IN STATUTE; AND AMENDING
7 SECTION 20-9-116, MCA.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 20-9-116, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "20-9-116. (Temporary) Resolution of intent to increase nonvoted levy -- notice. (1) The trustees
13 of a school district shall adopt a resolution no later than March 31 of each fiscal year and provide notice
14 pursuant to subsection (2) whenever the trustees intend to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy in the
15 ensuing school fiscal year for the purposes of funding any of the funds listed below:
16 (a) the tuition fund under 20-5-324;
17 (b) the adult education fund under 20-7-705;
18 (c) the transportation fund under 20-10-143 and 20-10-144;
19 (d) the bus depreciation reserve fund under 20-10-147; and
20 (e) the flexibility fund established in 20-9-543 for the purposes in 20-7-1602.
21 (2) The trustees shall provide notice of intent to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy for the
22 ensuing school fiscal year by:
23 (a) adopting a resolution of intent to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy that includes, at a
24 minimum, the estimated number of increased or decreased mills to be imposed and the estimated increased or
25 decreased revenue to be raised compared to nonvoted levies under subsections (1)(a) through (1)(e) imposed
26 in the current school fiscal year and, based on the district's taxable valuation most recently certified by the
27 department of revenue under 15-10-202, the estimated impacts of the increase or decrease on a home valued
28 at $100,000 and a home valued at $200,000 homes valued at $100,000, $300,000, and $600,000; and

1 (b) publishing in a newspaper that will give notice to the largest number of people of the district as
2 determined by the trustees and posting to the school district's website:
3 (i) the resolution under subsection (2)(a); and
4 (ii) the resolution under 20-9-502(3)(a)(i) if adopted by the trustees. (Terminates June 30, 2027--
5 sec. 7, Ch. 402, L. 2019.)
6 20-9-116. (Effective July 1, 2027) Resolution of intent to increase nonvoted levy -- notice. (1)
7 The trustees of a school district shall adopt a resolution no later than June 1 in fiscal year 2017 only and no
8 later than March 31 in fiscal year 2018 and subsequent fiscal years and provide notice pursuant to subsection
9 (2) whenever the trustees intend to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy in the ensuing school fiscal year for
10 the purposes of funding any of the funds listed below:
11 (a) the tuition fund under 20-5-324;
12 (b) the adult education fund under 20-7-705;
13 (c) the transportation fund under 20-10-143 and 20-10-144; and
14 (d) the bus depreciation reserve fund under 20-10-147.
15 (2) The trustees shall provide notice of intent to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy for the
16 ensuing school fiscal year by:
17 (a) adopting a resolution of intent to impose an increase in a nonvoted levy that includes, at a
18 minimum, the estimated number of increased or decreased mills to be imposed and the estimated increased or
19 decreased revenue to be raised compared to nonvoted levies under subsections (1)(a) through (1)(d) imposed
20 in the current school fiscal year and, based on the district's taxable valuation most recently certified by the
21 department of revenue under 15-10-202, the estimated impacts of the increase or decrease on a home valued
22 at $100,000 and a home valued at $200,000 homes valued at $100,000, $300,000, and $600,000; and
23 (b) publishing in a newspaper that will give notice to the largest number of people of the district as
24 determined by the trustees and posting to the school district's website:
25 (i) the resolution under subsection (2)(a); and
26 (ii) the resolution under 20-9-502(3)(a)(i) if adopted by the trustees."
