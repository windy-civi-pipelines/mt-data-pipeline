Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 918.1
2 INTRODUCED BY J. GILLETTE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE PROVISIONS OF THE GENERAL
5 APPROPRIATIONS ACT; REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
6 TO REPORT ON THE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF A PLAN TO COMPLY WITH THE
7 PRINCIPLES OF THE OLMSTEAD DECISION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE AND A
8 TERMINATION DATE.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Olmstead plan -- quarterly reporting. During the interim following the
13 69th legislative session, the department of public health and human services shall provide a report to the health
14 and human services budget committee at each committee meeting on the department's efforts to develop a
15 plan to implement the United States supreme court's decision in Olmstead v. L.C., 527 U.S. 581 (1999),
16 pursuant to section 1, Chapter 554, Laws of 2023.
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
19
20 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Termination. [This act] terminates September 15, 2026.
21 - END -
