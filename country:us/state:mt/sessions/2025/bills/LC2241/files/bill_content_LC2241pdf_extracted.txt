4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING LAWS RELATED TO WEATHER
5 MODIFICATION; PROHIBITING WEATHER MODIFICATION; PROVIDING FOR A CAUSE OF ACTION;
6 PROVIDING DEFINITIONS; AND REPEALING SECTIONS 85-3-101, 85-3-102, 85-3-103, 85-3-104, 85-3-105,
7 85-3-201, 85-3-202, 85-3-203, 85-3-204, 85-3-205, 85-3-206, 85-3-207, 85-3-208, 85-3-209, 85-3-210, 85-3-
8 211, 85-3-212, 85-3-213, 85-3-214, 85-3-301, 85-3-302, 85-3-303, 85-3-401, 85-3-411, 85-3-412, 85-3-413, 85-
9 3-414, 85-3-415, 85-3-416, 85-3-417, 85-3-421, 85-3-422, 85-3-423, AND 85-3-424, MCA.”
10
11 WHEREAS, it is documented that the federal government or other entities acting on the federal
12 government's behalf or at the federal government's request may conduct geoengineering experiments by
13 intentionally dispersing chemicals into the atmosphere, and those activities may occur within the State of
14 Montana; and
15 WHEREAS, the risk to human health and environmental welfare from broad scale geoengineering is
16 currently not well understood; and
17 WHEREAS, the Montana Department of Natural Resources and Conservation is responsible for
18 monitoring air, soil, and water quality and regulating industrial and agricultural emissions into the air, soil, and
19 water within the State of Montana to ensure the safety of the public, while not impeding agriculture or
20 commerce within the state; and
21 WHEREAS, it is the intent of the Legislature to protect the public health and welfare of Montanans.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
24
25 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Prohibiting weather modification. (1) A person may not intentionally
26 inject, release, or disperse chemicals, chemical compounds, substances, or apparatuses within the borders of
27 the state from an aircraft expressly for:
28 (a) engaging in weather modification and control;
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – ****
****
69th Legislature 2025 LC2241.1
1 (b) affecting temperature; or
2 (c) affecting the intensity of the sunlight.
3 (2) The department of justice, established in 2-15-2001, shall establish and make publicly available
4 on its website an e-mail address and an online form for persons to report observed violations pursuant to this
5 section.
6 (3) The department of justice may adopt rules necessary to implement subsection (2).
7 (4) As used in this section, the following definitions apply:
8 (a) "Aircraft" means any manned or unmanned aerial vehicle or device that is used or intended to
9 be used for flight in the air.
10 (b) "Weather modification and control" means changing or controlling or attempting to change or
11 control by artificial methods the natural development of atmospheric cloud forms or precipitation forms that
12 occur in the troposphere.
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Enforcement. When the department of justice believes that a violation of
15 [section 1] has occurred, it may initiate action under 75-2-413. The department of justice may pursue
16 enforcement action against the federal government for a violation of [section 1].
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Repealer. The following sections of the Montana Code Annotated are
19 repealed:
20 85-3-101. Definitions.
21 85-3-102. Standards for research in weather modification control.
22 85-3-103. Department powers.
23 85-3-104. Nonliability of state and agents for acts of private persons.
