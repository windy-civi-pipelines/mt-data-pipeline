1 ARTIFICIAL LIGHT OR NIGHT VISION SCOPES.
2 (c) The unlimited statewide quota provided for in subsection (2)(a) does not apply to the
3 geographic area established as wolf management units 313 and 316 in the 2024 version of the wolf and
4 furbearer trapping and hunting regulations published by the department. Units 313 and 316 must be managed
5 pursuant to 87-1-304 (7) and (8).
6 (3) The commission shall adopt rules to allow a landowner or the landowner's agent to take a wolf
7 on the landowner's property at any time without the purchase of a Class E-1 or Class E-2 wolf license when the
8 wolf is a potential threat to human safety, livestock, or dogs. The rules must:
9 (a) be consistent with the Montana gray wolf conservation and management plan and the adaptive
10 management principles of the commission and the department for the Montana gray wolf population;
11 (b) require a landowner or the landowner's agent who takes a wolf pursuant to this subsection (3)
12 to promptly report the taking to the department and to preserve the carcass of the wolf;
13 (c) establish a quota each year for the total number of wolves that may be taken pursuant to this
14 subsection (3); and
15 (d) allow the commission to issue a moratorium on the taking of wolves pursuant to this subsection
16 (3) before a quota is reached if the commission determines that circumstances require a limitation of the total
17 number of wolves taken.
18 (4) Public land permittees who have experienced livestock depredation must obtain a special kill
19 permit authorized in 87-5-131(3)(b) to take a wolf on public land without the purchase of a Class E-1 or Class
20 E-2 license.
21 (5) When determining wolf populations using integrated patch occupancy model estimates, the
22 department shall use the same modeling criteria for estimates made for subsequent years to ensure the data
23 results are able to be interpreted consistently. THE DEPARTMENT MAY USE NEW MODELING TECHNIQUES IN
24 CONJUNCTION WITH THE INTEGRATED PATCH OCCUPANCY MODEL TO ESTIMATE THE WOLF POPULATION MORE
25 ACCURATELY.
26 (5)(6) The department shall report annually to the environmental quality council in accordance with 5-
27 11-210 regarding the implementation of 87-5-131, 87-5-132, and this section."
