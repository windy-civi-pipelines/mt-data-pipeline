6 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Supervision and administration by supreme court. (1) The Montana
7 supreme court shall supervise the activities of the workers' compensation court, the workers' compensation
8 judge, and associated personnel in implementing [section 3] and this section.

22 amount equal to the amount appropriated for the workers' compensation court in the general appropriations act

24 the workers' compensation court fund.

6 NEW SECTION. Section 6. Transition -- rights of personnel -- rights to property -- rules and
7 orders -- legal proceedings -- rights of duties under existing transactions -- references. (1) (a) The
8 workers' compensation judge and each employee of the office of workers' compensation judge affected by the
9 transfer of the office from the executive branch to the judicial branch are entitled to all rights that the judge or
10 employee possessed as a judge or employee before [the effective date of this act], including:
11 (i) rights to tenure in office and of pay;
12 (ii) rights to vacation pay, sick pay, and leave;
13 (iii) rights under any retirement or personnel plan or labor union contract;
14 (iv) rights to compensatory time earned; and
15 (v) any other rights under any law or administrative policy.
16 (b) This section is not intended to create any new rights for the workers' compensation judge or
17 any employee but to continue only those rights in effect before [the effective date of this act].
18 (2) The supreme court succeeds to the rights to all real and personal property of that agency
19 relating to the functions or parts of the office of workers' compensation judge. The property includes real
20 property, records, office equipment, supplies, contracts, books, papers, documents, maps, appropriations,
21 accounts within and outside of the state treasury, funds, vehicles, and all other similar property. However, the
22 supreme court may not use or divert money in a fund or account for a purpose other than provided by law. The
23 governor shall resolve any conflict as to the proper disposition of the property, and the governor's decision is
24 final.
25 (3) The supreme court succeeds to the rules and orders of the office of workers' compensation
26 judge relating to the functions or parts of functions transferred in [this act]. The rules and orders of the office of
27 workers' compensation judge in effect before [the effective date of this act] remain in effect until amended,

16 NEW SECTION. Section 7. Retirement plan election for workers' compensation judge. A
17 workers' compensation judge who is an active member of the public employees' retirement system on [the
18 effective date of this act] may elect to remain under that system by notifying the public employees' retirement

21 NEW SECTION. Section 8. Directions to code commissioner. (1) Section 2-15-1707 is intended to
22 be renumbered and codified as a new chapter in Title 3.
23 (2) The code commissioner is instructed to renumber sections currently in Title 39, chapter 71, part
24 29, into the same new chapter in Title 3 as [sections 2 and 3] and 2-15-1707.
25 (3) The code commissioner is instructed to change all internal references within and to the
26 renumbered subsections in the Montana Code Annotated, including within sections enacted or amended by the
27 2025 legislature, to reflect the new section numbers assigned pursuant to this section.
