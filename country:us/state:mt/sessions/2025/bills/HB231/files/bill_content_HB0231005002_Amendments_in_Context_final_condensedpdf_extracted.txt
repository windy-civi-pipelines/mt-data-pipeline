14 REQUIREMENTS; PROVIDING FOR AN APPEAL PROCESS; PROVIDING FOR THE ADJUSTMENT OF

16 RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AMENDING SECTIONS 15-1-121, 15-6-134, 15-7-102, 15-15-101, 15-15-102,
17 15-15-103, 15-16-101, AND 15-17-125, AND 15-30-2120, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE
18 EFFECTIVE DATE DATES, APPLICABILITY DATES, AND A TERMINATION DATE DATES.”

11 (b) The tax rate for the first $400,000 of market value for commercial and industrial property is
12 1.4%.

14 subsection (3)(c)(4).
15 (5) As used in this section, "fair market rent" means the fair market rent based on the size of the
16 dwelling as published annually by the U.S. department of housing and urban development."

19 COORDINATION SECTION. Section 28. Coordination instruction. If Senate Bill No. 542, House
20 Bill No. 863, and [this act] are passed and approved, then the section in House Bill No. 863 that coordinates
21 with [this act] is void.
22
23 NEW SECTION. Section 29. Applicability -- retroactive applicability. (1) Except as provided in
24 subsection (2), [this act] applies retroactively to property tax years beginning after December 31, 2024.
25 (2) [Sections 3 and 4 1 THROUGH 6, 13, AND 15 THROUGH 20] apply to property tax years beginning
26 after December 31, 2026 2025.
