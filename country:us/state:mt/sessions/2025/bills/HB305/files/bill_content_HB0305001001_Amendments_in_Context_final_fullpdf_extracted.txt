6 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE MONTANA RESIDENTIAL MOBILE HOME LOT
7 RENTAL ACT; CREATING THE MONTANA MOBILE HOME PARK DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROGRAM;
8 PROVIDING THAT THE PROGRAM IS ADMINISTERED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE;
9 REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT TO PRODUCE AND DISTRIBUTE EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS RELATED
10 TO LANDLORD AND TENANT RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES; ALLOWING FOR COMPLAINTS TO BE
11 FILED AGAINST LANDLORDS OR TENANTS; PROVIDING FOR REMEDIES AND PENALTIES; REQUIRING
12 THE REGISTRATION OF MOBILE HOME PARKS AND THE COLLECTION OF FEES; CREATING A MOBILE
13 HOME PARK DISPUTE RESOLUTION ACCOUNT; REQUIRING NOTICE BEFORE THE SALE OF A MOBILE
14 HOME PARK WITH AT LEAST 25 UNITS; PROVIDING REQUIREMENTS FOR A NEW OWNER OF A
15 MOBILE HOME PARK WITH AT LEAST 25 UNITS PROVIDING A STATUTORY APPROPRIATION;
16 PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; ESTABLISHING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; AND AMENDING
17 SECTIONS 17-7-502 AND 70-33-103, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
20
21 (Refer to Introduced Bill)
22 Strike everything after the enacting clause and insert:
23
24 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Notification of intent to sell mobile home park -- rent and fee
25 increases on sale. (1) An owner of a mobile home park that contains at least 25 rental units shall give written
26 notice to all tenants by certified mail or personal delivery:
27 (a) at least 30 days before the owner lists or markets the mobile home park for sale; or
28 (b) when an owner receives an offer to purchase that the owner intends to consider if the owner

2 (2) After the sale of a mobile home park, the new owner:
