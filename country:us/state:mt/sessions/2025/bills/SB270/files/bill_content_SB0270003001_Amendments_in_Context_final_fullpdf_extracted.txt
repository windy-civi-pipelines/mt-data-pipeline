4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO ELK LICENSES; PROVIDING
5 FOR THE ISSUANCE AND PURCHASE OF ANTLERLESS ELK TAGS; PROVIDING RULEMAKING
6 AUTHORITY; AMENDING SECTIONS SECTION 87-2-104 AND 87-2-501, MCA; AND PROVIDING A
7 DELAYED EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 87-2-104, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "87-2-104. Number of licenses, permits, or tags allowed -- fees. (1) The department may prescribe
13 rules and regulations for the issuance or sale of a replacement license, permit, or tag if the original license,
14 permit, or tag is lost, stolen, or destroyed upon payment of a fee not to exceed $5.
15 (2) The department shall issue a replacement license, permit, or tag to a person who lawfully
16 harvested a game animal but the meat of the animal was determined by the department to be unfit for human
17 consumption due to disease or prior injury. To obtain a replacement license, permit, or tag pursuant to this
18 subsection, the person:
19 (a) shall surrender the entire animal determined to be unfit for human consumption; and
20 (b) may choose to be issued the replacement for the same license year or the next license year.
21 (3) When authorized by the commission for game management purposes, the department may:
22 (a) issue more than one Class A-3 resident deer A, Class A-4 resident deer B, CLASS A-9 RESIDENT
23 ANTLERLESS ELK B, Class B-7 nonresident deer A, Class E-1 resident wolf, Class E-2 nonresident wolf, or
24 special antelope license to an applicant; AND
25 (b) issue a special antlerless moose license, a special cow or calf bison license, or one or more
26 special adult ewe mountain sheep licenses to an applicant.; and
27 (c) issue one or more Class A-9 resident antlerless elk B tag licenses or Class B-12 nonresident

1 antlerless elk B tag licenses to an applicant. Unless otherwise reduced pursuant to subsection (6), the fee for a
2 Class B-12 license is $270.
3 (4) For all of the game management licenses issued under subsection (3), the commission shall
4 determine the hunting districts or portions of hunting districts for which the licenses are to be issued, the
5 number of licenses to be issued, and all terms and conditions for the use of the licenses.
6 (5) Unless otherwise determined by the commission, a holder of a valid Class B-10 nonresident big
7 game combination license or a valid Class B-11 nonresident deer combination license may purchase up to no
8 more than two Class B-8 nonresident deer B tags while other eligible persons may purchase only one. A holder
9 of a Class B-10 nonresident big game combination license may purchase no more than two Class B-12
10 nonresident antlerless elk B tag licenses while other eligible persons may purchase only one. The commission
11 shall determine the hunting districts or portions of hunting districts for which the tags are valid and all terms and
12 conditions for the use of the tags.
13 (6) Unless otherwise reduced pursuant to subsection (8), the fee for a Class B-12 nonresident
14 antlerless elk B tag license is $270.
15 (7) In accordance with rules determined by the commission, a resident as defined in 87-2-102 may
16 be issued:
17 (a) a tag that allows the resident to harvest one antlered elk during a license year; and
18 (b) one or more NO MORE THAN FOUR TWO Class A-9 antlerless elk B tag licenses during a license
19 year., except that the The department may develop a rule that describes variable license structures for
20 antlerless elk based on management goals.
21 (6)(8) The fee for a resident or nonresident license of any class issued under subsections (3) and (5)
22 may be reduced annually by the department."
23

25 "87-2-501. Class A-3, A-4, A-5, A-6, and A-9 resident deer, elk, and bear licenses -- fees. (1)

27 older or who will turn 12 years old before or during the season for which the license is issued, upon on payment

2 will entitle a holder who is 12 years of age or older to hunt the game animal or animals authorized by the
3 license held and to possess the carcasses of those game animals as authorized by department rules:
