7 NEW SECTION. SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON FOREIGN NATIONAL INTERFERENCE IN STATEWIDE BALLOT
8 ISSUES. (1) It is unlawful for a foreign national, directly or through an intermediary, to make a disbursement for
9 an electioneering communication, a contribution, or an expenditure, or to make an express or implied promise
10 to make a contribution or an expenditure, in connection with any statewide ballot issue.
11 (2) It is unlawful for a person to:
12 (a) solicit, accept, or receive any funds from a foreign national for any purpose described in
13 subsection (1); or

15 knows were received from a foreign national for any purpose described in this section.
16 (3) (a) Upon receipt of a contribution by a political committee created to support or to oppose a
17 ballot issue, the committee treasurer shall obtain from the donor an affirmation that the donor is not a foreign
18 national.

20 obligations under 13-37-207 and 13-37-208.
