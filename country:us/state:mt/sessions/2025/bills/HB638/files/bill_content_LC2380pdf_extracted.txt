4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING HUMAN RIGHTS LAWS; PROHIBITING
5 A STATE OR LOCAL GOVERNMENT AGENCY FROM REQUIRING OR REQUESTING A DIVERSITY
6 STATEMENT OR GRANTING ANY PREFERENTIAL TREATMENT BASED ON THE SUBMISSION OF A
7 DIVERSITY STATEMENT; PROVIDING AN EXCEPTION; AND PROVIDING DEFINITIONS.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Diversity statements prohibited -- exception. (1) A state or local
12 government agency may not require, request, solicit, or compel a person to provide a diversity statement.
13 (2) A state or local government agency may not grant any form of preferential consideration or
14 treatment to a person who, with or without solicitation from the state or local government agency, provides a
15 diversity statement.
16 (3) If federal law requires a state or local government agency to accept or require a diversity
17 statement, then the state or local government agency:
18 (a) may accept the diversity statement only to the extent required under federal law; and
19 (b) shall limit consideration of the information contained in the diversity statement to the extent
20 necessary to satisfy the requirement under federal law.
21 (4) Nothing in this section limits or prohibits a state or local government agency from establishing
22 policies that are necessary to comply with state or federal law, including laws relating to prohibited
23 discrimination and harassment.
24
25 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Definitions. As used in [sections 1 through 2], unless the context clearly
26 indicates otherwise, the following definitions apply:
27 (1) "Diversity statement" means a submission, statement, or document that promotes or is
28 intended to promote differential treatment based on an individual's race, color, ethnicity, sex, sexual orientation,

1 national origin, religion, or gender identity.
2 (2) "State or local government agency" means:
3 (a) any branch, department, office, board, bureau, commission, agency, university unit, college, or
4 other instrumentality of state government; or
5 (b) a county, city, town, school district, or other unit of local government and any instrumentality of
6 local government.
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Codification instruction. [Sections 1 through 2] are intended to be
9 codified as a new chapter in Title 49, and the provisions of Title 49 apply to [sections 1 through 2].
