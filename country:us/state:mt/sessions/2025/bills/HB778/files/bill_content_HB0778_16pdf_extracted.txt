69th Legislature 2025 HB 778.1
2 INTRODUCED BY R. GREGG, B. LER, L. DEMING, B. MITCHELL
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING REQUIREMENTS REGARDING HOME
5 SCHOOLS; SEPARATING THE REQUIREMENTS FOR HOME SCHOOLS FROM THE REQUIREMENTS
6 FOR NONPUBLIC SCHOOLS; AMENDING SECTION 20-5-109, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE
7 EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 20-5-109, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "20-5-109. Nonpublic and home school requirements for compulsory enrollment exemption. (1)
13 To qualify its students for exemption from compulsory enrollment under 20-5-102, a nonpublic or home school:
14 (1) (a) shall maintain records on pupil attendance and disease immunization and make the records
15 available to the county superintendent of schools on request;
16 (2) (b) shall provide at least the minimum aggregate hours of pupil instruction in accordance with 20-1-
17 301 and 20-1-302;
18 (3) (c) must be housed in a building that complies with applicable local health and safety regulations;
19 and
20 (4) (d) shall provide an organized course of study that includes instruction in the subjects required of
21 public schools as a basic instructional program pursuant to 20-7-111; and.
22 (5) (2) in the case of home schools, To qualify its students for exemption from compulsory enrollment
23 under 20-5-102, a home school:
24 (a) shall notify the county superintendent of schools of the county in which the home school is
25 located in each school fiscal year of the student's attendance at the school;
26 (b) shall maintain records on pupil attendance and make the records available to the county
27 superintendent on request;
28 (c) shall provide at least the minimum aggregate hours of pupil instruction in accordance with 20-1-

5 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
