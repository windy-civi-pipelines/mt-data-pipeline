- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Julianne Burkhardt, SB0493.001.001
1 SENATE BILL NO. 493
2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT CREATING THE OFFENSE OF CRIMINAL TRESPASS BY
5 UNMANNED AERIAL VEHICLE; PROVIDING EXCEPTIONS; PROVIDING A DEFINITION; AND PROVIDING
6 AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Criminal trespass by unmanned aerial vehicle -- exceptions. (1)
11 Except as provided in subsection (3), a person commits the offense of criminal trespass by unmanned aerial
12 vehicle if the person knowingly causes an unmanned aerial vehicle to fly 200 feet or lower over the property or
13 residence of another person without the authorization of the property owner or resident.
14 (2) A person convicted of the offense of criminal trespass by unmanned aerial vehicle shall be
15 fined $500.
16 (3) Subsection (1) does not apply to the operation of an unmanned aerial vehicle system:
17 (a) conducted by a unit or agency of the federal government, the state, or a tribal or local
18 government, including any individual conducting an operation pursuant to contract or other agreement entered
19 into with the unit or agency, for the purpose of protecting the public safety, environment, and welfare, including
20 firefighting, law enforcement, and emergency response;
21 (b) by a peace officer acting in accordance with 46-5-109;
22 (c) within the boundaries of an easement with the permission of the owner of the dominant
23 tenement; or
24 (d) being used by an employee or contractor of a utility as defined in 69-5-102 or a provider of
25 broadband service as defined in 90-1-602 to help assess property damage or catastrophe damage or to inspect
26 property and equipment, including cell towers and wireless telecommunications infrastructure and including the
27 deployment of drones; or
28 (e) by an owner or operator of the system who has an active license issued by the federal aviation

3 (4) As used in this section, "unmanned aerial vehicle" has the same meaning as provided in 76-13-

5
6 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
