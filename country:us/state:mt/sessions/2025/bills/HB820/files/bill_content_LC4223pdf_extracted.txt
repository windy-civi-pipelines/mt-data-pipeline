69th Legislature 2025 LC 4223
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING FREE ON-CAMPUS PARKING PERMITS FOR
5 VETERAN AND MONTANA NATIONAL GUARD SERVICE MEMBER FACULTY, STAFF, AND STUDENTS
6 WHO ARE EMPLOYED OR ENROLLED AT A UNIT OF THE MONTANA UNIVERSITY SYSTEM.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Veteran and Montana national guard service member on-campus
11 parking permit exemption. (1) The regents of the Montana university system may not assess on-campus
12 parking permit fees for veteran and Montana national guard service member faculty, staff, and students who
13 are employed or enrolled at a unit of the Montana university system, as provided in 20-25-201.
14 (2) The regents of Montana university system shall direct the president of each unit to provide
15 preference to veteran and Montana national guard service member faculty, staff, and students when issuing on-
16 campus parking permits.
17 (3) A veteran or Montana national guard service member faculty, staff, or student shall provide
18 proof of service to a unit's parking permitting office to be eligible for a free on-campus parking permit.
19 (4) The president of each unit of the Montana university system may make rules concerning free
20 on-campus parking for veterans and Montana national guard service member faculty, staff, and students.
21
22 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
23 integral part of Title 20, chapter 25, part 3, and the provisions of Title 20, chapter 25, part 3, apply to [section 1].
24
25 - END -
