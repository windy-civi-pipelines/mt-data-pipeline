8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Privacy policy. (1) Medical pregnancy centers and pregnancy centers
9 shall maintain a consumer health data and personal data privacy policy that clearly and conspicuously
10 discloses:
11 (a) the categories of personally identifiable information and medical data collected and the purpose
12 for which the data is collected, including how the data will be used;
13 (b) the categories of sources from which the data is collected;
14 (c) the categories of third parties and specific affiliates with whom the medical pregnancy center
15 and pregnancy center shares the consumer health data; and
16 (d) how a consumer may exercise their rights to protect this information.
17 (2) A medical pregnancy center or a pregnancy center shall prominently publish a link to its
18 consumer health data privacy policy on its homepage.

20 is not considered a regulated entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act.
21 (4) Medical pregnancy centers or pregnancy centers may not collect, use, or share additional
22 categories of consumer health data not disclosed in the policy without first disclosing the additional categories
23 and obtaining the consumer's affirmative written consent prior to the collection, use, or sharing of the consumer
24 health data.
25 (5) It is a violation of this chapter for a medical pregnancy center or pregnancy center to contract
26 with a processor to process consumer health data in a manner that is inconsistent with the medical privacy
27 center or pregnancy center consumer data privacy policy.
