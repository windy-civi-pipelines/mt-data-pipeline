Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 15.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 15
2 INTRODUCED BY B. USHER
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENT OF ASSOCIATE WATER JUDGE MADE BY THE CHIEF JUSTICE
6 OF THE MONTANA SUPREME COURT AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED
7 JANUARY 31, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the State of Montana has made the
10 appointment, below designated, that has been submitted to the Senate by the Chief Justice pursuant to section
11 3-7-221, MCA:
12 As Associate Water Judge of the State of Montana, in accordance with sections 3-1-901 and 3-7-221,
13 MCA:
14 Bina R. Peters, Bozeman, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2028.
15
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
17 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
18 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
19 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
20 MCA.
21 - END -
