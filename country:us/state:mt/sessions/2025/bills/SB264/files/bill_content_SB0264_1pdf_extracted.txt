Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 SB 264.1
2 INTRODUCED BY R. TEMPEL
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ELIMINATING A DEFENSE TO THE EXHAUST NOISE
5 LIMITATION STATUTE; AND AMENDING SECTION 61-9-435, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 61-9-435, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "61-9-435. Exhaust noise limitation. (1) Except as provided in subsection (3) (2), a person may not
11 operate a motor vehicle with an exhaust system that emits a noise in excess of 95 decibels, as measured by
12 the society of automotive engineers' standard j1169 (May 1998).
13 (2) A person charged with violating this section may not be convicted if the person had reasonable
14 grounds to believe that the vehicle was not operated in violation of the standard in subsection (1).
15 (3)(2) This section does not apply to a motorcycle or quadricycle that is subject to 61-9-418."
16 - END -
