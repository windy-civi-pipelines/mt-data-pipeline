5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE ELDERLY HOMEOWNER AND RENTER INCOME
6 TAX CREDIT; REVISING THE DEFINITION OF "HOUSEHOLD INCOME"; INCREASING THE MAXIMUM
7 CREDIT AMOUNT; REVISING THE PHASEOUT OF THE CREDIT; PROVIDING AN INFLATIONARY
8 ADJUSTMENT FOR THE MAXIMUM CREDIT AMOUNT AND THE INCOME AT WHICH THE CREDIT
9 PHASES OUT; AMENDING SECTIONS 15-30-2337 AND 15-30-2340, MCA; REPEALING SECTIONS 4, 5, 9
10 AND 10, CHAPTER 476, LAWS OF 2021; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND A
11 RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY DATE.”
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
14
15 Section 1. Section 15-30-2337, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "15-30-2337. Residential property tax credit for elderly -- definitions. As used in 15-30-2337
17 through 15-30-2341, the following definitions apply:
18 (1) "Claim period" means the tax year for individuals required to file Montana individual income tax
19 returns and the calendar year for individuals not required to file returns.
20 (2) "Claimant" means a person who is eligible to file a claim under 15-30-2338.
21 (3) "Department" means the department of revenue.
22 (4) "Gross household income" means all income received by all individuals of a household while
23 they are members of the household.
24 (5) "Gross rent" means the total rent in cash or its equivalent actually paid during the claim period
25 by the renter or lessee for the right of occupancy of the homestead pursuant to an arm's-length transaction with
26 the landlord.
27 (6) "Homestead" means:

10 amount of property tax billed less the deduction specified in subsection (4) (3). ;
11 (2)(b) In in the case of a claimant who rents the homestead for which a claim is made, the credit is
12 the amount of rent-equivalent tax paid less the deduction specified in subsection (4) (3). ; and
13 (3)(c) In in the case of a claimant who both owns and rents the homestead for which a claim is made,
14 the credit is:
15 (a)(i) the amount of property tax billed on the owned portion of the homestead less the deduction
16 specified in subsection (4) (3); plus
17 (b)(ii) the amount of rent-equivalent tax paid on the rented portion of the homestead less the
18 deduction specified in subsection (4) (3).
