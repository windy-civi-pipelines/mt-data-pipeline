Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 38.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 38
2 INTRODUCED BY D. LENZ
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE BOARD OF CHIROPRACTORS MADE BY THE
6 GOVERNOR AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE
7 SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
10 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
11 As members of the Board of Chiropractors, in accordance with section 2-15-1737, MCA:
12 Michael Matury, Great Falls, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending July 1, 2027.
13 Julie Murack, Conrad, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending July 1, 2025.
14 Dustin Rising, Bozeman, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending July 1, 2028.
15 Caitlin Walter, Great Falls, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending July 1, 2026.
16
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
18 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
19 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
20 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
21 MCA.
22 - END -
