- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0624.2
2 INTRODUCED BY E. TILLEMAN, R. MINER, B. CARTER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING LAWS RELATED TO HOBBYIST
5 APIARIES; PROVIDING FOR AN INCREASED NUMBER OF ALLOWED HIVE AND SITE REGISTRATIONS;
6 AND AMENDING SECTION 80-6-114, MCA.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 80-6-114, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "80-6-114. Hobbyist apiary site -- registrations. (1) The department may grant hobbyist apiary site
12 registrations to hobbyist beekeepers under the following conditions:
13 (a) The the applicant may not own a total of more than five 10 hives, and all of the hives must be
14 placed on the hobbyist apiary site. ;
15 (b) The the applicant must shall own the bees and the hives and must shall personally manage
16 and operate the bees and the hives. ; and
17 (c) Only one only five hobbyist apiary site registration is registrations are allowed for an applicant
18 and only two 10 FIVE hobbyist apiary site registrations are allowed for a family unit.
19 (2) A certificate of registration of a hobbyist apiary site may not be leased, assigned, or transferred.
20 A person, other than the hobbyist apiary site registrant, may not exercise any rights or privileges, directly or
21 indirectly, authorized by the certificate of registration."
22 - END -
