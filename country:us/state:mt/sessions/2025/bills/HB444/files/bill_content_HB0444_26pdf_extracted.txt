5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING LAWS RELATED TO RESIDENTIAL
6 TENANTS' SECURITY DEPOSITS; PROVIDING LANDLORDS THE ABILITY TO PROVIDE NOTICES BY E-
7 MAIL; AND TO RETURN A SECURITY DEPOSIT ELECTRONICALLY; SPECIFYING HOW THE LANDLORD
8 MAY RETURN A SECURITY DEPOSIT; AMENDING SECTIONS 70-25-201, AND 70-25-202, AND 70-25-205,
9 MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 70-25-201, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "70-25-201. Security deposit -- deductions authorized therefrom. (1) A landlord renting property
15 covered by this chapter may deduct from the security deposit a sum equal to the damage alleged to have been
16 caused by the tenant, together with a sum equal to the unpaid rent, late charges, utilities, penalties due under
17 lease provisions, and other money owing to the landlord at the time of deduction, including rent owed under 70-
18 24-441(3), and a sum for actual cleaning expenses, including a reasonable charge for the landlord's labor.
19 (2) At the request of either party, the premises may be inspected within 1 week prior to termination
20 of the tenancy.
21 (3) (A) Cleaning charges may not be imposed for normal maintenance performed on a cyclical
22 basis by the landlord as noted by the landlord at the time that the tenant occupies the space unless the landlord
23 is forced to perform this maintenance because of negligence of the tenant. Additionally, cleaning charges may
24 not be deducted until written notice has been given to the tenant. The notice must include the cleaning not
25 accomplished by the tenant and the additional AMOUNT and type or types of cleaning that need to be done by
26 the tenant to bring the premises back to its condition at the time of its renting.
27 (B) After the delivery of the notice PURSUANT TO 70-24-108 OR 70-33-106, the tenant has 24 hours
28 to complete the required cleaning, unless the rental agreement is already terminated pursuant to 70-24-427 or

1 70-33-427 and the landlord has a pending claim for actual damages filed in court. The notice may be
2 transmitted to an electronic mail address provided by the tenant in the rental agreement, delivered in hand to
3 the tenant, or mailed with a certificate of mailing or by certified mail to the tenant at the place indicated by the
4 tenant as the place for receipt of the communication or, in the absence of a designation, to the tenant's last-
5 known address. If notice is transmitted by electronic mail, it is complete on receipt of a read receipt generated
6 by an electronic mail system or an electronic mail reply other than an automatically generated electronic mail
7 reply. If THE notice is mailed by certified mail, service of the notice is considered to have been made 3 days
8 after the date of the mailing.
9 (C) A tenant who fails to notify the landlord of the intent to vacate or who vacates the premises
10 without notice relieves the landlord of the requirement of giving notice and allows the landlord to deduct the
11 cleaning charges from the deposit, or the landlord may leave a copy of the notice in a conspicuous location in
12 the rental unit and notify the tenant by e-mail, phone, or text, and notice is considered delivered.
13 (4) A person may not deduct or withhold from the security deposit any amount for purposes other
14 than those set forth in this section."
15
16 Section 2. Section 70-25-202, MCA, is amended to read:
17 "70-25-202. List of damages and refund -- delivery to departing tenant. (1) Except as provided in
18 subsection (2):
19 (a) (I) Each landlord, within 30 days subsequent to the termination of a tenancy or within 30 days
20 subsequent to a surrender and acceptance of the leasehold premises, whichever occurs first, shall provide the
21 departing tenant with a written list of any rent due and any damage and cleaning charges, brought after the
22 provisions of 70-25-201 have been followed, with regard to the leasehold premises that the landlord alleges are
23 the responsibility of the tenant. Delivery of the list must be accompanied by payment of the difference, if any,
24 between the security deposit and the permitted charges set forth in 70-25-201.
25 (II) Delivery must be accomplished by transmitting the list to an electronic mail address provided
26 by the tenant in the rental agreement and the refund by electronic fund transfer as set forth in 70-25-205, by

1 OTHER FORMS OF REFUND STATED IN THE RENTAL AGREEMENT AT THE COMMENCEMENT OF THE RENTAL AGREEMENT.
2 DELIVERY OF THE WRITTEN LIST MUST BE NOTICED PURSUANT TO 70-24-108 OR 70-33-106. THE LANDLORD SHALL MAIL
3 THE REFUND AND WRITTEN LIST TO THE DEPARTING TENANTS AT the new address provided by the tenant or, if a new
4 address is not provided, to the tenant's last-known address.
5 (b) (I) If after inspection there are no damages to the premises, no cleaning required, and no rent
6 unpaid and if the tenant can demonstrate that no utilities are unpaid by the tenant, the landlord shall return the
7 security deposit within 10 days by electronic fund transfer as set forth in 70-25-205 or by mailing it to the new
8 address provided by the tenant or, if a new address is not provided, to the tenant's last-known address.
9 (II) DELIVERY OF THE REFUND MAY BE ACCOMPLISHED BY DIFFERENT FORMS OF PAYMENT THAT INCLUDE
10 ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS, CASH, CHECK, OR OTHER FORMS OF REFUND DELIVERY STATED IN THE RENTAL
11 AGREEMENT AT THE COMMENCEMENT OF THE RENTAL AGREEMENT. DELIVERY OF THE WRITTEN LIST MUST BE NOTICED
12 PURSUANT TO 70-24-108 AND 70-33-106. THE LANDLORD SHALL MAIL THE REFUND AND WRITTEN LIST TO THE
13 DEPARTING TENANT AT THE NEW ADDRESS PROVIDED BY THE TENANT OR, IF A NEW ADDRESS IS NOT PROVIDED, AT THE
14 TENANT'S LAST-KNOWN ADDRESS.
15 (c) It is not a wrongful withholding of security deposit funds if the landlord mails the funds to the
16 last-known address of a tenant who has departed and the tenant does not receive the funds because the tenant
17 has not given the landlord the tenant's new address, but the landlord remains liable to the tenant for the amount
18 due the tenant.
19 (2) This section does not apply if a rental agreement is terminated pursuant to 70-24-427 or 70-33-
20 427 and the landlord has a pending claim for actual damages filed in court."
21

26 amount owing to the tenant by the landlord.
