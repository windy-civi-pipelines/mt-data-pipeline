- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0856.2
2 INTRODUCED BY S. FITZPATRICK, B. LER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE TIME PERIOD WHEN AN INCUMBENT'S OFFICE
5 BECOMES VACANT; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION; AMENDING SECTION 2-16-501, MCA; AND
6 PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES AND A RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 2-16-501, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "2-16-501. Vacancies created. An office becomes vacant on the occurrence of any one of the
12 following events before the expiration of the term of the incumbent:
13 (1) the death of the incumbent;
14 (2) a determination pursuant to Title 53, chapter 21, part 1, that the incumbent suffers from a
15 mental disorder and is in need of commitment;
16 (3) the effective date stipulated in the resignation of the incumbent;
17 (4) removal of the incumbent from office;
18 (5) the incumbent's ceasing to be a resident of the state or, if the office is local, of the district, city,
19 county, town, or township for which the incumbent was chosen or appointed or within which the duties of the
20 incumbent's office are required to be discharged;
21 (6) except as provided in 10-1-1008, absence of the incumbent from the state, without the
22 permission of the legislature, beyond the period allowed by law;
23 (7) the incumbent's ceasing to discharge the duty of the incumbent's office for the period of 3 4
24 consecutive months, except when prevented by sickness, when absent from the state by permission of the
25 legislature, or as provided in 10-1-1008;
26 (8) conviction of the incumbent of a felony or of an offense involving moral turpitude or a violation
27 of the incumbent's official duties;
28 (9) the incumbent's refusal or neglect to file the incumbent's official oath or bond within the time
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 856
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0856.2
1 prescribed;
2 (10) the decision of a competent tribunal declaring void the incumbent's election or appointment."
3
4 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Appropriation. There is appropriated $100 from the general find FUND to
5 the legislative services division for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025, for the purpose of monitoring and
6 reporting the status of [this act] to legislators who request this information.
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective dates. (1) Except as provided in subsection (2), [this act] is
9 effective on passage and approval.
10 (2) [Section 2] is effective July 1, 2025.
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Retroactive applicability. [This act] applies retroactively, within the
13 meaning of 1-2-109, to vacancies beginning on or after January 1, 2025.
14 - END -
