3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LEGISLATIVE GOALS FOR PUBLIC EDUCATION TO
5 ENCOURAGE SCHOOLS TO PROVIDE INSTRUCTION IN READING AND WRITING IN CURSIVE
6 HANDWRITING TO STUDENTS; AMENDING SECTION 20-1-102, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
7 DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 20-1-102, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "20-1-102. Legislative goals for public elementary and secondary schools. It is the goal of the
13 legislature that Montana's public elementary and secondary school system, in cooperation with parents or
14 guardians, create a learning environment for each student that:
15 (1) develops a sound foundation for literacy and numeracy during the early years that is built upon
16 on and reinforced throughout the educational experience;
17 (2) furthers the ability to reason critically, creatively, and strategically;
18 (3) fosters the ability to effectively understand and communicate ideas, knowledge, and thoughts;
19 (4) develops a sense of personal and civic responsibility;
20 (5) provides an in-depth understanding of the American political, social, and economic systems
21 and the historical context from which they arose;
22 (6) provides familiarization with political, social, and economic systems found elsewhere in the
23 world;
24 (7) develops a strong work ethic, postsecondary readiness, and employment skills;
25 (8) encourages a healthy lifestyle; and
26 (9) supports instruction of financial literacy, where in which students obtain the knowledge and
27 skills required to succeed financially; and
28 (10) provides instruction for all students in reading and writing in cursive handwriting, to develop
- 1 - LC 2216
****
69th Legislature 2025 LC 2216
1 cognitive and fine motor skills in young students, and to ensure that all students are able to read original
2 documents, such as the United States constitution, which are foundational to understanding the history and
3 contemporary context of the nation's government."
4
5 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
6 - END -
