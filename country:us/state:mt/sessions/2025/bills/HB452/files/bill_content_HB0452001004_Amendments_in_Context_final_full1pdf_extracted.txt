1 HOUSE BILL NO. 452
2 INTRODUCED BY G. PARRY
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CRIME OF ASSAULT ON AN EMPLOYEE
5 OF A PUBLIC UTILITY, AN ELECTRIC COOPERATIVE, OR A TELECOMMUNICATIONS CARRIER; AND
6 PROVIDING PENALTIES.
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Assault on employee of public utility, electric cooperative, or
11 telecommunications carrier. (1) A person commits the offense of assault on an employee of a public utility, as
12 defined in 69-3-101, electric cooperative, as defined in 35-18-102, or telecommunications carrier, as defined in
13 69-3-803, if the person purposely or knowingly causes:
14 (a) bodily injury to an employee of a public utility, electric cooperative, or telecommunications
15 carrier while the employee is performing the employee's duties;
16 (b) reasonable apprehension of serious bodily injury in an employee of a public utility, electric
17 cooperative, or telecommunications carrier while the employee is performing the employee's duties by use of:
18 (i) a weapon; or
19 (ii) what reasonably appears to be a weapon;
20 (c) bodily injury to an employee of a public utility, electric cooperative, or telecommunications
21 carrier while the employee is performing the employee's duties with a weapon; or
22 (d) serious bodily injury to an employee of a public utility, electric cooperative, or
23 telecommunications carrier while the employee is performing the employee's duties.
24 (2) (a) A person convicted of assault on an employee of a public utility, electric cooperative, or
25 telecommunications carrier:
26 (i) under subsection (1)(a), (1)(b)(i), or (1)(c) shall be imprisoned in the state prison for a term not
27 to exceed 10 years and may be fined an amount not to exceed $50,000; or
28 (ii) under subsection (1)(b)(ii) shall be imprisoned in the state prison for a term not to exceed 8
