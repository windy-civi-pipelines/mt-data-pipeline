69th Legislature 2025 HB0335.2
2 INTRODUCED BY J. KARLEN, R. MARSHALL, B. MITCHELL
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO PROFESSIONAL LICENSING
5 BOARD APPOINTMENTS; REQUIRING THE GOVERNOR TO APPOINT A QUALIFIED BOARD MEMBER
6 APPLICANT WITHIN A CERTAIN TIMEFRAME;ESTABLISHING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; AND
7 AMENDING SECTION 37-1-123, MCA AND AMENDING SECTION 37-1-106, MCA.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 (Refer to Introduced Bill)
12 Strike everything after the enacting clause and insert:
13
14 SECTION 1. Section 37-1-106, MCA, is amended to read:
15 "37-1-106. Biennial report. (1) The department, in cooperation with each licensing board, shall
16 prepare a biennial report. The biennial report of the department shall must contain for each board:
17
18 (a) a summary of the board's activities, ;
19
20 (b) the board's goals and objectives, ;
21
22 (c) a detailed breakdown of board revenues and expenditures, ;
23
24 (d) statistics illustrating board activities concerning licensing, ;
25
26 (e) a summary of complaints received and their disposition, ;
27
28 (f) the number of licenses revoked or suspended, ;

1
2 (g) legislative or court action affecting the board, and ;
3
4 (h) the number of board members appointed pursuant to 37-1-123 who are still serving following
5 the expiration of their appointed term; and
6 (i) any other information the department or board considers relevant.
7
8 (2) The department shall submit the report to the economic affairs interim committee, in
9 accordance with 5-11-210, and the office of budget and program planning as a part of the information required
10 by 17-7-111."
