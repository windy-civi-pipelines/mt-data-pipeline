Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 342.1
2 INTRODUCED BY B. MERCER, E. BUTTREY, S. FITZPATRICK
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATING TO MEDICAL MALPRACTICE AND
5 HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS RELATING TO THE DUTY OF CARE; PROVIDING THAT A SPECIFIC RISK
6 DOES NOT CHANGE OR HEIGHTEN THE DUTY BEYOND THE REASONABLE STANDARD OF CARE; AND
7 PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Medical malpractice -- duty of care -- foreseeability of risks. In
12 medical malpractice actions, the foreseeability of risks or of a specific risk does not change or heighten the duty
13 owed beyond the reasonable standard of care applicable to the medical provider.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
16 integral part of Title 27, chapter 1, part 7, and the provisions of Title 27, chapter 1, part 7, apply to [section 1].
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
19
20 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Applicability. [This act] applies to medical malpractice actions filed on or
21 after [the effective date of this act] and is intended to clarify any court ruling to the contrary.
22 - END -
