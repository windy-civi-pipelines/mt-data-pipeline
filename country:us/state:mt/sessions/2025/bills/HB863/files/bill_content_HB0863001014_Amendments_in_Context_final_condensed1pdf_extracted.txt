4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING STATE FINANCE LAWS;
5 ESTABLISHING A DAILY RATE FOR DETENTION CENTERS; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION;
6 AMENDING SECTION SECTIONS 7-32-2242 AND 17-7-102, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 7-32-2242, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "7-32-2242. Use of detention center -- payment of costs. (1) Local government, state, and federal
12 law enforcement and correctional agencies may use any detention center for the confinement of arrested
13 persons and the punishment of offenders, under conditions imposed by law and with the consent of the
14 governing body responsible for the detention center.
15 (2) (a) Except as provided in 7-32-2245, if a person is confined in a detention center by an
16 arresting agency not responsible for the operation of the detention center, the actual costs of holding the person
17 in confinement must be paid by the arresting agency unless otherwise agreed to by the arresting agency and
18 the operator of the detention center.
19 (b) If a city or town commits a person to the detention center of the county in which the city or town
20 is located for a reason other than detention pending trial for or detention for service of a sentence for violating
21 an ordinance of that city or town, the costs must be paid by the county, except as provided in 7-32-2245. If the
22 department of corrections is the arresting agency and the inmate is a probation violator, the costs must be paid
23 by the county in which the district court that retains jurisdiction over the inmate is located, except as provided in
24 7-32-2245.
25 (c) The department of corrections is responsible to pay actual costs for defendants following the
26 pronouncement of sentence pursuant to 46-19-101.
27 (d) Payments must be made to the government unit responsible for the detention center or to the

3 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Appropriation. (1) There is appropriated $100,000 from the Montana-
4 Ireland trade and development special revenue account to the department of commerce for the biennium
5 beginning July 1, 2025, for implementing the provisions of Senate Bill No. 320.
6 (2) It is the intent of the legislature that the appropriation be included in the base budget for the
7 department of commerce for the biennium beginning July 1, 2027.
8
9 COORDINATION SECTION. Section 4. Coordination instruction. If both House Bill No. 643 and
10 [this act] are passed and approved and if both contain a section that amends 7-32-2242, then the section
11 amending 7-32-2242 in House Bill No. 643 is void.
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
