12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Class F--nonresident wildlife management area shed hunting
13 license. (1) A person who is not a resident as defined in 87-2-102, on payment of a $50 fee, is entitled to
14 receive a Class F license that authorizes the holder of the license to shed hunt as defined in 87-1-201 87-2-101
15 in a wildlife management area.
16 (2) A person found in violation of this section:
17 (a) for a first offense, must be given a warning;
18 (b) for a second offense, is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be fined not more than twice the cost
19 of a Class F license. In addition, the person, on conviction or forfeiture of bond or bail, may be subject to
20 forfeiture of any current shed hunting license and the privilege to shed hunt in a wildlife management area for a
21 period of time set by the court.
22 (c) for a third offense, is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be fined not more than $500. In
23 addition, the person, on conviction or forfeiture of bond or bail, shall forfeit any current shed hunting license and
24 the privilege to shed hunt in a wildlife management area for a period of time set by the court.
