2 INTRODUCED BY J. COHENOUR, M. MARLER, T. RUNNING WOLF, J. SECKINGER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NONRESIDENT WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT
5 AREA SHED HUNTING LICENSE; IMPLEMENTING A WAITING PERIOD FOR NONRESIDENTS;
6 ESTABLISHING VIOLATIONS AND ESTABLISHING PENALTIES; DEFINING "SHED HUNT" AND "SHED
7 HUNTING"; PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 87-1-304, 87-2-101, 87-
8 2-201, AND 87-2-202, MCA.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Class F--nonresident wildlife management area shed hunting
13 license. (1) A person who is not a resident as defined in 87-2-102, on payment of a $50 fee, is entitled to
14 receive a Class F license that authorizes the holder of the license to shed hunt as defined in 87-1-201 87-2-101
15 in a wildlife management area.
16 (2) A person found in violation of this section:
17 (a) for a first offense, must be given a warning;
18 (b) for a second offense, is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be fined not more than twice the cost
19 of a Class F license. In addition, the person, on conviction or forfeiture of bond or bail, may be subject to
20 forfeiture of any current shed hunting license and the privilege to shed hunt in a wildlife management area for a
21 period of time set by the court.
22 (c) for a third offense, is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be fined not more than $500. In
23 addition, the person, on conviction or forfeiture of bond or bail, shall forfeit any current shed hunting license and
24 the privilege to shed hunt in a wildlife management area for a period of time set by the court.
25
26 Section 2. Section 87-1-304, MCA, is amended to read:
27 "87-1-304. Fixing of seasons and bag and possession limits. (1) Subject to the provisions of 87-5-

5 Section 4. Section 87-2-201, MCA, is amended to read:
6 "87-2-201. Conservation license required and prerequisite for other licenses. (1) Except as
7 provided in 87-2-803(6) and 87-2-815, it is unlawful for any person to engage in any of the following without first
8 having purchased a conservation license as provided in this part:
9 (a) purchase or apply for a hunting, fishing, nonresident wildlife management area shed hunting, or
10 trapping license;
11 (b) use lands owned or controlled by the department; or
12 (c) engage in general recreational use as defined in 77-1-101 on state trust land pursuant to an
13 agreement established under 77-1-815.
14 (2) If the department of natural resources and conservation and the department enter into an
15 agreement pursuant to 77-1-815, the department shall submit every other legislative session a report to the
16 legislature in accordance with 5-11-210 that documents the number of conservation licenses sold and revenue
17 received pursuant to this section. The first report shall be provided to the legislature by January 1, 2027."
18
19 Section 5. Section 87-2-202, MCA, is amended to read:
20 "87-2-202. Application -- fee. (1) Except as provided in 87-2-817(2), a conservation license must be
21 sold upon written application. The application must contain the applicant's name, age, [last four digits of the
22 applicant's social security number,] street address of permanent residence, mailing address, qualifying length of
23 time as a resident in the state of Montana, and status as a citizen of the United States or as an alien and must
24 be signed by the applicant. The applicant shall present a valid Montana driver's license, a Montana driver's
25 examiner's identification card, a tribal identification card, or other identification specified by the department to
26 substantiate the required information when applying for a conservation license. It is the applicant's burden to
27 provide documentation establishing the applicant's identity and qualifications to purchase a conservation

19 Ch. 596, L. 2003.)"

21 NEW SECTION. Section 6. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
22 integral part of Title 87, chapter 2, part 5, and the provisions of Title 87, chapter 2, part 5, apply to [section 1].
