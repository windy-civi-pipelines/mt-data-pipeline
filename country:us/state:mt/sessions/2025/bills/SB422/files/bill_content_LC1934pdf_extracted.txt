4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING REQUIREMENTS FOR HEALTH INSURANCE
5 COVERAGE RELATING TO ADVANCED METASTATIC CANCER; PROHIBITING INSURERS FROM
6 REQUIRING CERTAIN ACTS FROM THE INSURED RELATED TO PRESCRIPTION DRUGS AND
7 METASTATIC CANCER; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 2-18-704, 33-31-111, AND 33-35-306, MCA.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Coverage for advanced metastatic cancer and associated
12 conditions. (1) Insurers who sell health insurance coverage available through the group market or individual
13 market in this state and who provide coverage for advanced metastatic cancer and associated conditions may
14 not, before the health insurance provides coverage of a prescription drug approved by the United States food
15 and drug administration, require that the insured:
16 (a) fail to successfully respond to a different drug; or
17 (b) prove a history of failure of a different drug.
18 (2) This section applies only to a drug whose use is:
19 (a) consistent with best practices for the treatment of advanced metastatic cancer or an associated
20 condition;
21 (b) supported by peer-reviewed, evidence-based literature; and
22 (c) approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration.
23 (3) For the purposes of this section, the following definitions apply:
24 (a) "Advanced metastatic cancer" means cancer that has spread from the primary or original site of
25 the cancer to nearby tissues, lymph nodes, or other areas of the body.
26 (b) "Associated condition" means the symptoms or side effects that are associated with advanced
27 metastatic cancer or the cancer's treatment and which, in the judgment of the health care practitioner, would
28 further jeopardize the health of a patient if left untreated.
