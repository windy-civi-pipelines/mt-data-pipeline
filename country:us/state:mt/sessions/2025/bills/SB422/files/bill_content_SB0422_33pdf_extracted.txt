4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING REQUIREMENTS FOR HEALTH INSURANCE
5 COVERAGE RELATING TO ADVANCED OR METASTATIC CANCER; PROHIBITING INSURERS FROM
6 REQUIRING CERTAIN ACTS FROM THE INSURED RELATED TO PRESCRIPTION DRUGS AND
7 ADVANCED OR METASTATIC CANCER; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 2-18-704, 33-31-111, AND 33-35-306,
8 MCA.”

10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Coverage for advanced OR metastatic cancer and associated
13 conditions. (1) Insurers who sell health insurance coverage available through the group market or individual
14 market in this state and who provide coverage for advanced OR metastatic cancer and associated conditions
15 may not, before the health insurance provides coverage of a prescription drug approved by the United States
16 food and drug administration AND AS LONG AS PRESCRIBING IS CONSISTENT WITH THE MOST COST-EFFECTIVE
17 THERAPY AVAILABLE ACCORDING TO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE CANCER NETWORK GUIDELINES, require that the
18 insured:
19 (a) fail to successfully respond to a different drug; or
20 (b) prove a history of failure of a different drug.
21 (2) This section applies only to a drug whose use is:
22 (a) consistent with best practices for the treatment of advanced OR metastatic cancer or an
23 associated condition;
24 (b) supported by peer-reviewed, evidence-based literature; and
25 (c) approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration.
26 (3) For the purposes of this section, the following definitions apply:
27 (a) "Advanced OR metastatic cancer" means cancer that has spread from the primary or original
28 site of the cancer to nearby tissues, lymph nodes, or other areas of the body OR THAT HAS PROGRESSED BEYOND

1 EARLY STAGES AND IS CONSIDERED TERMINAL CANCER.
2 (b) "Associated condition" means the symptoms or side effects that are associated with advanced
3 metastatic cancer or the cancer's treatment and which, in the judgment of the health care practitioner, would
4 further jeopardize the health of a patient if left untreated. "TERMINAL CANCER" MEANS CANCER THAT CANNOT BE
5 CURED WITH TREATMENT AND IS EXPECTED TO LEAD TO DEATH.
