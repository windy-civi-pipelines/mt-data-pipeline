11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Coverage for advanced metastatic cancer and associated
12 conditions. (1) Insurers who sell health insurance coverage available through the group market or individual
13 market in this state and who provide coverage for advanced metastatic cancer and associated conditions may
14 not, before the health insurance provides coverage of a prescription drug approved by the United States food
15 and drug administration AND AS LONG AS PRESCRIBING IS CONSISTENT WITH THE MOST COST-EFFECTIVE THERAPY
16 AVAILABLE ACCORDING TO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE CANCER NETWORK GUIDELINES, require that the insured:
17 (a) fail to successfully respond to a different drug; or
18 (b) prove a history of failure of a different drug.
19 (2) This section applies only to a drug whose use is:
20 (a) consistent with best practices for the treatment of advanced metastatic cancer or an associated
21 condition;
22 (b) supported by peer-reviewed, evidence-based literature; and
23 (c) approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration.
24 (3) For the purposes of this section, the following definitions apply:
25 (a) "Advanced metastatic cancer" means cancer that has spread from the primary or original site of
