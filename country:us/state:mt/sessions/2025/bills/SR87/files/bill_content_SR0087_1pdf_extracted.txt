Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 87.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 87
2 INTRODUCED BY M. NOLAND
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE BOARD OF AERONAUTICS MADE BY THE GOVERNOR
6 AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED MARCH 21, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
9 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
10 As members of the Board of Aeronautics, in accordance with section 2-15-2506, MCA:
11 Wade Cebulski, Seeley Lake, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
12 Tim Damrow, Missoula, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
13 Tim Robertson, Lewistown, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
14 Gregory Smith, Lewistown, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
15 Grayson Sperry, Belgrade, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
16
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
18 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
19 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
20 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
21 MCA.
22 - END -
