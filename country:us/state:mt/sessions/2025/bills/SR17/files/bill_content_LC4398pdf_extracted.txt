69th Legislature LC 4398
1 _____________ RESOLUTION NO. _____________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA CONCURRING IN, CONFIRMING, AND
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE BOARD OF OUTFITTERS MADE BY THE GOVERNOR
6 AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
9 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
10 As members of the Board of Outfitters, in accordance with section 2-15-1773, MCA:
11 Ernest Barker, Augusta, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2028.
12 Chris Gentry, Ennis, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2027.
13 Matthew Greemore, Twin Bridges, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2025.
14 Wagner Harmon, Bainville, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2027.
15 John Way, Ennis, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending June 30, 2026.
16
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
18 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
19 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
20 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
21 MCA.
22 - END -
