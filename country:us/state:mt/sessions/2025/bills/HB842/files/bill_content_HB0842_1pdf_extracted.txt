2026
69th Legislature 2025 HB 842.1
2 INTRODUCED BY B. BARKER, K. WALSH, G. KMETZ, G. NIKOLAKAKOS, G. PARRY, E. ALBUS, J.
3 DARLING, S. ESSMANN, S. KELLY
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE LEGISLATURE MAY ESTABLISH A
6 SALES TAX NOT TO EXCEED 4% USED SOLELY TO REDUCE PROPERTY TAXES THAT FUND PUBLIC
7 SCHOOLS AND THE MONTANA UNIVERSITY SYSTEM; AND PROVIDING THAT THE PROPOSED ACT BE
8 SUBMITTED TO THE QUALIFIED ELECTORS OF MONTANA.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Sales tax to reduce education property taxes -- exemptions. (1) The
13 legislature may enact a sales tax not to exceed 4% to reduce property taxes that fund public schools and the
14 Montana university system.
15 (2) If enacted, a sales tax should minimize repeated taxation by taxing final goods and services
16 and exempt the following:
17 (a) housing and utilities;
18 (b) groceries;
19 (c) fuel used for transportation and agriculture;
20 (d) health care; and
21 (e) financial and insurance services.
22
23 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
24 integral part of Title 15, and the provisions of Title 15 apply to [section 1].
25
26 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on approval by the electorate.
27
28 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Submission to electorate. [This act] shall be submitted to the qualified
