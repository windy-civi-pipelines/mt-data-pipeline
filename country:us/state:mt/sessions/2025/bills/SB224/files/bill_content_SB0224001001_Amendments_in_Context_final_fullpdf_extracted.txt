10 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ESTABLISHING INDIGENOUS PEOPLES' DAY AS A LEGAL
11 HOLIDAY IN MONTANA; AND AMENDING SECTION 1-1-216, MCA.”
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
14
15 Section 1. Section 1-1-216, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "1-1-216. Legal holidays and business days. (1) The following are legal holidays in the state of
17 Montana:
18 (a) Each Sunday;
19 (b) New Year's Day, January 1;
20 (c) Martin Luther King Jr. Day, the third Monday in January;
21 (d) Lincoln's and Washington's BirthdaysPresidents' Day, the third Monday in February;
22 (e) Memorial Day, the last Monday in May;
23 (f) Independence Day, July 4;
24 (g) Labor Day, the first Monday in September;
25 (h) Indigenous Peoples' Day and Columbus Day, the second Monday in October;
26 (i) Veterans' Day, November 11;
27 (j) Thanksgiving Day, the fourth Thursday in November;
28 (k) Christmas Day, December 25;
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – SB 224
Amendment - 1st Reading-white - Requested by: (S) State Administration
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Andria Hardin, SB0224.001.001
1 (l) State general election day.
2 (2) (a) If any of the holidays in subsection (1)(b) through (1)(l) fall on a Sunday, the Monday
3 following is a holiday.
4 (b) If any of the holidays in subsection (1)(b) through (1)(l) fall on a Saturday, the Friday preceding
5 is a holiday.
6 (c) All other days are business days."
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Notification to Montana supreme court. The secretary of state shall
9 send a copy of [this act] to the Montana supreme court. The Montana supreme court is requested to update the
10 definition of "legal holiday" in its rules of civil procedure to reflect the provisions of [section 1].
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Notification to local governments. The secretary of state shall send a
13 copy of [this act] to the Montana league of cities and towns and to the Montana association of counties.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Notification to tribal governments. The secretary of state shall send a
16 copy of [this act] to each federally recognized tribal government in Montana.
17 - END -
