69th Legislature 2025 LC0002.1
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ESTABLISHING INDIGENOUS PEOPLES' DAY AS A LEGAL
5 HOLIDAY IN MONTANA; AND AMENDING SECTION 1-1-216, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 1-1-216, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "1-1-216. Legal holidays and business days. (1) The following are legal holidays in the state of
11 Montana:
12 (a) Each Sunday;
13 (b) New Year's Day, January 1;
14 (c) Martin Luther King Jr. Day, the third Monday in January;
15 (d) Lincoln's and Washington's Birthdays, the third Monday in February;
16 (e) Memorial Day, the last Monday in May;
17 (f) Independence Day, July 4;
18 (g) Labor Day, the first Monday in September;
19 (h) Indigenous Peoples' Day and Columbus Day, the second Monday in October;
20 (i) Veterans' Day, November 11;
21 (j) Thanksgiving Day, the fourth Thursday in November;
22 (k) Christmas Day, December 25;
23 (l) State general election day.
24 (2) (a) If any of the holidays in subsection (1)(b) through (1)(l) fall on a Sunday, the Monday
25 following is a holiday.
26 (b) If any of the holidays in subsection (1)(b) through (1)(l) fall on a Saturday, the Friday preceding
27 is a holiday.
28 (c) All other days are business days."
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – ****
****
69th Legislature 2025 LC0002.1
1
2 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Notification to Montana supreme court. The secretary of state shall
3 send a copy of [this act] to the Montana supreme court. The Montana supreme court is requested to update the
4 definition of "legal holiday" in its rules of civil procedure to reflect the provisions of [section 1].
5
6 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Notification to local governments. The secretary of state shall send a
7 copy of [this act] to the Montana league of cities and towns and to the Montana association of counties.
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Notification to tribal governments. The secretary of state shall send a
10 copy of [this act] to each federally recognized tribal government in Montana.
11 - END -
