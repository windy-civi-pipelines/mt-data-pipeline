69th Legislature 2025 HB 815.1
2 INTRODUCED BY F. NAVE, D. BEDEY, L. REKSTEN
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT CREATING A PROCESS AND TIMELINE FOR TRANSFERRING
5 ADMINISTRATION OF SCHOOL ELECTIONS FROM SCHOOL CLERKS TO COUNTY ELECTION
6 ADMINISTRATORS; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Oversight of school elections -- timeline -- role of school districts
11 and county election administrators. (1) The legislature intends that no later than July 1, 2029, all Montana
12 school elections will be administered by county election administrators.
13 (2) The office of public instruction, county superintendents of schools, and county election
14 administrators shall, no later than March 1, 2026, provide a report to the state administration and veterans'
15 affairs interim committee on the statewide plan for moving administration of school elections from school clerks
16 to county election administrators. The report must include:
17 (a) a description of the existing structure for administering school elections, including but not
18 limited to the types of school elections, current school election deadlines, and the current division of
19 responsibilities between the school clerks and the county election administrators;
20 (b) any financial and logistical concerns identified while developing the statewide plan;
21 (c) a timeline for the transition of duties; and
22 (d) any other information the office of public instruction, county superintendents of schools, and
23 county election administrators considers relevant to provide to the state administration and veterans' affairs
24 interim committee.
25 (3) The state administration and veterans' affairs interim committee shall consider the report and
26 make recommendations to the 70th legislature on statutory changes needed to implement the transition.
27
28 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
