69th Legislature 2025 HB 221.1
2 INTRODUCED BY L. SCHUBERT, W. GALT, E. ALBUS, K. LOVE, R. GREGG, T. MILLETT, E. BUTTREY, C.
3 HINKLE, G. HUNTER, R. MARSHALL, J. KASSMIER, D. LOGE, M. REGIER, B. LER, J. SCHILLINGER, K.
4 ZOLNIKOV, L. DEMING, B. BARKER, L. BREWSTER, N. DURAM, T. FALK, G. HERTZ, D. LENZ, B.
5 MERCER, B. MITCHELL, G. NIKOLAKAKOS, J. TREBAS, Z. WIRTH, S. MANESS, K. BOGNER, J. HINKLE
6
7 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT MOVING MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS TO EVEN-NUMBERED
8 YEARS; PROVIDING A TRANSITION FOR MUNICIPAL OFFICERS; AMENDING SECTION 13-1-104, MCA;
9 AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 13-1-104, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "13-1-104. Times for holding general elections. (1) A general election must be held throughout the
15 state on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November.
16 (2) In every even-numbered year, the following elections must be held on general election day:
17 (a) an election on any ballot issue submitted to electors pursuant to Article III, section 6, unless the
18 legislature orders a special election, or Article XIV, section 8, of the Montana constitution;
19 (b) an election of federal officers, members of the legislature, state officers, multicounty district
20 officers elected at a statewide election, district court judges, and county officers; and
21 (c) an election of officers for municipalities required by law to hold the election; and
22 (c)(d) any other election required by law to be held on general election day in an even-numbered
23 year.
24 (3) In every odd-numbered year, the following elections must be held on the same day as the
25 general election:
26 (a) an election of officers for municipalities required by law to hold the election; and
27 (b) any other Only an election required by law to be held on general election day in an odd-
28 numbered year may be held in an odd-numbered year."
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 221
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 221.1
1
2 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Transition. (1) For a municipal officer whose term of office ends in
3 January 2026, there must be an election in 2025 for a one-year term of office.
4 (2) For a municipal officer whose term of office ends in January 2028 or January 2030, there must
5 be a special election for a one-year term proceeding when their term of office is set to end. This special election
6 must take place during the preceding even-numbered year general election.
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Saving clause. [This act] does not affect rights and duties that matured,
9 penalties that were incurred, or proceedings that were begun before [the effective date of this act].
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Applicability. [This act] applies to all municipal elections scheduled on
14 or after [the effective date of this act].
15 - END -
