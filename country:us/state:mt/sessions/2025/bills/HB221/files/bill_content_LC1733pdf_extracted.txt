(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT MOVING MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS TO EVEN-NUMBERED
5 YEARS; PROVIDING A TRANSITION FOR MUNICIPAL OFFICERS; AMENDING SECTION 13-1-104, MCA;
6 AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 13-1-104, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "13-1-104. Times for holding general elections. (1) A general election must be held throughout the
12 state on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November.
13 (2) In every even-numbered year, the following elections must be held on general election day:
14 (a) an election on any ballot issue submitted to electors pursuant to Article III, section 6, unless the
15 legislature orders a special election, or Article XIV, section 8, of the Montana constitution;
16 (b) an election of federal officers, members of the legislature, state officers, multicounty district
17 officers elected at a statewide election, district court judges, and county officers; and
18 (c) an election of officers for municipalities required by law to hold the election; and
19 (d) any other election required by law to be held on general election day in an even-numbered
20 year.
21 (3) In every odd-numbered year, the following elections must be held on the same day as the
22 general election:
23 ( a)an election of officers for municipalities required by law to hold the election; and
24 (b) any other Only an election required by law to be held on general election day in an odd-
25 numbered year may be held in an odd-numbered year."
26
27 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Transition. (1) For a municipal officer whose term of office ends in
28 January 2026, there must be an election in 2025 for a one-year term of office.

1 (2) For a municipal officer whose term of office ends in January 2028 or January 2030, there must
2 be a special election for a one-year term proceeding when their term of office is set to end. This special election
3 must take place during the preceding even-numbered year general election.
4
5 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Saving clause. [This act] does not affect rights and duties that matured,
6 penalties that were incurred, or proceedings that were begun before [the effective date of this act].
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Applicability. [This act] applies to all municipal elections scheduled on
11 or after [the effective date of this act].
