4 A JOINT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
5 MONTANA RECOGNIZING THE 250TH BIRTHDAY OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.
6
7 WHEREAS, the Montana Legislature wishes to acknowledge milestones in the history of the United
8 States of America; and
9 WHEREAS, colonists in the British colonies in North America were frustrated by the overreach of the
10 power of the British King and Parliament; and
11 WHEREAS, the colonists considered themselves more American and believed they were under forced
12 British rule; and
13 WHEREAS, the colonial leaders wrote a Declaration of Independence, issued on July 4, 1776, breaking
14 away from British rule; and
15 WHEREAS, the Declaration of Independence enunciates grievances against British rule; and
16 WHEREAS, the Preamble of the Declaration of Independence states that "all men are created equal,
17 that they are endowed by their Creator with certain unalienable Rights"; and
18 WHEREAS, the Declaration of Independence declares further that all men have the right to "Life,
19 Liberty and the pursuit of Happiness"; and
20 WHEREAS, France and Spain joined in wartime alliance with the American colonies under the
21 command of George Washington, who would become the first President of the United States; and
22 WHEREAS, sixteen United States Presidents can trace their ancestry to those who fought or provided
23 assistance in the War for American Independence; and
24 WHEREAS, in the year 2026, the United States of America will commemorate its 250th Anniversary in
25 celebrating our "Declaration of Independence"; and
26 WHEREAS, the Montana Legislature desires to promote this unique, patriotic anniversary and urge all
27 Montanans to celebrate and learn about the Declaration of Independence and America's founding; and
28 WHEREAS, the Sons of the American Revolution was founded on April 30, 1889, 100 years after the
- 1 - LC 1645
69th Legislature 2025 LC 1645
1 inauguration of George Washington; and
2 WHEREAS, the Daughters of the American Revolution was founded on October 11, 1890; and
3 WHEREAS, both the Sons of the American Revolution and the Daughters of the American Revolution
4 share a mission to promote American history and the appreciation of our founding documents.
5
6 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
7 THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8 That we, the members of the 2025 Montana Legislature, applaud the goals and educational outreach of
9 both the Sons of the American Revolution and the Daughters of the American Revolution and partners
10 previously mentioned, and convey to these organizations our heartfelt best wishes as we celebrate the 250th
11 Anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 2026.
12 - END -
