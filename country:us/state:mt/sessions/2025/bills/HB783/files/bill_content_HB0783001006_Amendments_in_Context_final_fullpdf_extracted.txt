5 MEDICATIONS RELATING TO DIABETES, OBESITY, OR POLYCYSTIC OVARY SYNDROME; PROVIDING

11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Coverage of certain treatments and medications relating to diabetes
14 and obesity and polycystic ovary syndrome -- state group insurance. (1) (a) Each individual disability
15 policy, certificate of insurance, and membership contract that is delivered, issued for delivery, renewed,
16 extended, or modified in this state An insurance contract or plan issued under this part must provide coverage
17 for:
18 (i) glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists for the treatment of class 3 or higher obesity,
19 polycystic ovary syndrome, or other conditions these drugs are authorized to treat by the food and drug
20 administration if it is determined to be medically necessary; and
21 (ii) polycystic ovary syndrome if it is determined to be medically necessary.
22 (b) For the purposes of subsection (1)(a), the term "medically necessary" includes but is not limited
23 to a diagnosis of diabetes or class 3 obesity or polycystic ovary syndrome.
24 (2) Coverage under this section may be subject to deductibles, coinsurance, and copayment
25 provisions. Special deductible, coinsurance, copayment, or other limitations that are not generally applicable to
26 other medical services covered under the plan may not be imposed on coverage of glucagon-like peptide-1
27 receptor agonists. A health insurance issuer may use cost containment measures, which may include but are
28 not limited to step therapy and prior authorization.
