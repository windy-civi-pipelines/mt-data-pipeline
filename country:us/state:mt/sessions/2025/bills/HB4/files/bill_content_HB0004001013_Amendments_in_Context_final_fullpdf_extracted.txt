6 APPROPRIATED BY BUDGET AMENDMENT TO VARIOUS STATE AGENCIES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR

13 provide necessary expenditures for the years for which the appropriations are made. The unspent balance of
14 an appropriation reverts to the fund from which it was appropriated on conclusion of the final fiscal year for
15 which its expenditures are authorized by [section 2] and this section.
