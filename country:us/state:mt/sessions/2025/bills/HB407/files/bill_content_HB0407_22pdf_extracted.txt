19 NEW SECTION. SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE -- PURPOSE. (1) [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] MAY BE CITED AS THE
20 "KRATOM CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT".
21 (2) IT IS THE PURPOSE OF [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] TO PROHIBIT THE SELLING, GIVING, OR OTHER
22 DISTRIBUTION OF KRATOM PRODUCTS TO THE PUBLIC UNLESS SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7]
23 OR OTHER STATUTORY ACT OF THE LEGISLATURE. IT IS THE EXPRESS PURPOSE OF [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] TO
24 PROHIBIT THE SALE OF KRATOM PRODUCTS TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE.
25
26 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. DEFINITIONS. AS USED IN [sections 1 through 7], UNLESS CONTEXT REQUIRES
27 OTHERWISE, THE FOLLOWING DEFINITIONS APPLY:
28 (1) "ATTRACTIVE TO CHILDREN" MEANS KRATOM PRODUCTS MANUFACTURED IN THE SHAPE OF CARTOONS

1 OR ANIMALS OR MANUFACTURED OR PACKAGED IN A FORM THAT BEARS ANY REASONABLE RESEMBLANCE TO AN EXISTING
2 CANDY PRODUCT THAT IS FAMILIAR TO THE PUBLIC AS A WIDELY DISTRIBUTED, BRANDED FOOD PRODUCT SO THAT A
3 PRODUCT COULD BE MISTAKEN FOR THE BRANDED PRODUCT, ESPECIALLY BY CHILDREN.
4 (2) "DEPARTMENT" MEANS THE DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE.
5 (3) "KRATOM" MEANS THE PLANT MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA OR ANY PART OF THAT PLANT.
6
7 (4) "KRATOM DISTRIBUTOR" MEANS A PERSON IMPORTING INTO OR PURCHASING IN MONTANA KRATOM
8 PRODUCTS FOR SALE OR RESALE TO KRATOM RETAILERS REGISTERED IN MONTANA.
9 (5) "KRATOM EXTRACT" MEANS A SUBSTANCE OR COMPOUND OBTAINED BY EXTRACTION OF THE
10 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA LEAF THAT IS INTENDED FOR INGESTION AND CONTAINS MORE THAN TRACE AMOUNTS OF
11 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA AND CONTAINS OTHER ALKALOIDS OF THE KRATOM PLANT, AND WHICH DOES NOT CONTAIN ANY
12 CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES OR LEVELS OF RESIDUAL SOLVENTS HIGHER THAN IS ALLOWED IN THE UNITED STATES
13 PHARMACOPEIA 467.
14
15 (6) "KRATOM PROCESSOR" MEANS A PERSON WHO MANUFACTURERS OR PRODUCES KRATOM PRODUCTS.
16
17 (7) "KRATOM PRODUCT" MEANS A FINISHED ARTICLE CONTAINING ANY PART OF A LEAF OF THE PLANT
18 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA IN FRESH, DEHYDRATED, OR DRIED FORM, OR A KRATOM EXTRACT, INCLUDING KRATOM
19 BEVERAGES PREPARED AND SOLD IN FOOD ESTABLISHMENTS.
20 (8) "KRATOM RETAILER" MEANS A PERSON THAT SELLS OR OTHERWISE PROVIDES KRATOM PRODUCTS TO
21 THE PUBLIC.
22 (9) "PRODUCTION BATCH" MEANS A QUANTITY OF KRATOM PRODUCT FOR INDIVIDUAL USE FROM A
23 PROCESSED LOT.
24 (10) "SYNTHESIZED MATERIAL" MEANS AN ALKALOID, METABOLITE, OR ALKALOID DERIVATIVE THAT HAS BEEN
25 CREATED BY CHEMICAL SYNTHESIS OR BIOSYNTHETIC MEANS, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO FERMENTATION,
26 RECOMBINANT TECHNIQUES, OR YEAST-DERIVED, ENZYMATIC TECHNIQUES, RATHER THAN TRADITIONAL FOOD
27 PREPARATION TECHNIQUES, SUCH AS HEATING OR EXTRACTING, THAT SYNTHETICALLY ALTERS THE COMPOSITION OF
28 ANY KRATOM ALKALOID OR CONSTITUENT.

2 NEW SECTION. SECTION 3. KRATOM PRODUCT RESTRICTIONS. A KRATOM PROCESSOR MAY NOT PREPARE,
3 DISTRIBUTE, SELL, OR EXPOSE FOR SALE A KRATOM PRODUCT THAT:
4 (1) CONTAINS A LEVEL OF 7-HYDROXYMITRAGYNINE IN THE ALKALOID FRACTION THAT IS GREATER THAN
5 2% OF THE ALKALOID COMPOSITION OF THE KRATOM PRODUCT;
6 (2) CONTAINS SYNTHESIZED MATERIAL;
7 (3) CONTAINS OR IS ADULTERATED WITH A DANGEROUS NON-KRATOM SUBSTANCE IF IT CONTAINS A
8 POISONOUS OR OTHERWISE DELETERIOUS NON-KRATOM INGREDIENT, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO THE SUBSTANCES
9 LISTED AS A CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES UNDER STATE OR FEDERAL LAW;
10 (4) ARE COMBUSTIBLE, INTENDED TO BE USED FOR VAPORIZATION, OR INJECTABLE;
11 (5) ARE MANUFACTURED IN A MANNER THAT IS ATTRACTIVE TO CHILDREN;
12
13 (6) DOES NOT HAVE PROOF OF APPROPRIATE QUALITY TESTING FOR EACH PRODUCTION BATCH FROM AN
14 ISO 17025 LABORATORY IN THE FORM OF A CERTIFICATE OF ANALYSIS REPRESENTING THAT:
15 (A) THE KRATOM PRODUCT DOES NOT CONTAIN LEVELS OF RESIDUAL SOLVENTS, BIOLOGICAL
16 CONTAMINANTS, OR HEAVY METAL CONTAMINANTS THAT MEET THE STANDARD FOR DIETARY SUPPLEMENT PRODUCTS;
17 AND
18
19 (B) THE PRODUCTION BATCH AS DEFINED IN [SECTION 2] MEETS CONCENTRATION AND CONTENT LIMITS; OR
20 (7) CONTAINS LEVELS OF RESIDUAL SOLVENTS HIGHER THAN IS ALLOWED IN [SECTION 2(5)].
