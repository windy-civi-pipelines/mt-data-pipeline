19 NEW SECTION. SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE -- PURPOSE. (1) [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] MAY BE CITED AS THE
20 "KRATOM CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT".
21 (2) IT IS THE PURPOSE OF [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] TO PROHIBIT THE SELLING, GIVING, OR OTHER
22 DISTRIBUTION OF KRATOM PRODUCTS TO THE PUBLIC UNLESS SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7]
23 OR OTHER STATUTORY ACT OF THE LEGISLATURE. IT IS THE EXPRESS PURPOSE OF [SECTIONS 1 THROUGH 7] TO
24 PROHIBIT THE SALE OF KRATOM PRODUCTS TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE.
25
26 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. DEFINITIONS. AS USED IN [sections 1 through 7], UNLESS CONTEXT REQUIRES
27 OTHERWISE, THE FOLLOWING DEFINITIONS APPLY:
28 (1) "ATTRACTIVE TO CHILDREN" MEANS KRATOM PRODUCTS MANUFACTURED IN THE SHAPE OF CARTOONS

1 OR ANIMALS OR MANUFACTURED OR PACKAGED IN A FORM THAT BEARS ANY REASONABLE RESEMBLANCE TO AN EXISTING
2 CANDY PRODUCT THAT IS FAMILIAR TO THE PUBLIC AS A WIDELY DISTRIBUTED, BRANDED FOOD PRODUCT SO THAT A
3 PRODUCT COULD BE MISTAKEN FOR THE BRANDED PRODUCT, ESPECIALLY BY CHILDREN.
4 (2) "DEPARTMENT" MEANS THE DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE.
5 (3) "KRATOM" MEANS THE PLANT MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA OR ANY PART OF THAT PLANT.
6 (4) "KRATOM DISTRIBUTOR" MEANS A PERSON IMPORTING INTO OR PURCHASING IN MONTANA KRATOM
7 PRODUCTS FOR SALE OR RESALE TO KRATOM RETAILERS REGISTERED IN MONTANA.
8 (5) "KRATOM EXTRACT" MEANS A SUBSTANCE OR COMPOUND OBTAINED BY EXTRACTION OF THE
9 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA LEAF THAT IS INTENDED FOR INGESTION AND CONTAINS MORE THAN TRACE AMOUNTS OF
10 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA AND CONTAINS OTHER ALKALOIDS OF THE KRATOM PLANT, AND WHICH DOES NOT CONTAIN ANY
11 CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES OR LEVELS OF RESIDUAL SOLVENTS HIGHER THAN IS ALLOWED IN THE UNITED STATES
12 PHARMACOPEIA 467.
13 (6) "KRATOM PROCESSOR" MEANS A PERSON WHO MANUFACTURERS OR PRODUCES KRATOM PRODUCTS.
14 (7) "KRATOM PRODUCT" MEANS A FINISHED ARTICLE CONTAINING ANY PART OF A LEAF OF THE PLANT
15 MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA IN FRESH, DEHYDRATED, OR DRIED FORM, OR A KRATOM EXTRACT, INCLUDING KRATOM
16 BEVERAGES PREPARED AND SOLD IN FOOD ESTABLISHMENTS.
17 (8) "KRATOM RETAILER" MEANS A PERSON THAT SELLS OR OTHERWISE PROVIDES KRATOM PRODUCTS TO
18 THE PUBLIC.
19 (9) "PRODUCTION BATCH" MEANS A QUANTITY OF KRATOM PRODUCT FOR INDIVIDUAL USE FROM A
20 PROCESSED LOT.
21 (10) "SYNTHESIZED MATERIAL" MEANS AN ALKALOID, METABOLITE, OR ALKALOID DERIVATIVE THAT HAS BEEN
22 CREATED BY CHEMICAL SYNTHESIS OR BIOSYNTHETIC MEANS, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO FERMENTATION,
23 RECOMBINANT TECHNIQUES, OR YEAST-DERIVED, ENZYMATIC TECHNIQUES, RATHER THAN TRADITIONAL FOOD
24 PREPARATION TECHNIQUES, SUCH AS HEATING OR EXTRACTING, THAT SYNTHETICALLY ALTERS THE COMPOSITION OF
25 ANY KRATOM ALKALOID OR CONSTITUENT.

27 NEW SECTION. SECTION 3. KRATOM PRODUCT RESTRICTIONS. A KRATOM PROCESSOR MAY NOT PREPARE,
28 DISTRIBUTE, SELL, OR EXPOSE FOR SALE A KRATOM PRODUCT THAT:

2 2% OF THE ALKALOID COMPOSITION OF THE KRATOM PRODUCT;
3 (2) CONTAINS SYNTHESIZED MATERIAL;
4 (3) CONTAINS OR IS ADULTERATED WITH A DANGEROUS NON-KRATOM SUBSTANCE IF IT CONTAINS A

10 NEW SECTION. SECTION 5. LABELING. (1) A KRATOM PRODUCT PRODUCED, MANUFACTURED,

16 (C) A STATEMENT THAT KRATOM MAY BE HABIT FORMING;

18 STATES FOOD AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION. THIS PRODUCT IS NOT INTENDED TO DIAGNOSE, TREAT, CURE, OR PREVENT

21 (F) DIRECTIONS FOR USE THAT INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING:

17 NEW SECTION. SECTION 6. DEPARTMENT -- ENFORCEMENT. (1) KRATOM PROCESSORS, KRATOM
18 DISTRIBUTORS, AND KRATOM RETAILERS ARE PROHIBITED FROM SELLING ANY KRATOM PRODUCT THAT DOES NOT HAVE A
19 CURRENT REGISTRATION WITH THE DEPARTMENT. A KRATOM PROCESSOR MAY ONLY SELL PRODUCTS TO A KRATOM
20 DISTRIBUTOR. A KRATOM DISTRIBUTOR MAY ONLY SELL KRATOM PRODUCTS TO A KRATOM RETAILER.
21 (2) PROCESSORS, DISTRIBUTORS, AND RETAILERS OF KRATOM PRODUCTS ARE SUBJECT TO THE
22 FOLLOWING PENALTIES:
23 (A) A FINE OF $10,000 ON THE PROCESSOR, DISTRIBUTOR, OR RETAILER FOR THE SALE OF EACH
24 UNREGISTERED PRODUCT OR FOR SALES TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE;
25 (B) A FINE OF $15,000 ON THE PROCESSOR, DISTRIBUTOR, OR THE RETAILER FOR A SECOND VIOLATION OF
26 THE SALE OF THE SAME UNREGISTERED PRODUCT OR FOR SALES TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE; OR
27 (C) FOR A THIRD VIOLATION FOR THE SALE OF AN UNREGISTERED PRODUCT OR THE SALE TO A PERSON
28 UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE, THE PROCESSOR, DISTRIBUTOR, OR RETAILER MUST BE PROHIBITED FROM THE SALE OF ANY
