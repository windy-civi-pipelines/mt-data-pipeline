Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 25.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 25
2 INTRODUCED BY W. GALT
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO HARD-ROCK MINING IMPACT BOARD MADE BY THE
6 GOVERNOR AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE
7 SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
10 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
11 As members of the Hard-Rock Mining Impact Board, in accordance with section 2-15-1822, MCA:
12 Jay Baum, Columbus, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
13 Clint Rech, Big Timber, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
14 Ray Sheldon, Huntley, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
15 Brent Teske, Libby, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
16 Mark Thompson, Butte, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
17
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
19 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
20 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
21 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
22 MCA.
23 - END -
