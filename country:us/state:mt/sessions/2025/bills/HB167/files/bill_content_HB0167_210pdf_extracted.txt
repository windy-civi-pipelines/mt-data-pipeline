- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0167.2
2 INTRODUCED BY J. DARLING, W. MCKAMEY, S. VANCE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REMOVING THE REQUIREMENT TO POST ABSENTEE LISTS
5 AT POLLING LOCATIONS; AMENDING SECTION SECTIONS 13-13-233, AND 13-13-234, MCA; AND
6 PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 13-13-233, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "13-13-233. Issuing and recording absentee ballots -- certificate to election judges. (1) Absentee
12 ballots must be official numbered paper ballots beginning with ballot number 1 and following consecutively
13 according to the number of applications for absentee ballots.
14 (2) The election administrator shall keep a record of all absentee ballots issued.
15 (3) When the election administrator delivers the voted absentee ballots pursuant to 13-13-232(1),
16 the THE election administrator shall KEEP A RECORD OF THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION SUBJECT TO THE APPROPRIATE
17 RETENTION SCHEDULE also provide a certificate stating:
18 (a) the ballot numbers of the absentee ballots mailed or transmitted pursuant to 13-13-214, 13-21-
19 106(3)(a), and 13-21-224, delivered pursuant to 13-13-229, or marked in person pursuant to 13-13-222;
20 (b) the number of ballots to be reserved for late absentee voting pursuant to 13-13-211(2); and
21 (c) the names of the electors within the precinct to whom the ballots were provided.
22 (4) The chief election judge shall post in a conspicuous location at the polling place a list of the
23 names of electors appearing on the certificate required under subsection (3)."
24
25 SECTION 2. SECTION 13-13-234, MCA, IS AMENDED TO READ:
26 "13-13-234. Duty of election judges -- pollbook. (1) The election judges, at the opening of the polls,
27 shall:
28 (a) note on the pollbook opposite the appropriate ballot numbers the fact that the ballots were
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 167

1 issued as absentee ballots; and
2 (b) reserve the numbers for electors who may vote late under 13-13-211(2).
3 (2) The election judges shall insert only the name of the elector entitled to each particular number
4 according to the certificate provided by the election administrator pursuant to 13-13-233(3) and the number of
5 the elector's ballot."
6
7 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
8 - END -
