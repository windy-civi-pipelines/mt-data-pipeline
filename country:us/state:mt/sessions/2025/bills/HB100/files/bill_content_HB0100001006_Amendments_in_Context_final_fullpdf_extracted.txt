13 Section 3. Section 2-6-1009, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "2-6-1009. Written notice of denial -- failure to meet response deadline -- civil action -- costs to
15 prevailing party in certain actions to enforce constitutional or statutory rights. (1) A public agency that
16 denies an information request to release information or records shall provide a written explanation for the
17 denial.
18 (2) If a person who makes an information request receives a denial from a public agency and
19 believes that the denial violates the provisions of this chapter, the person may file a complaint pursuant to the
20 Montana Rules of Civil Procedure in district court.
21 (3) If a person who makes an information request to an executive branch agency a public agency
22 that is not a local government does not receive a response from the agency as required in 2-6-1006(3), the
23 person may file a complaint in district court.
24 (4) A person alleging a deprivation of rights who prevails in an action brought in district court to
25 enforce the person's rights under Article II, section 9, of the Montana constitution or under the provisions of Title
26 2, chapter 6, parts 10 through 12, may be awarded costs and reasonable attorney fees."
