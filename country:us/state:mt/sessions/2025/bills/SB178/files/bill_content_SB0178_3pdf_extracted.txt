18 Section 3. Section 85-2-404, MCA, is amended to read:
19 "85-2-404. Abandonment of appropriation right. (1) If an appropriator ceases to use all or a part of
20 an appropriation right with the intention of wholly or partially abandoning the right or if the appropriator ceases
21 using the appropriation right according to its terms and conditions with the intention of not complying with those
22 terms and conditions, the appropriation right is, to that extent, considered abandoned and must immediately
23 expire.
24 (2) If an appropriator ceases to use all or part of an appropriation right or ceases using the
25 appropriation right according to its terms and conditions for a period of 10 successive years and there was
26 water available for use, there is a prima facie presumption that the appropriator has abandoned the right for the
27 part not used.

4 intent by the appropriator to wholly or partially abandon the appropriation right or to not comply with the terms
