4 A JOINT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
5 MONTANA CONGRATULATING DONALD J. TRUMP ON HIS INAUGURATION AS PRESIDENT OF THE
6 UNITED STATES.
7
8 WHEREAS, the citizens of the United States, including the steadfast people of Montana, have
9 exercised their constitutional right to vote and Donald J. Trump has been duly elected as the 47th President of
10 the United States of America, decisively defeating radical leftist Kamala Harris in the 2024 presidential election,
11 with the esteemed J.D. Vance elected as Vice President; and
12 WHEREAS, President Trump secured a resounding landslide victory in Montana, reflecting that
13 Montanans rejected the Democratic Party and its policies, winning 51 of the 56 counties in the state; and
14 WHEREAS, during his campaign, President Trump faced and triumphed over baseless, politically
15 motivated charges and relentless attacks orchestrated by partisan adversaries, demonstrating unparalleled
16 resilience and unwavering determination in his pursuit of the nation's highest office; and
17 WHEREAS, President Trump survived a heinous assassination attempt on July 13, 2024, during a
18 campaign rally in Butler, Pennsylvania, where he sustained a gunshot wound to his right ear, and a second
19 despicable assassination attempt was thwarted on September 15, 2024, at Trump International Golf Club in
20 West Palm Beach, Florida, where a suspect armed with a rifle was apprehended before causing harm; and
21 WHEREAS, the Montana Legislature overwhelmingly acknowledges and wholeheartedly supports
22 President Trump's unwavering commitment to strengthening the United States of America through sound
23 governance, a robust economy, and the promotion of unity among its citizens; and
24 WHEREAS, the state of Montana stands in full support of President Trump's decisive border policies,
25 including unwavering efforts to secure the southern border and enforce immigration laws, aiming to protect the
26 nation's sovereignty, ensure public safety, and uphold the rule of law; and
27 WHEREAS, the state of Montana vigorously supports and encourages President Trump in his
28 relentless efforts to combat the insidious "woke" agenda, aligning with Montana's steadfast commitment to
- 1 - LC 2659
69th Legislature 2025 LC 2659
1 uphold traditional values, preserve its cultural heritage, and safeguard the well-being of its citizens; and
2 WHEREAS, the people of Montana and the Montana Legislature urge President Trump to continue his
3 valiant fight against the radical left, specifically targeting individuals and groups such as "The Squad" and
4 Senator Bernie Sanders, who advocate for extreme progressive policies that threaten the foundational
5 principles of the republic and endanger the cherished Montana way of life; and
6 WHEREAS, the Montana Legislature extends its profound gratitude to President Trump for his
7 endorsement of Senator Tim Sheehy, whose candidacy led to the defeat of far-left radical Senator Jon Tester,
8 thereby contributing to the restoration of conservative representation for Montana in the United States Senate;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, the state of Montana opposes the far-left judicial attacks on President Trump, recognizing
11 that such politically motivated actions undermine the integrity of our legal system. Furthermore, Montana faces
12 similar challenges from activist judges who legislate from the bench, disregarding the will of the people and
13 overstepping their constitutional authority; and
14 WHEREAS, President Trump's visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to conservative
15 principles resonate deeply with the proud citizens of Montana, reflecting shared values of individual liberty,
16 economic prosperity, and steadfast adherence to the founding principles of our great nation; and
17 WHEREAS, Montana recognizes the paramount importance of supporting the Office of the President to
18 ensure a prosperous, secure, and sovereign future for all Americans; and
19 WHEREAS, President Trump took the Oath of Office on January 20, 2025, and has officially begun his
20 term as President, ready to fulfill the sacred duties of leading the nation, protecting its Constitution, and serving
21 its people with unwavering commitment.
22
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
24 THE STATE OF MONTANA:
25 (1) That the Montana Legislature proudly congratulates President Donald J. Trump on his
26 inauguration and expresses unwavering confidence in his ability to lead the nation during his term with strength
27 and integrity.
28 (2) That the Montana Legislature extends its best wishes for President Trump's success in
- 2 - LC 2659
69th Legislature 2025 LC 2659
1 promoting policies that uphold the rights, freedoms, and opportunities of all Americans and in restoring the
2 nation's greatness.
3 (3) That Montana reaffirms its unyielding commitment to work in close partnership with the federal
4 government in the pursuit of policies that benefit both Montana and the United States as a whole, ensuring the
5 prosperity and security of our citizens.
6 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Secretary of State transmit a copy of this resolute resolution to
7 the President of the United States Donald J. Trump, to the Vice President of the United States J.D. Vance, and
8 to each esteemed member of the Montana Congressional Delegation.
9 - END -
