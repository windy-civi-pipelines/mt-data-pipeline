4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REPEALING THE SUNSET DATE OF LAWS REGARDING
5 JUDICIAL REVIEW OF WATER RIGHTS; AUTHORIZING WATER COURT REVIEW OF CERTAIN
6 DECISIONS OF THE DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES AND CONSERVATION; CLARIFYING
7 WATER COURT DUTIES; REPEALING SECTION 6, CHAPTER 126, LAWS OF 2017; AND PROVIDING AN
8 IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Repealer. Section 6, Chapter 126, Laws of 2017, is repealed.
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
