69th Legislature 2025 LC 4137
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO THE PRESENTATION OF AN
5 ELECTRICIAN'S LICENSE; AND AMENDING SECTION 37-68-315, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 37-68-315, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "37-68-315. Presentation of license. (1) An employee of a private or public employment agency or
11 labor union, a A building code compliance inspector licensed in the state, an employee of the department, or a
12 person who is professionally responsible for a job site, or an electrician licensed under this chapter has the right
13 to ask a person who is working at a jobsite doing work that requires an electrician's license to present the
14 person's license. If the person performing the work is unable to furnish the person's license, the requesting
15 person may report that fact to the board or the department.
16 (2) An employee of the department may issue a citation to and collect a fine, as provided in 37-68-
17 316, from a person at a jobsite where the person is performing electrical work if the person fails to display an
18 electrician's license at the request of the department employee."
19 - END -
