Section 1:
69th Legislature - 2025 Drafter: Joseph Carroll, HJ0003.001.001
1 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. 3
2 INTRODUCED BY Z. WIRTH, S. GIST, L. JONES, R. MARSHALL, K. WALSH, R. MINER, G. KMETZ, B.
3 MITCHELL, G. PARRY
4
5 A JOINT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
6 MONTANA RECOMMENDING THE TRANSPORTATION COMMISSION PRIORITIZE CERTAIN PROJECTS
7 SERIOUSLY CONSIDER SAFETY IMPROVEMENTS AT BOWMAN'S CORNER.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Transportation Commission established by section 2-15-2502, MCA, selects highway
10 construction and reconstruction projects for prioritization, pursuant to section 60-2-110, MCA; and
11 WHEREAS, the members of the Legislature have a particularized interest in highway construction and
12 reconstruction projects due to their representation of small districts throughout the state.
13
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
15 THE STATE OF MONTANA:
16 That the Legislature recommends the Transportation Commission prioritize seriously consider safety
17 improvements at the intersection of U.S. Highway 287 and Montana Highway 200, popularly known as
18 Bowman's Corner.
19 - END -
