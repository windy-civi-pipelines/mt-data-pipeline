1 _____________ RESOLUTION NO. _____________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA CONCURRING IN, CONFIRMING, AND
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE PUBLIC SAFETY OFFICER STANDARDS AND TRAINING
6 COUNCIL MADE BY THE GOVERNOR AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED
7 JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
10 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
11 As members of the Public Safety Officer Standards and Training Council, in accordance with section
12 44-4-402, MCA:
13 Shellie Brady, Bozeman, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
14 Jess Edwards, Browning, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
15 Anthony Hutchings, Manhattan, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
16 Mark Kraft, Sidney, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
17 Meaghan Mulcahy, Butte, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
18 C. Kristine White, Forsyth, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
19
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
21 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
22 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
23 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
24 MCA.
25 - END -
