- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0411.2
2 INTRODUCED BY J. GILLETTE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT EXEMPTING AGRICULTURAL PROPERTY FROM OPEN SPACE
5 PROPERTY TAX LEVIES; AMENDING SECTION 7-6-2527, 76-6-109, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN
6 IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND A RETROACTIVEAPPLICABILITY DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10
11 SECTION 1. SECTION 76-6-109, MCA, IS AMENDED TO READ:
12 "76-6-109. Powers of public bodies -- county real property acquisition procedure maintained.
13 (1) A public body has the power to carry out the purposes and provisions of this chapter, including the following
14 powers in addition to others granted by this chapter:
15 (a) to borrow funds and make expenditures necessary to carry out the purposes of this chapter;
16 (b) to advance or accept advances of public funds;
17 (c) to apply for and accept and use grants and any other assistance from the federal government
18 and any other public or private sources, to give security as may be required, to enter into and carry out
19 contracts or agreements in connection with the assistance, and to include in any contract for assistance from
20 the federal government conditions imposed pursuant to federal laws as the public body may consider
21 reasonable and appropriate and that are not inconsistent with the purposes of this chapter;
22 (d) to make and execute contracts and other instruments necessary or convenient to the exercise
23 of its powers under this chapter;
24 (e) in connection with the real property acquired or designated for the purposes of this chapter, to
25 provide or to arrange or contract for the provision, construction, maintenance, operation, or repair by any
26 person or agency, public or private, of services, privileges, works, streets, roads, public utilities, or other
27 facilities or structures that may be necessary to the provision, preservation, maintenance, and management of
28 the property as open-space land;
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 411
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0411.2
1 (f) to insure or provide for the insurance of any real or personal property or operations of the
2 public body against any risks or hazards, including the power to pay premiums on the insurance;
3 (g) to demolish or dispose of any structures or facilities that may be detrimental to or inconsistent
4 with the use of real property as open-space land; and
5 (h) to exercise any of its functions and powers under this chapter jointly or cooperatively with
6 public bodies of one or more states, if they are authorized by state law, and with one or more public bodies of
7 this state and to enter into agreements for joint or cooperative action.
8 (2) For the purposes of this chapter, the state, a city, town, or other municipality, or a county may:
9 (a) appropriate funds;
10 (b) subject to 15-10-420, levy taxes and assessments according to existing codes and statutes;
11 (c) issue and sell its general obligation bonds in the manner and within the limitations prescribed
12 by the applicable laws of the state, subject to subsection (3); and
13 (d) exercise its powers under this chapter through a board or commission or through the office or
14 officers that its governing body by resolution determines or as the governor determines in the case of the state.
15 (3) Property taxes levied to pay Open space levies and property taxes pledged to the payment of
16 the principal and interest on general obligation bonds issued by a city, town, other municipality, or county
17 pursuant to this chapter may not be levied against the following property:
18 (a) agricultural land eligible for valuation, assessment, and taxation as agricultural land under 15-
19 7-202;
20 (b) forest land as defined in 15-44-102;
21 (c) all agricultural improvements on agricultural land referred to in subsection (3)(a);
22 (d) all noncommercial improvements on forest land referred to in subsection (3)(b); and
23 (e) agricultural implements and equipment described in 15-6-138(1)(a).
24 (4) This chapter does not supersede the provisions of Title 7, chapter 8, parts 22 and 25."
25
26 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. APPLICABILITY. [THIS ACT] APPLIES TO ALL OPEN SPACE LEVIES AND BONDS,
27 REGARDLESS OF THE APPROVAL DATE, AND TO PROPERTY TAX YEARS BEGINNING ON OR AFTER JANUARY 1, 2026.
28 - END -
