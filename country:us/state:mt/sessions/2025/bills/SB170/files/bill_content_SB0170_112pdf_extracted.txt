Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 SB 170.1
2 INTRODUCED BY J. WINDY BOY, C.NEUMANN
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ELIMINATING THE TERMINATION DATE OF THE COMMUNITY
5 HEALTH AIDE PROGRAM; AMENDING SECTION 5, CHAPTER 412, LAWS OF 2019; REPEALING SECTION
6 7, CHAPTER 412, LAWS OF 2019, AND SECTION 1, CHAPTER 298, LAWS OF 2023; AND PROVIDING AN
7 IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 5, Chapter 412, Laws of 2019, is amended to read:
12 "Section 5. Direction to department of public health and human services. The department of
13 public health and human services is directed to apply to the centers for medicare and medicaid services for a
14 state plan amendment or a research and demonstration project waiver as may be needed to authorize medicaid
15 coverage of services provided by individuals certified in accordance with [section 2]."
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Repealer. Section 7, Chapter 412, Laws of 2019, and section 1, Chapter
18 298, Laws of 2023, are repealed.
19
20 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Notification to tribal governments. The secretary of state shall send a
21 copy of [this act] to each federally recognized tribal government in Montana.
22
23 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
24 - END -
