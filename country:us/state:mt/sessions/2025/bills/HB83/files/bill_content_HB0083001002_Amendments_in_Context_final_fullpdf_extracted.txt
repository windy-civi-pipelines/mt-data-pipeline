Section 1:
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Rachel Weiss, HB0083.001.002
1 HOUSE BILL NO. 83
2 INTRODUCED BY T. RUNNING WOLF
3 BY REQUEST OF THE STATE TRIBAL RELATIONS COMMITTEE
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT CREATING THE MISSING INDIGENOUS PERSONS TASK
6 FORCE STATE SPECIAL REVENUE ACCOUNT; PROVIDING THAT THE FUNDS IN THE ACCOUNT ARE
7 SUBJECT TO THE LEGISLATIVE AUDIT ACT; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION; AND PROVIDING AN
8 EFFECTIVE DATE AND A TERMINATION DATE.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Missing indigenous persons task force account -- audit. (1) There is
13 a missing indigenous persons task force account in the state special revenue fund established in 17-2-102. The
14 account is administered by the department of justice.
15 (2) Money transferred from any lawful source, including but not limited to gifts, grants, donations,
16 securities, and other assets, public or private, may be deposited in the account.
17 (3) Money deposited in the account must be used pursuant to 44-2-411 and is subject to audits as
18 required by Title 5, chapter 13, part 3.
19 (4) Funds deposited in the account each year must be used by the department only for allowable
20 costs as determined by the department and approved by the missing indigenous persons task force.
21 (5) Interest and income earned on the account and any unspent or unencumbered money in the
22 account at the end of a fiscal year must remain in the account.
23
24 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Appropriation. There is appropriated $1 from the missing indigenous
25 persons task force account established in [section 1] to the department of justice for the biennium beginning
26 July 1, 2025, for the purposes described in 44-2-411.
27
28 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Notification to tribal governments. The secretary of state shall send a

6 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
