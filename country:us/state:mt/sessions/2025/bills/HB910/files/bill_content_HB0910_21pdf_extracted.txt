4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE TAXATION OF VAPOR PRODUCTS;
5 REVISING THE DEFINITION OF "TOBACCO PRODUCTS"; PROVIDING A DEFINITION; AMENDING
6 SECTION SECTIONS 16-11-102, AND 16-11-111, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”

9
10 Section 1. Section 16-11-102, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "16-11-102. Definitions. (1) As used in this chapter, the following definitions apply, unless the context
12 requires otherwise:
13 (a) "Contraband" means:
14 (i) any tobacco product possessed, sold, offered for sale, distributed, held, owned, acquired,
15 transported, imported, or caused to be imported in violation of this part;
16 (ii) any cigarette or roll-your-own tobacco that is possessed, sold, offered for sale, distributed,
17 held, owned, acquired, transported, imported, or caused to be imported in violation of part 4 or 5;
18 (iii) any cigarettes that bear trademarks that are counterfeit under state or federal trademark laws;
19 (iv) any cigarettes bearing false or counterfeit insignia or tax stamps from any state; or
20 (v) any cigarettes or tobacco products that violate 16-10-306.
21 (b) "Department" means the department of revenue provided for in 2-15-1301.
22 (c) "Person" means an individual, firm, partnership, corporation, association, company, committee,
23 other group of persons, or other business entity, however formed.
24 (2) As used in this part, the following definitions apply, unless the context requires otherwise:
25 (a) "Cigarette" means any product that contains nicotine, is intended to be burned or heated under
26 ordinary conditions of use, and consists of or contains:
27 (i) any roll of tobacco wrapped in paper or in any substance not containing tobacco;
28 (ii) tobacco, in any form, that is functional in the product and that, because of its appearance, the

4 appearance or the type of tobacco used in the filler and regardless of its packaging and labeling, is likely to be
5 offered to or purchased by consumers as a cigarette described in subsection (2)(a)(i).
6 (b) "Controlling person" means a person who owns an equity interest of 10% or more of a business
7 or the equivalent.
8 (c) "Directory" means the tobacco product directory as provided in 16-11-504.
9 (d) "Full face value of insignia" means the total amount of the tax levied under this part.

11 the provisions of this part.
12 (f) "Licensed retailer" means any person, other than a wholesaler, subjobber, or tobacco product
13 vendor, who is licensed under the provisions of this part.
14 (g) "Licensed subjobber" means a subjobber licensed under the provisions of this part. The person
15 must be treated as a wholesaler.
16 (h) "Licensed wholesaler" means a wholesaler licensed under the provisions of this part.
17 (i) "Manufacturer" means any person who fabricates tobacco products from raw materials for the
18 purpose of resale.
19 (j) "Manufacturer's original container" means the original master shipping case or original shipping
20 case used by the tobacco product manufacturer to ship multipack units, such as boxes, cartons, and sleeves, to
21 warehouse distribution points.

25 leaf, is not wrapped by a machine, and does not contain a filter, tip, or any characterizing nontobacco flavor.
26 (ii) The term does not include a cigarette.

3 SECTION 2. Section 16-11-111, MCA, is amended to read:
4 "16-11-111. Cigarette, tobacco products, and moist snuff sales tax -- exemption for sale to
5 tribal member. (1) (a) A tax on the purchase of cigarettes for consumption, use, or any purpose other than
6 resale in the regular course of business is imposed and must be precollected by the wholesaler and paid to the
7 state of Montana. The tax is $1.70 on each package containing 20 cigarettes. Whenever packages contain
8 other than 20 cigarettes, there is a tax on each cigarette equal to 1/20 the tax on a package containing 20
9 cigarettes.
10 (b) The tax computed under subsection (1)(a) applies to illegally packaged cigarettes under 16-11-
11 307.
12 (2) The tax imposed in subsection (1) does not apply to quota cigarettes.
13 (3) Subject to the refund or credit provided in subsection (4), the tax must be precollected on all
14 cigarettes entering a Montana Indian reservation.
15 (4) Pursuant to the procedure provided in subsection (5), a wholesaler making a sale of cigarettes
16 to a retailer within the boundaries of a Montana Indian reservation may apply to the department for a refund or
17 credit for taxes precollected on cigarettes sold by the retailer to a member of the federally recognized Indian
18 tribe or tribes on whose reservation the sale is made. A wholesaler who does not file a claim within 1 year of the
19 shipment date forfeits the refund or credit.
20 (5) The distribution of tax-free cigarettes to a tribal member must be implemented through a
21 system of preapproved wholesaler shipments. A licensed Montana wholesaler shall contact the department for
22 approval prior to the shipment of the untaxed cigarettes. The department may authorize sales based on
23 whether the quota, as established in a cooperative agreement between the department and an Indian tribe or
24 as set out in this chapter, has been met. If authorized as a tax-exempt sale, the wholesaler, upon providing
25 proof of order and delivery to a retailer within the boundaries of a Montana Indian reservation selling cigarettes
26 to members of a federally recognized tribe or tribes of that reservation, must be given a refund or credit. Once
27 the quota has been filled, the department shall immediately notify all affected wholesalers that further sales on
28 that reservation must be taxed and that a claim for a refund or credit will not be honored for the remainder of
- 5 - Authorized Print Version – HB 910
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0910.2
1 the quota period. Quota allocations are not transferable between quota periods or between reservations.
2 (6) The total amount of refunds or credits allowed by the department to all wholesalers claiming the
3 refund or credit under subsection (4) for any month may not exceed an amount that is equal to the tax due on
4 the quota allocation. The department shall determine the amount of refunds or credits for each Indian
5 reservation at the beginning of each fiscal year, using the most recent census data available from the bureau of
6 Indian affairs or as provided in a cooperative agreement with the tribe or tribes of the Indian reservation.
7 (7) There must be collected and paid to the state of Montana a tax of 50% of the wholesale price,
8 to the wholesaler, of all tobacco products other than cigarettes, premium cigars, vapor products, and moist
9 snuff. The tax on a premium cigar is the lesser of 50% of the wholesale price or 35 cents a premium cigar. The
10 tax on moist snuff is 85 cents an ounce based upon the net weight of the package listed by the manufacturer.
11 For packages of moist snuff that are less than or greater than 1 ounce, the tax must be proportional to the size
12 of the package. The tax on vapor products that contain nicotine is 20% of the wholesale price. Tobacco
13 products shipped from Montana and destined for retail sale and consumption outside the state are not subject
14 to this tax.
15 (8) The tax imposed by subsection (7) must be precollected and paid by a wholesaler to the
16 department upon sale to a Montana retailer. A wholesaler who fails to report or pay the tax required by this part
17 must be assessed penalty and interest as provided in 15-1-216.
18 (9) A retailer who purchases tobacco products for resale on which the tobacco products tax has
19 not been collected and paid to the department shall comply with all the provisions of this part and the rules
20 adopted to implement this part as if it were a wholesaler.
21 (10) A retailer must assume that the tobacco products tax has not been collected and paid to the
22 department in the absence of a statement on the retailer's invoice or sales slip for the tobacco products that
23 states that the applicable Montana tobacco products tax is included in the total billing cost."
24
25 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Applicability. [This act] applies to tobacco products sold by wholesalers
26 after June 30, 2025.
