69th Legislature 2025 HB 235.1
2 INTRODUCED BY N. DURAM, E. BYRNE, C. SCHOMER, G. HUNTER, D. LOGE, M. NOLAND

4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING MOTOR VEHICLE LAW TO PROVIDE
5 FOR THE VOLUNTARY CANCELLATION OR SUSPENSION OF A VEHICLE'S REGISTRATION; PROVIDING
6 FOR THE LIABILITY AND IMPLEADER OF A PERSON USING A VEHICLE WITH A CANCELED OR
7 SUSPENDED REGISTRATION; PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 61-
8 6-301 AND 61-14-101, MCA.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Voluntary registration cancellation. (1) A person whose vehicle is
13 registered in the state may cancel the registration in a manner prescribed by department rulemaking. The
14 person shall remove the vehicle's license plates if the vehicle is in the person's possession. If the vehicle is
15 registered to multiple adults, they must all consent to the cancellation.
16 (2) A vehicle with a canceled registration may not be driven on the ways of this state open to the
17 public.
18
19 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Voluntary registration suspension. (1) A person whose vehicle is
20 registered in the state may suspend the registration in a manner prescribed by department rulemaking. If the
21 vehicle is registered to multiple adults, they must all consent to the suspension.
22 (2) A vehicle with a suspended registration may not be driven on the ways of this state open to the
23 public.
24
25 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Responsibility and impleader of driver of vehicle with canceled or
26 suspended registration. (1) The user of a vehicle with a canceled or suspended registration must be liable for
27 all tolls, fines, and damages arising from the operation of the vehicle.
28 (2) The owner of a vehicle with a canceled or suspended registration may implead the driver of the

3 Section 4. Section 61-6-301, MCA, is amended to read:
4 "61-6-301. Required motor vehicle insurance -- family member exclusion. (1) (a) Except as
5 provided in subsection (1)(b), an owner of a motor vehicle that is registered and operated in Montana the state
6 by the owner or with the owner's permission shall continuously provide insurance against loss resulting from
7 liability imposed by law for bodily injury or death or damage to property suffered by any person caused by
8 maintenance or use of a motor vehicle in an amount not less than that required by 61-6-103, or a certificate of
9 self-insurance issued in accordance with 61-6-143.
10 (b) (i) Notwithstanding the mandatory motor vehicle liability insurance protection provided for in
11 subsection (1)(a), nothing in this part may be construed to prohibit the exclusion from insurance coverage of a
12 named family member in a motor vehicle liability insurance policy.
13 (ii) The owner of a vehicle registered in the state is not obligated to provide insurance as required
14 by subsection (1)(a) after cancelling the registration of the vehicle under [section 1] or during a period of
15 suspended registration of the vehicle under [section 2].
16 (2) It is unlawful for a person to operate a motor vehicle upon on ways of this state open to the
17 public as defined in 61-8-101 without a valid policy of liability insurance in effect in an amount not less than that
18 required by 61-6-103 unless the person has been issued a certificate of self-insurance under 61-6-143 or is
19 operating a vehicle exempt under 61-6-303."
20

23 registration of motor vehicles, including:
