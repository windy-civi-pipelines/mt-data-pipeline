16 Section 1. Section 44-1-504, MCA, is amended to read:
17 "44-1-504. (Temporary) Special revenue account to partially fund highway patrol officers'
18 salaries expenses. (1) There is an account in the state special revenue fund provided for in 17-2-102.
19 (2) The money in the account is for the department of justice to fund, pursuant to 2-18-303(5):
20 (a) the base salary and associated operating costs for highway patrol officer positions; and
21 (b) biennial salary increases for highway patrol officers; and
22 (c) other operating expenses of the highway patrol.
23 (3) (a) By August 15, 2021, the state treasurer shall transfer $4 million from the account to the
24 highway patrol officers' retirement pension trust fund.
25 (b) By August 15, 2022, the state treasurer shall transfer $2 million from the account to the
26 highway patrol officers' retirement pension trust fund.
27 (4)(3) Starting July 1, 2023, and in of each fiscal year thereafter, the state treasurer shall transfer
28 $500,000 from the account to the highway patrol officers' retirement pension trust fund by August 15.

21 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Victims of crime state special revenue account -- administration by
22 board of crime control. (1) There is an account in the state special revenue fund provided for in 17-2-102 to
23 be known as the victims of crime state special revenue account.
24 (2) There must be deposited in the account money received pursuant to 61-3-321(20).
25 (3) The money deposited in the account is allocated to the board of crime control to fund services
26 related to victims of crime.
