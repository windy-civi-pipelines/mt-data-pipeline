1 HOUSE BILL NO. 336
2 INTRODUCED BY C. SCHOMER, W. MCKAMEY, E. BUTTREY
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAMS FOR
5 CERTAIN PROFESSIONAL LICENSE TYPES; REQUIRING A BOARD OR PROGRAM TO GRANT A
6 LICENSE TO AN APPLICANT WHO COMPLETES A NATIONALLY RECOGNIZED APPRENTICESHIP;
7 PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AND PROVIDING LICENSE TYPES ALLOWED IN THE FEDERAL
8 APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Apprenticeships -- licensure. (1) A board or program shall grant a
13 license listed in subsection (3) of this section to a person who completes an apprenticeship program under Title
14 39, chapter 6, in the relevant occupation or profession.
15 (2) An applicant for licensure through an apprenticeship program shall meet the same exam and
16 fee requirements as an applicant who completes an educational program prior to licensure.
17 (3) The following licenses are subject to the provisions of this section:
18 (a) for the board of barbers and cosmetologists, Title 37, chapter 31:
19 (i) barbering;
20 (ii) barbering nonchemical;
21 (iii) cosmetology;
22 (iv) electrology;
23 (v) esthetics; and
24 (vi) manicuring;
25 (b) for the board of behavioral health, Title 37, chapter 39:
26 (i) addiction counseling; and
27 (ii) peer support specialist;
