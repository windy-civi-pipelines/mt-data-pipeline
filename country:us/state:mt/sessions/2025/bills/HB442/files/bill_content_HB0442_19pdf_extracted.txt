69th Legislature 2025 HB 442.1
2 INTRODUCED BY V. MOORE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN
5 PROFESSIONAL OCCUPATIONS; ELIMINATING THE REQUIREMENT FOR THE MEDICAL LEGAL PANEL
6 TO SEND A LICENSING BOARD THE OUTCOME OF A DECISION; ELIMINATING THE REQUIREMENT
7 FOR THE CHIROPRACTIC LEGAL PANEL TO SEND THE LICENSING BOARD THE OUTCOME OF A
8 DECISION; REMOVING THE REQUIREMENT FOR AN INSURER TO REPORT TO THE BOARD OF
9 MEDICAL EXAMINERS FOR ALLEGED PROFESSIONAL NEGLIGENCE; AMENDING SECTIONS 27-6-605,
10 27-12-605, 37-3-404, AND 37-3-405, MCA; AND REPEALING SECTION 37-3-402, MCA.”
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
13
14 Section 1. Section 27-6-605, MCA, is amended to read:
15 "27-6-605. Decision to be filed and copies sent to parties, and attorneys, and licensing board.
16 The decision shall be communicated in writing to the parties and attorneys concerned, and a copy thereof shall
17 be retained in the permanent files of the panel. A copy of the decision shall be sent to the health care provider's
18 professional licensing board."
19
20 Section 2. Section 27-12-605, MCA, is amended to read:
21 "27-12-605. Decision to be filed and copies sent to parties, and attorneys, and licensing board.
22 A copy of the decision must be:
23 (1) given to the parties and their attorneys; and
24 (2) retained in the permanent files of the panel."
25
26 Section 3. Section 37-3-404, MCA, is amended to read:
27 "37-3-404. Immunity of person providing information. A person, organization, association, society,
28 or health care facility which that in good faith provides information to the state board of medical examiners as
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 442
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 442.1
1 required by 37-3-401, 37-3-402, or 37-3-403 is not subject to suit for civil damages as a result thereof of
2 providing information."
3
4 Section 4. Section 37-3-405, MCA, is amended to read:
5 "37-3-405. Suspension of license or privilege for failure to report. The willful failure of any person,
6 organization, association, society, or health care facility to comply with 37-3-401, 37-3-402, or 37-3-403 is
7 grounds for suspension of any a license or privilege granted by the state of Montana. Upon such failure, the
8 The agency granting the license or privilege may, in its discretion, suspend the license or privilege for a period
9 not to exceed 1 month for a failure to comply."
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Repealer. The following section of the Montana Code Annotated is
12 repealed:
13 37-3-402. Insurer to report to board.
14 - END -
