Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 692.1
2 INTRODUCED BY B. MERCER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING THE SMART ACT; REQUIRING
5 DEPARTMENTS TO PROVIDE ANNUAL PLANS EACH YEAR; REQUIRING DEPARTMENTS TO INCLUDE
6 PROGRAM-LEVEL INITIATIVES IN THE REPORT; AMENDING SECTION 2-12-104, MCA; AND PROVIDING
7 AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 2-12-104, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "2-12-104. Annual plan. (1) Each department shall produce an annual plan no later than September
13 1, 2023.
14 (2) Each department shall publish a subsequent annual plan no later than September 1 every 2
15 years afterward each year.
16 (3) At a minimum, an annual plan must include the following components:
17 (a) a description of the functions and divisions of the department, including a discussion of the
18 department's priorities;
19 (b) initiatives of the department for each program, as that term is used in 17-7-711, that reflect the
20 benefits and outcomes the department expects to achieve on behalf of the public or specific groups through its
21 divisions; and
22 (c) specific and measurable performance measures for its initiatives, including the preferred
23 outcomes and outputs with respect to each initiative.
24 (4) Performance measures must:
25 (a) be tracked on an ongoing basis;
26 (b) assess a department's preferred outcomes and outputs;
27 (c) be quantitative when possible; and
28 (d) be designed to provide meaningful and useful information to policymakers and the public.
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 692
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 692.1
1 (5) The annual plan adopted by a department must be posted on the websites of the department."
2
3 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
4 - END -
- 2 - Authorized Print Version – HB 692
