4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT RECOGNIZING SPECIE LEGAL TENDER IN THE STATE;
5 RECOGNIZING GOLD AND SILVER COIN OR BULLION ISSUED BY THE UNITED STATES; PROVIDING
6 THAT EXCHANGES OF SPECIE LEGAL TENDER ARE EXEMPT FROM INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAX;
7 DEFINING "SPECIE LEGAL TENDER"; AMENDING SECTIONS 15-30-2101 AND 15-30-2120, MCA; AND
8 PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Short title. This part may be cited as the "Specie Legal Tender Act".
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Definition. Subject to [section 3(3)], as used in [sections 1 through 4],
15 "specie legal tender" means gold or silver coin that is issued by the United States.
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Specie legal tender -- legal tender -- prohibition on compelling
18 acceptance of legal tender -- court or congressional action to authorize gold or silver coin or bullion as
19 legal tender. (1) Specie legal tender is legal tender in the state.
20 (2) Except as expressly provided by contract, a person may not compel any other person to tender
21 or accept specie legal tender.
22 (3) Gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver coin that is issued by the United States, is
23 considered to be specie legal tender and is legal tender if:
24 (a) a court of competent jurisdiction issues a final, unappealable judgment or order determining
25 that the state may recognize the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than the gold or silver coin that is issued by
26 the United States, as legal tender in this state; or
27 (b) CONGRESS enacts legislation that:
28 (i) expressly provides that the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver coin that is
- 1 - LC 2030

1 issued by the United States, is legal tender in this state; or
2 (ii) expressly allows the state to recognize the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver
3 coin that is issued by the United States, as legal tender in the state.
4 (4) A central bank digital currency is not specie legal tender and is not legal tender in this state.
5
6 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Tax exemption -- exchange of legal tender. A transaction involving the
7 exchange of one form of legal tender for another form of legal tender:
8 (1) is not subject to individual income tax as provided in 15-30-2120;
9 (2) is not subject to any sales tax, including sales taxes that may be approved on or after [the
10 effective date of this act].

4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT RECOGNIZING SPECIE LEGAL TENDER IN THE STATE;
5 RECOGNIZING GOLD AND SILVER COIN OR BULLION ISSUED BY THE UNITED STATES; PROVIDING
6 THAT EXCHANGES OF SPECIE LEGAL TENDER ARE EXEMPT FROM INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAX;
7 DEFINING "SPECIE LEGAL TENDER"; AMENDING SECTIONS 15-30-2101 AND 15-30-2120, MCA; AND
8 PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”

12 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Short title. This part may be cited as the "Specie Legal Tender Act".
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Definition. Subject to [section 3(3)], as used in [sections 1 through 4],
15 "specie legal tender" means gold or silver coin that is issued by the United States.
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Specie legal tender -- legal tender -- prohibition on compelling
18 acceptance of legal tender -- court or congressional action to authorize gold or silver coin or bullion as
19 legal tender. (1) Specie legal tender is legal tender in the state.
20 (2) Except as expressly provided by contract, a person may not compel any other person to tender
21 or accept specie legal tender.
22 (3) Gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver coin that is issued by the United States, is
23 considered to be specie legal tender and is legal tender if:
24 (a) a court of competent jurisdiction issues a final, unappealable judgment or order determining
25 that the state may recognize the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than the gold or silver coin that is issued by
26 the United States, as legal tender in this state; or
27 (b) CONGRESS enacts legislation that:
28 (i) expressly provides that the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver coin that is

2 (ii) expressly allows the state to recognize the gold or silver coin or bullion, other than gold or silver

4 (4) A central bank digital currency is not specie legal tender and is not legal tender in this state.

6 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Tax exemption -- exchange of legal tender. A transaction involving the
7 exchange of one form of legal tender for another form of legal tender:
8 (1) is not subject to individual income tax as provided in 15-30-2120;
9 (2) is not subject to any sales tax, including sales taxes that may be approved on or after [the
10 effective date of this act].
