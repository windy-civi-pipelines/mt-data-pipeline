5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE STATEMENT OF POLICY AND THE DEFINITION
6 OF "BLIGHTED AREA" FOR MUNICIPALITIES EXERCISING URBAN RENEWAL POWERS, INCLUDING

12 Section 1. Section 7-15-4202, MCA, is amended to read:
13 "7-15-4202. Existence of blighted areas and resulting problems -- statement of policy. It is
14 hereby found and declared:
15 (1) that blighted areas which constitute a serious and growing menace, injurious to the public
16 health, safety, morals, and welfare of the residents of the state, exist in municipalities of the state;
17 (2) that the existence of such areas:
18 (1) The legislature finds and declares blighted areas exist within municipalities of the state that:
19 (a) are injurious to the public health, safety, and welfare of the residents of the state;
20 (a)(b) contributes substantially and increasingly contribute to the spread of disease and crime and
21 depreciation of property values;
22 (b)(c) constitutes constitute an economic and social liability;
23 (c)(d) substantially impairs or arrests impair the sound growth of municipalities communities;
24 (d) retards the provision of housing accommodations;
25 (e) constitute an impediment to developing and maintaining adequate housing; and
26 (e)(f) aggravates aggravate traffic problems and hazards.; and
27 (f) substantially impairs or arrests the elimination of traffic hazards and the improvement of traffic
28 facilities; and

23 (b) inadequate provision for ventilation, light, proper sanitary facilities, or open spaces as
24 determined by competent appraisers on the basis of an examination of the building standards of the
25 municipality;
26 (c) inappropriate or mixed uses of land or buildings;
27 (d) high density of population and overcrowding;
