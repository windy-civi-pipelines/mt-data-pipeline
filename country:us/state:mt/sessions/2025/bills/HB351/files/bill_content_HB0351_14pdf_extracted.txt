13 Section 1. Section 15-70-401, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "15-70-401. Definitions. As used in this part, the following definitions apply:
15 (1) "Agricultural use" means use of gasoline or special fuel by a person who earns income while
16 engaging in the business of farming or ranching and who files farm or income reports for tax purposes as
17 required by the United States internal revenue service.
18 (2) "Aviation fuel" means gasoline or any other liquid fuel by whatever name the liquid fuel may be
19 known or sold, compounded for use in and sold for use in aircraft, including but not limited to any and all
20 gasoline or liquid fuel meeting or exceeding the minimum specifications prescribed by the United States for use
21 by its military forces in aircraft.
22 (3) (a) "Biodiesel" means a fuel produced from monoalkyl esters of long-chain fatty acids derived
23 from vegetable oils, renewable lipids, animal fats, or any combination of those ingredients. The fuel must meet
24 the requirements of ASTM D6751, also known as the Standard Specification for Biodiesel Fuel (B100) Blend
25 Stock for Distillate Fuels, as adopted by the American society for testing and materials.
26 (b) Biodiesel is also known as "B-100".
27 (4) "Bulk delivery" means placing gasoline or special fuel not intended for resale in storage or
28 containers. The term does not mean gasoline or special fuel delivered into the supply tank of a motor vehicle.
