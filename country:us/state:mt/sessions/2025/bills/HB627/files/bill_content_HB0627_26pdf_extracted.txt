69th Legislature 2025 HB0627.2
2 INTRODUCED BY B. MERCER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING PROCUREMENT REPORTING REQUIREMENTS;
5 AND REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATION TO PROVIDE CERTAIN REPORTS TO THE
6 LEGISLATIVE FINANCE COMMITTEE ON PROCUREMENT SOLICITATIONS, CONTRACTS, AND
7 CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS; AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES AND A TERMINATION DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Report to interim committee. (1) By March 1, June 1, September 1,
12 and December 1 of each year, the department shall provide a written report to the legislative finance committee
13 in accordance with 5-11-210 containing the information listed in subsection (2) THAT IS related to procurement
14 solicitations and contracts AND IS AVAILABLE TO THE DEPARTMENT IN THE DEPARTMENT'S INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
15 RESOURCES.
16 (2) The report must include:
17 (a) for a procurement solicitation for a contract that the department anticipates will cost at least
18 $250,000 $200,000 over the life of the contract:
19 (i) the name of each purchasing agency issuing the solicitation;
20 (ii) what the purchasing agency intends to procure through the solicitation;
21 (iii) the portion of the purchasing agency's appropriation that it intends to use to pay the vendor ;
22 (iv)(III) the closing date of the solicitation; and
23 (v)(IV) any other information the department finds relevant to the proposal;
24 (b) for a contract executed with or without a solicitation that is expected to cost $250,000 $200,000
25 or more over the life of the contract:
26 (i) the names of the purchasing agency; and
27 (ii) the contractor and the date of execution; and
28 (c) for a contract modification that causes the total value of a contract to exceed $250,000

1 $200,000 over the life of the contract:
2 (i) the names of the purchasing agency; and
3 (ii) the contractor and the date of execution.
4 (3) BY FEBRUARY 15, MAY 15, AUGUST 15, AND NOVEMBER 15 OF EACH YEAR, EACH GOVERNMENTAL
5 BODY SHALL SUBMIT THE INFORMATION LISTED IN SUBSECTION (2) RELATED TO PROCUREMENT SOLICITATIONS AND
6 CONTRACTS TO THE DEPARTMENT USING THE INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY RESOURCES SPECIFIED BY THE DEPARTMENT.
7
8 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. REPORT TO INTERIM COMMITTEE. (1) BY MARCH 1, JUNE 1, SEPTEMBER 1,
9 AND DECEMBER 1 OF EACH YEAR, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PROVIDE A WRITTEN REPORT TO THE LEGISLATIVE FINANCE
10 COMMITTEE IN ACCORDANCE WITH 5-11-210 CONTAINING THE INFORMATION LISTED IN SUBSECTION (2) RELATED TO
11 PROCUREMENT SOLICITATIONS AND CONTRACTS.
12 (2) THE REPORT MUST INCLUDE:
13 (A) FOR A PROCUREMENT SOLICITATION FOR A CONTRACT THAT THE DEPARTMENT ANTICIPATES WILL COST
14 AT LEAST $200,000 OVER THE LIFE OF THE CONTRACT:
15 (I) THE NAME OF EACH PURCHASING AGENCY ISSUING THE SOLICITATION;
16 (II) WHAT THE PURCHASING AGENCY INTENDS TO PROCURE THROUGH THE SOLICITATION;
17 (III) THE PORTION OF THE PURCHASING AGENCY'S APPROPRIATION THAT IT INTENDS TO USE TO PAY THE
18 VENDOR;
19 (IV) THE CLOSING DATE OF THE SOLICITATION; AND
20 (V) ANY OTHER INFORMATION THE DEPARTMENT FINDS RELEVANT TO THE PROPOSAL;
21 (B) FOR A CONTRACT EXECUTED WITH OR WITHOUT A SOLICITATION THAT IS EXPECTED TO COST $200,000
22 OR MORE OVER THE LIFE OF THE CONTRACT:
23 (I) THE NAMES OF THE PURCHASING AGENCY; AND
24 (II) THE CONTRACTOR AND THE DATE OF EXECUTION; AND
25 (C) FOR A CONTRACT MODIFICATION THAT CAUSES THE TOTAL VALUE OF A CONTRACT TO EXCEED
26 $200,000 OVER THE LIFE OF THE CONTRACT:
27 (I) THE NAMES OF THE PURCHASING AGENCY; AND
28 (II) THE CONTRACTOR AND THE DATE OF EXECUTION.
