Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 SB 167.1
2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT TRANSFERRING FUNDS TO THE NOXIOUS WEED
5 MANAGEMENT TRUST FUND; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Transfer of funds. By June 30, 2025, the state treasurer shall transfer
10 $10 million from the general fund to the noxious weed management trust fund provided for in 80-7-811 and
11 required by Article IX, section 6, of the Montana constitution.
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
14 - END -
