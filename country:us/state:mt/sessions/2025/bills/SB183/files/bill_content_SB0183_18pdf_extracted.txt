Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 SB 183.1
2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ, J. SECKINGER, E. ALBUS
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING AN OUTFITTER ENDORSEMENT FOR GUIDE
5 LICENSURE; AMENDING SECTION 37-47-303, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE
6 DATE.”
7
8 WHEREAS, professional relationships between guides and endorsing outfitters has been an industry
9 tradition and licensure standard for generations; and
10 WHEREAS, existing statute allows for the licensure of guides without requiring an outfitter
11 endorsement, thus reducing the industry's ability to regulate its own professional standards; and
12 WHEREAS, public health, safety, and welfare is best served by ensuring that guides meet the
13 qualifications and industry standards of Montana's outfitters in addition to those of the Board of Outfitters.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
16
17 Section 1. Section 37-47-303, MCA, is amended to read:
18 "37-47-303. Guide's qualifications. An applicant for a guide's license must:
19 (1) be 18 years of age or older and be physically capable and mentally competent to perform the
20 duties of a guide;
21 (2) be endorsed and recommended by an outfitter with a valid license, unless otherwise qualified
22 under guide standards established by the board pursuant to 37-47-201(4); and
23 (3) have been issued a valid conservation license."
24
25 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
26 - END -
