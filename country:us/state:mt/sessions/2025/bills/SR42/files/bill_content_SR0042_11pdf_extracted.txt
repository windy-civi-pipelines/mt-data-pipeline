Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 42.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 42
2 INTRODUCED BY B. USHER
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENT OF THOMAS LEE BRUNER TO THE OFFICE OF WORKERS'
6 COMPENSATION COURT JUDGE MADE BY THE GOVERNOR AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN
7 COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointment, below designated, that
10 has been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
11 As Workers' Compensation Court Judge, in accordance with 2-15-1707, MCA:
12 Thomas Lee Bruner, Whitehall, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending September 8, 2029.
13
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
15 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
16 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointment and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
17 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
18 MCA.
19 - END -
