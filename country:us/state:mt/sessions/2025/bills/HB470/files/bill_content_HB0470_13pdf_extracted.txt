4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATING TO MORTGAGES AND FALSE AND
5 DECEPTIVE ADVERTISING BY PROHIBITING THE USE OF TRIGGER LEADS; DEFINING "TRIGGER
6 LEAD"; PROVIDING AUTHORITY OF THE BANKING COMMISSIONER TO REGULATE TRIGGER LEADS;
7 AND AMENDING SECTIONS 32-9-103 AND 32-9-149, MCA.”

9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. False or deceptive advertising -- trigger leads. In order to prevent or
12 mitigate identity theft, in addition to practices identified by the commissioner in rule under 32-9-149, false and
13 deceptive advertising includes the use of a trigger lead as defined in 32-9-103.
14
15 Section 2. Section 32-9-103, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "32-9-103. Definitions. As used in this part, the following definitions apply:
17 (1) "Administrative or clerical tasks" mean the receipt, collection, and distribution of information
18 common for the processing or underwriting of a loan in the mortgage industry, without performing any analysis
19 of the information, and communication with a consumer to obtain information necessary for the processing or
20 underwriting of a residential mortgage loan.
21 (2) "Advertising" means a commercial message in any medium, including social media and
22 software, that promotes, either directly or indirectly, a residential mortgage loan transaction.
23 (3) "Application" means a request, in any form, for an offer of residential mortgage loan terms or a
24 response to a solicitation of an offer of residential mortgage loan terms and includes the information about the
25 borrower that is customary or necessary in a decision on whether to make such an offer.
26 (4) "Approved education course" means any course approved by the NMLS.
27 (5) "Approved test provider" means any test provider approved by the NMLS.
28 (6) "Bona fide not-for-profit entity" means an entity that:

26 Section 3. Section 32-9-149, MCA, is amended to read:
27 "32-9-149. Use of name -- advertising. (1) A licensee engaged in a business regulated by this part
28 may not operate under a name other than the name licensed by the department.

1 (2) A licensee may not:
2 (a) advertise that an applicant has unqualified access to credit without disclosing that material
3 limitations on the availability of credit may exist, such as the percentage required as a down payment, that a
4 higher interest rate or points could be required, or that restrictions as to the maximum principal amount of the
5 mortgage loan offered could apply;
6 (b) advertise a mortgage loan with a prevailing interest rate indicated in the advertisement unless
7 the advertisement specifically states that the interest rate could change or not be available at the time of

16 following information must be included:
17 (a) a name and unique identifier for a mortgage loan originator advertising as an individual; or
18 (b) the name and unique identifier only of the licensed entity when the licensed entity is advertising
19 on its own behalf or as an entity with one or more mortgage loan originators listed.
20 (5) The department may adopt rules to establish requirements for licensee advertising using the
21 internet or any electronic format."
