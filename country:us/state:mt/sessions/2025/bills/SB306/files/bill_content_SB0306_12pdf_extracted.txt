69th Legislature 2025 SB 306.1
2 INTRODUCED BY K. BOGNER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING THAT A COUNTY TREASURER MAY NOT SELL A
5 TAX DEED TO RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY TO A FOREIGN ENTITY; DEFINING "FOREIGN ENTITY";
6 AMENDING SECTIONS 15-17-121 AND 15-18-220, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 15-17-121, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "15-17-121. Definitions. Except as otherwise specifically provided, when terms mentioned in Title 15,
12 chapters 17 and 18, are used in connection with taxation, they are defined in the following manner:
13 (1) "Assignee" means a person, other than the person to whom the property is assessed, who
14 pays the delinquent taxes, including penalties, interest, and costs, and receives a tax lien certificate
15 representing a lien on the property and an assignment certificate.
16 (2) "Assignment certificate" means the document described in 15-17-323.
17 (3) (a) "Cost" means the cost incurred by the county as a result of a taxpayer's failure to pay taxes
18 when due. It includes but is not limited to any actual out-of-pocket expenses incurred by the county plus the
19 administrative cost of:
20 (i) preparing the list of delinquent taxes;
21 (ii) preparing the notice of pending attachment of a tax lien;
22 (iii) assigning the county's interest in a tax lien to a third party;
23 (iv) identifying interested persons entitled to notice of the pending issuance of a tax deed;
24 (v) notifying interested persons;
25 (vi) sale or resale;
26 (vii) issuing the tax deed; and
27 (viii) any other administrative task associated with accounting for or collecting delinquent taxes.
28 (b) The term includes costs that are required by law and incurred by an assignee. The county

10 Section 2. Section 15-18-220, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "15-18-220. Sale at public auction -- notice of auction -- cancellation of assignment for
12 unsuccessful auction. (1) Upon receipt of an application for a tax deed pursuant to 15-18-219, the county
13 treasurer shall hold a public auction in the county in which the property is located within 60 days of receipt of
14 the application. The county treasurer shall publish notice of the auction as provided in 7-1-2121 that includes
15 the date, time, and location of the auction, the legal description of the property, the deposit requirement, and
16 the minimum opening bid. The auction must be held during the regular office hours of the county treasurer.
17 (2) (a) The opening bid on the property must be the amount required in subsection (2)(b), and the
18 county treasurer may not accept a bid below the opening bid.
19 (b) The opening bid for the property is equal to the sum of:
20 (i) the amount required to redeem the tax lien, which includes delinquent taxes, penalties, interest,
21 and costs;
22 (ii) amounts paid by the assignee upon application for the tax deed pursuant to 15-18-219(2);
23 (iii) tax deed fees provided for in 15-18-211(2)(a) and recording fees; and
24 (iv) an amount equal to half of the most recent assessed value of the land and of the dwelling or
25 half of the value of the land and of the dwelling as determined in an independent appraisal. If the opening bid is
26 based on an independent appraisal, the appraisal must be provided to the county treasurer, must meet the
27 standards set by the Montana board of real estate appraisers, and must have been conducted within 6 months
28 of the date of the auction.

3 subsection (3)(b), the high bidder shall post with the county treasurer a nonrefundable deposit of 5% of the bid
4 or $200, whichever is greater, at the time of sale. The deposit is applied to the sale price at the time of full
5 payment. Notice of the deposit requirement must be posted at the auction site, and the county treasurer may
6 require bidders to show their ability to post the deposit. The county treasurer may refuse to recognize the bid of
7 a person who has previously bid and refused, for any reason, to honor the bid.
8 (b) If Except as provided in subsection (7), if the assignee is the high bidder, the assignee shall
9 pay to the county treasurer auction costs and any amounts included in the opening bid and not already paid,
10 including filing fees, tax deed fees, and one-half of the most recent assessed value of the land and of the
11 dwelling. If the assignee does not make full payment within 24 hours, excluding weekends and legal holidays,
12 the county treasurer shall cancel the assignment and file with the county clerk and recorder a notice of
13 cancellation on a form provided for in 15-18-225.
14 (c) If Except as provided in subsection (7), if full payment of the purchase price and auction costs
15 is not made within 24 hours of the sale, excluding weekends and legal holidays, by a high bidder who is not the
16 assignee, the county treasurer shall cancel the high bid and allow the next highest bidder to purchase the tax
17 deed for the amount bid. If the next highest bidder does not make full payment of the purchase price and the
18 auction costs within 24 hours, excluding weekends and legal holidays, of notification by the county treasurer,
19 the county treasurer shall repeat the process and contact the next highest bidder until the purchase price and
20 auction costs are paid or until there are no bidders remaining. If no bidder pays the purchase price and auction
21 costs, the county treasurer shall cancel the assignment and file with the county clerk and recorder a notice of
22 cancellation on a form provided for in 15-18-225.
23 (d) If there are no bidders at the auction, the county treasurer shall cancel the assignment and file
24 with the county clerk and recorder a notice of cancellation on a form provided for in 15-18-225.

26 land and of the dwelling is considered surplus funds and, upon sale of the property, must be distributed as
27 provided in 15-18-221. If the purchase price is higher than the opening bid, the difference between the
28 purchase price and the opening bid is considered surplus funds and must be distributed as provided in 15-18-
