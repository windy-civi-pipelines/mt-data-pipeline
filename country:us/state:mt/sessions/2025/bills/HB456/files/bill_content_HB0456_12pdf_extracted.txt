69th Legislature 2025 HB 456.1
2 INTRODUCED BY J. KARLEN, J. REAVIS, L. MUSZKIEWICZ, B. EDWARDS, J. ISALY, M. LEE, P. STRAND,
3 J. SECKINGER, B. CLOSE, P. ELVERUM, S. FYANT, J. WEBER, A. GRIFFITH, M. CUNNINGHAM, D. BAUM,
4 B. CARTER, J. COHENOUR, C. KEOGH, E. STAFMAN, D. JOY
5
6 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT EXPANDING THE BEST BEGINNINGS CHILD CARE
7 SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM TO PROVIDE SCHOLARSHIPS TO CHILD-CARE WORKERS EMPLOYED BY A
8 LICENSED DAY-CARE CENTER OR REGISTERED FAMILY OR GROUP DAY-CARE HOME; PROVIDING
9 AN APPROPRIATION; AMENDING SECTIONS 52-2-714 AND 52-2-715, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN
10 EFFECTIVE DATE.”
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
13
14 Section 1. Section 52-2-714, MCA, is amended to read:
15 "52-2-714. Best beginnings child care scholarship program. There is a best beginnings child care
16 scholarship program administered by the department. The program is established to provide scholarships to
17 qualified low-income families and eligible child-care workers employed at a licensed day-care center or
18 registered family or group day-care home whose child received care provided by a licensed or registered child
19 care child-care provider or day-care facility."
20
21 Section 2. Section 52-2-715, MCA, is amended to read:
22 "52-2-715. Family income eligibility -- child-care worker eligibility -- requirements. (1) In Except
23 as provided in subsection (2), in determining income eligibility for qualified low-income families for the best
24 beginnings child care scholarship program, the department shall:
25 (1)(a) set a qualifying income threshold at no less than 185% of the federal poverty level for each
26 family size; and
27 (2)(b) set a maximum qualifying income level that is no higher than that allowed by the federal child
28 care and development block grant.
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 456
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 456.1
1 (2) (a) A child-care worker is eligible for the best beginnings child care scholarship program if the
2 worker is employed at a licensed day-care center or registered family or group day-care home and is engaged
3 in any of the eligible activities, as established by the department pursuant to 45 CFR 98.16(g), for at least the
4 required minimum number of hours.
5 (b) The income eligibility requirements provided in subsection (1) do not apply to a child-care
6 worker who is otherwise eligible under this subsection (2)."
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Appropriation. There is appropriated $5.5 million from the general fund
9 to the department of public health and human services for each year of the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, for
10 the purposes of providing best beginnings child care scholarships to eligible child-care workers as provided in
11 [section 1].
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
14 - END -
