9 Section 3. Section 75-6-130, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "75-6-130. Development plans -- department requirements -- rulemaking. (1) Unless authorized
11 by a development plan approved by the department of environmental quality under this section, a public water
12 or wastewater supply system may not certify or authorize additional connections to its water or wastewater
13 system that would exceed its approved rated capacity.
14 (2) The department shall approve a municipal or eligible county water and/or sewer district
15 development plan to allow additional connections to a public water or wastewater supply system if the
16 municipality or eligible county water and/or sewer district demonstrates that:
17 (a) the additional connections will not exceed the approved rated capacity of the system;
18 (b) the additional connections will not create a risk to public health or the environment;
19 (c) the plan will not cause a violation of any provision of or rule adopted under this part or Title 75,
20 chapter 5 or 6, or any condition or requirement of an approval or order issued pursuant to this part or Title 75,
21 chapter 5 or 6; and
22 (d) the additional connections will not cause the municipality or eligible county water and/or sewer
23 district to exceed the flow rate, volume, or place of use of its water right as defined under 85-2-102.
24 (3) A municipality or eligible county water and/or sewer district with a development plan approved
25 under this section shall submit an annual report detailing its compliance with the approved plan to the
26 department.

28 connections that would exceed the system's approved rated capacity only if the connection would serve a
