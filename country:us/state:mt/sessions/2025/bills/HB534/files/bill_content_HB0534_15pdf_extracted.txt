26 water supply system, either public or private, or a wastewater or sewerline or other potential source of
27 contamination so that a flow of water into or contamination of the public water supply system from the other
28 source of water or contamination is possible.

8 Section 3. Section 75-6-130, MCA, is amended to read:
9 "75-6-130. Development plans -- department requirements -- rulemaking. (1) Unless authorized
10 by a development plan approved by the department of environmental quality under this section, a public water
11 or wastewater supply system may not certify or authorize additional connections to its water or wastewater
12 system that would exceed its approved rated capacity.
13 (2) The department shall approve a municipal or eligible county water and/or sewer district
14 development plan to allow additional connections to a public water or wastewater supply system if the
15 municipality or eligible county water and/or sewer district demonstrates that:
16 (a) the additional connections will not exceed the approved rated capacity of the system;
17 (b) the additional connections will not create a risk to public health or the environment;
18 (c) the plan will not cause a violation of any provision of or rule adopted under this part or Title 75,
19 chapter 5 or 6, or any condition or requirement of an approval or order issued pursuant to this part or Title 75,
20 chapter 5 or 6; and
