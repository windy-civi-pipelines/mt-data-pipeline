4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING ALCOHOL LICENSE LAWS; PROVIDING
5 DEFINITIONS; PROVIDING LICENSED ENTITIES THAT MUST HAVE APPLICANTS SUBMIT

11 Section 1. Section 16-1-106, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "16-1-106. Definitions. As used in this code, the following definitions apply:
13 (1) "Agency franchise agreement" means an agreement between the department and a person
14 appointed to sell liquor as a commission merchant rather than as an employee.
15 (2) "Agency liquor store" means a store operated under an agency franchise agreement in
16 accordance with this code for the purpose of selling liquor at either the posted or the retail price for off-premises
17 consumption.
18 (3) "Alcohol" means ethyl alcohol, also called ethanol, or the hydrated oxide of ethyl.
19 (4) "Alcoholic beverage" means a compound produced and sold for human consumption as a drink
20 that contains more than 0.5% of alcohol by volume.
21 (5) (a) "Beer" means:
22 (i) a malt beverage containing not more than 8.75% of alcohol by volume; or
23 (ii) an alcoholic beverage containing not more than 14% alcohol by volume:
24 (A) that is made by the alcoholic fermentation of an infusion or decoction, or a combination of both,
25 in potable brewing water, of malted cereal grain; and
26 (B) in which the sugars used for fermentation of the alcoholic beverage are at least 75% derived
27 from malted cereal grain measured as a percentage of the total dry weight of the fermentable ingredients.
28 (b) The term does not include a caffeinated or stimulant-enhanced malt beverage.
