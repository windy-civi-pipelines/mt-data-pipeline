Section 1:

69th Legislature 2025 HB 824.1
2 INTRODUCED BY J. LYNCH, J. ISALY, M. LEE, D. HARVEY, D. HAWK, M. ROMANO, S. DEMAROIS
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING THAT COUNTY NUMBER DECALS MAY BE
5 DISPLAYED ON PERSONALIZED PLATES; AND AMENDING SECTION 61-3-403, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 61-3-403, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "61-3-403. Color and design of personalized license plates -- exception. Except as provided in
11 61-3-407 and 61-3-466, the personalized license plates must be the same color and design as standard license
12 plates and must consist of numbers or letters, or any combination of numbers or letters, not exceeding eight
13 positions and not less than two positions, provided that there are no conflicts with existing standard or special
14 license plate series under this title. A registration decal must be displayed on personalized license plates as
15 provided in 61-3-332. A county number decal may be displayed on personalized plates."
16 - END -
