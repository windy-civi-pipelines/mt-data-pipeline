Section 1:
Transportation
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Maxwell Parson, SB0392.001.003
1 SENATE BILL NO. 392
2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE LAVERNE PARRISH MEMORIAL
5 HIGHWAY; DIRECTING THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION TO INSTALL SIGNS AND TO
6 INCLUDE THE MEMORIAL HIGHWAY ON THE NEXT PUBLICATION OF THE STATE MAP; AND
7 PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 WHEREAS, Laverne Parrish was born on July 16, 1918, to Clatious and Weltha Parrish; and
10 WHEREAS, Laverne Parrish graduated from Ronan High School in 1937; and
11 WHEREAS, Laverne Parrish enlisted in the Army National Guard in 1941, where he became a medical
12 aidman; and
13 WHEREAS, Laverne Parrish was assigned to the 161st Infantry, 25th Infantry Division and participated
14 in several major campaigns, including Guadalcanal, the Solomon Islands, and the Philippines; and
15 WHEREAS, on January 18, 1945, Laverne Parrish risked his life to save two wounded soldiers who
16 were under enemy fire, moving one man to cover and exposing himself to enemy fire across open ground to
17 provide aid to the second man; and
18 WHEREAS, on January 24, 1945, Laverne Parrish again risked his life to aid 12 wounded soldiers in an
19 open field, crossing and recrossing areas under enemy fire and making five successive trips to bring wounded
20 soldiers to safety; and
21 WHEREAS, on January 24, 1945, having treated nearly all 37 wounded soldiers in his company,
22 Laverne Parrish was killed by enemy mortar fire; and
23 WHEREAS, Laverne Parrish was posthumously awarded the Medal of Honor for extraordinary heroism
24 in Luzon, Philippine Islands, in 1945.
25
26 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
27
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – SB 392
Amendment - 1st Reading-white - Requested by: John Esp - (S) Highways and
Transportation
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Maxwell Parson, SB0392.001.003
1 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Laverne Parrish memorial highway. There is established the Laverne
2 Parrish memorial highway on the existing state highway 93 from mile marker 48 to mile marker 53 51.
3 (2) The department shall design and install appropriate signs marking the location of the Laverne
4 Parrish memorial highway.
5 (3) Maps that identify roadways in the state must be updated to include the location of the Laverne
6 Parrish memorial highway when the department updates and publishes the state maps.
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
9 integral part of Title 60, chapter 1, part 2, and the provisions of Title 60, chapter 1, part 2, apply to [section 1].
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
12 - END -
