Section 1:
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0454.2
2 INTRODUCED BY M. THIEL, L. BENNETT, A. REGIER, J. ETCHART, V. MOORE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE FREQUENCY OF PATIENT VISITS REQUIRED
5 BY A LICENSED PHYSICAL THERAPIST WHEN SUPERVISING A PHYSICAL THERAPY ASSISTANT; AND
6 AMENDING SECTION 37-11-105, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 37-11-105, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "37-11-105. Supervision of physical therapist assistant, physical therapy aide, physical therapy
12 student, or physical therapist assistant student. (1) A physical therapist assistant shall practice under the
13 supervision of a licensed physical therapist who is responsible for and participates in a patient's care. This
14 supervision requires the licensed physical therapist to make an onsite visit or a visit by means of telehealth to
15 the client at least once for every six eight visits made by the assistant or once every 2 weeks 30 days,
16 whichever occurs first.
17 (2) A licensed physical therapist may not concurrently supervise more than two full-time assistants
18 or the equivalent. This supervision does not require the presence of the assistant.
19 (3) A physical therapy aide shall practice under the onsite supervision of a licensed physical
20 therapist or a licensed assistant. A licensed assistant may not concurrently supervise more than one full-time
21 aide or the equivalent. A licensed physical therapist may not concurrently supervise more than four aides or the
22 equivalent or two assistants and two aides or the equivalent.
23 (4) A physical therapy student or physical therapist assistant student shall practice with the onsite
24 supervision of a licensed physical therapist."
25
26 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. [THIS ACT] IS EFFECTIVE ON PASSAGE AND APPROVAL.
27 - END -
