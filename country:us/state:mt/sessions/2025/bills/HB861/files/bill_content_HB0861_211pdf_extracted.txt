Section 1:
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0861.2
2 INTRODUCED BY E. TILLEMAN, M. NIKOLAKAKOS, L. REKSTEN, M. ROMANO
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT APPROPRIATING MONEY TO PROVIDE STATEWIDE ACCESS
5 TO A K-12 DIGITAL TOOLKIT; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE
6 AND A TERMINATION DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Appropriation. (1) The following money is appropriated from the general
11 fund to provide statewide access to a K-12 digital toolkit that provides state standards-aligned, multimodal
12 instructional materials across all content areas, including access to career and technical educational materials
13 and local Montana industry partners to support workforce development:
14 (a) for fiscal year 2026:
15 (i) $1.1 million to the office of public instruction; and
16 (ii) $750,000 to the department of labor and industry; and
17 (b) for fiscal year 2027:
18 (i) $1.1 million to the office of public instruction; and
19 (ii) $750,000 to the department of labor and industry.
20 (2) The legislature intends that the appropriations in this section be considered part of the ongoing
21 base for the next legislative session.
22
23 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
24
25 NEW SECTION. SECTION 3. TERMINATION. [THIS ACT] TERMINATES JUNE 30, 2029.
26 - END -
