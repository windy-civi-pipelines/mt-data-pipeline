Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 6.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 6
2 INTRODUCED BY B. USHER
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENT MADE BY THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE AND SUBMITTED BY
6 WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 24, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Speaker of the House of Representatives of the State of Montana has made the
9 appointment, below designated, that has been submitted to the Senate by the Speaker of the House pursuant
10 to section 3-1-1101, MCA:
11 As a member of the Judicial Standards Commission, in accordance with section 3-1-1101, MCA:
12 Judge Ashley Harada, Billings, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending July 1, 2029.
13
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
15 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
16 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointment and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
17 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State, to the Speaker of the House, and to the Chief Justice
18 of the Montana Supreme Court.
19 - END -
