69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Jameson Walker, HB0427.001.002
1 HOUSE BILL NO. 427
2 INTRODUCED BY K. ZOLNIKOV
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR AND INDUSTRY OR
5 A LOCAL BUILDING DEPARTMENT TO PROVIDE THE TEXT OR CITATION OF THE SPECIFIC SECTIONS
6 OF THE BUILDING CODE RELIED ON TO DELAY A PERMIT APPLICATION OR STOP A CONSTRUCTION
7 PROJECT; PROVIDING A REMEDY; PROVIDING REMEDIES AGAINST INDIVIDUAL EMPLOYEES OF THE
8 DEPARTMENT OR LOCAL BUILDING DEPARTMENT; AND CREATING A CAUSE OF ACTION; AND
9 PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Building code section to be provided. (1) If the department or local
14 building department delays the issuance of a permit or stops work on a construction project because of
15 noncompliance with the state building code adopted pursuant to 50-60-203 or a building code adopted by a
16 county, city, or town pursuant to 50-60-301, the person applying for a permit or constructing the project may
17 request in writing or electronically that the department or local building department provide the person with the
18 text or citation of the specific sections of the building code applicable to the permit application or construction
19 project that the department or local building department is relying on to cause the delay of the permit
20 application or the stoppage of the construction project.
21 (2) The department or local building department shall provide the text or citations requested under
22 subsection (1) within 7 calendar days.
23 (3) Failure to provide the text or citation of the specific sections of the building code applicable to
24 the permit application or the construction project that the department or local building department is relying on
25 to cause the delay of the permit application or the stoppage of the construction project within 7 calendar days
26 waives the application of those sections of the building code to the permit application or the construction
27 project.
28 (4) (a) A person aggrieved by the failure to provide the text or citation of the specific sections of the

1 building code under subsection (1) may file a civil action in district court for appropriate relief, including any
2 compensatory damages related to a delay of the permit application or a stoppage of the construction project.
3 (b) The civil action as provided in subsection (4)(a) may apply to an employee of the department or
4 local building department, who may be held personally responsible for any compensatory damages related to a
5 delay of the permit application or a stoppage of the construction project if the employee acted in bad faith,
6 maliciously, or willfully.
7 (b)(c) A prevailing plaintiff is entitled to reasonable court costs and attorney fees.
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
10 integral part of Title 50, chapter 60, part 1, and the provisions of Title 50, chapter 60, part 1, apply to [section 1].
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Applicability. [This act] applies to construction projects that begin or are
15 in process on or after [the effective date of this act].
16 - END -
