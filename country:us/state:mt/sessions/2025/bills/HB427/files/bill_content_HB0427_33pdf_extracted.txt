Section 1:
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0427.3
2 INTRODUCED BY K. ZOLNIKOV
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR AND INDUSTRY OR
5 A LOCAL BUILDING DEPARTMENT TO PROVIDE THE TEXT OR CITATION OF THE SPECIFIC SECTIONS
6 OF THE BUILDING CODE RELIED ON TO DELAY A PERMIT APPLICATION OR STOP A CONSTRUCTION
7 PROJECT; PROVIDING A REMEDY; AND CREATING A CAUSE OF ACTION.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Building code section to be provided -- DEPARTMENT PENALTY. (1) If
12 the department or local building department delays the issuance of a BUILDING permit BEYOND THE STANDARD
13 REVIEW PERIOD OF A COMPLETE APPLICATION, or stops work on a construction project because of noncompliance
14 with the state building code adopted pursuant to 50-60-203 or a building code adopted by a county, city, or
15 town pursuant to 50-60-301, the person applying for a permit or constructing the project may request in writing
16 or electronically that the department or local building department provide the person with the text or citation of
17 the specific sections of the building code applicable to the permit application or construction project that the
18 department or local building department is relying on to cause the delay of the permit application or the
19 stoppage of the construction project.
20 (2) IF A PROJECT ALSO REQUIRES ZONING, SUBDIVISION, OR OTHER APPROVAL, THE DEPARTMENT OR
21 LOCAL BUILDING DEPARTMENT MAY REQUIRE THOSE REVIEWS TO BE COMPLETED BEFORE DETERMINING A BUILDING
22 APPLICATION IS COMPLETE.
23 (2) (3) The department or local building department shall provide the text or citations requested under
24 subsection (1) within 7 calendar BUSINESS days.
25 (3) Failure to provide the text or citation of the specific sections of the building code applicable to
26 the permit application or the construction project that the department or local building department is relying on
27 to cause the delay of the permit application or the stoppage of the construction project within 7 calendar
28 BUSINESS days waives the application of those sections of the building code to the permit application or the
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 427
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0427.3
1 construction project.
2 (4) IF THE DEPARTMENT OR LOCAL BUILDING DEPARTMENT DOES NOT RESPOND TO THE REQUEST IN
3 ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSECTION (3), IT SHALL PAY TO THE REQUESTING PARTY A SUM OF $50 FOR EACH DAY BEYOND
4 THE REQUIRED RESPONSE PERIOD UNTIL THE TEXT OR CITATION IS PROVIDED TO THE REQUESTING PARTY.
5 (4) (5) (a) A person aggrieved by DELAYS ASSOCIATED WITH the failure to provide the text or citation of
6 the specific sections of the building code AS REQUIRED under subsection (1) may ALSO file a civil action in district
7 court for appropriate relief, including any compensatory damages related to a delay of the permit application or
8 a stoppage of the construction project.
9 (b) A THE prevailing plaintiff PARTY is entitled to reasonable court costs and attorney fees.
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
12 integral part of Title 50, chapter 60, part 1, and the provisions of Title 50, chapter 60, part 1, apply to [section 1].
13 - END -
