18 each signature sheet if sheets are circulated separately. The text of the issue must be in the format prescribed

22 must be attached to each section.

6 NEW SECTION. Section 8. Governing body review of petition. (1) The governing body of the local
7 government may, within 60 days of receiving the petition, take the action called for in the petition. If the action is
8 taken, the question need not be submitted to the electors. If the county election administrator has already
9 certified the ballot issue pursuant to [section 9], the results of the election are void.
10 (2) If the governing body does not within 60 days take the proposed action and the ballot issue is
11 certified by the county election administrator, then the question must be submitted to the electors at the election
12 specified in the petition.

5 NEW SECTION. Section 10. Delay of effective date of ordinance -- suspension of emergency
6 ordinance. (1) If an approved petition containing sufficient signatures is filed prior to the ordinance's effective
7 date or within 60 days after the passage of the ordinance, whichever is later, a petition requesting an election

9 by the electors.

11 date of an emergency ordinance, the emergency ordinance is suspended until it is ratified by the electors.
