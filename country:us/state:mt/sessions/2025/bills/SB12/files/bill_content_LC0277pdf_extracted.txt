69th Legislature 2025 LC 0277
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3 BY REQUEST OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATION
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT ELIMINATING THE BOARD OF COUNTY PRINTING AND
6 TRANSFERRING ITS DUTIES TO THE DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATION; PROVIDING RULEMAKING
7 AUTHORITY; AMENDING SECTIONS 18-7-401, 18-7-402, 18-7-404, AND 18-7-405, MCA; AND REPEALING
8 SECTIONS 2-15-1026 AND 18-7-403, MCA.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 Section 1. Section 18-7-401, MCA, is amended to read:
13 "18-7-401. Purpose of part. The purpose of this part is to provide:
14 (1) for the board of county printing department of administration to set maximum prices that may
15 be charged for county legal advertising; and
16 (2) for the purchase of county printed forms and materials."
17
18 Section 2. Section 18-7-402, MCA, is amended to read:
19 "18-7-402. Definition. Unless the context requires otherwise, in this part "board" "department" means
20 the board of county printing department of administration provided for in 2-15-1026 2-15-1001."
21
22 Section 3. Section 18-7-404, MCA, is amended to read:
23 "18-7-404. Establishment of maximum prices. (1) The board department shall adopt, by rule, and
24 publish a schedule of maximum prices to be charged for county legal advertising.
25 (2) The board department shall conduct hearings when required to determine maximum rates for
26 county legal advertising. Notice of the hearing must be mailed to the Montana association of counties and the
27 Montana newspaper association.
28 (3) The board department shall deliver, free of charge, to each board of county commissioners in
- 1 - LC 277
****
69th Legislature 2025 LC 0277
1 this state a copy of every schedule of maximum prices adopted by the board department within 30 days of its
2 publication, together with a notice of the date fixed by the board department when the prices will be effective.
3 (4) The county commissioners shall require each establishment that prints county legal advertising
4 to verify that:
5 (a) the legal advertisement was published on the dates ordered by the county and in the style set
6 by the board department; and
7 (b) the price was not in excess of the maximum price set by the board department.
8 (5) The board department may not establish maximum prices for printed county forms."
9
10 Section 4. Section 18-7-405, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "18-7-405. Adoption of printing standards. The board department shall adopt necessary standards
12 for typeface, type size, type style, and type leading for county legal advertising."
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Repealer. The following sections of the Montana Code Annotated are
15 repealed:
16 2-15-1026. Board of county printing -- composition -- allocation -- compensation.
17 18-7-403. Meetings of board of county printing.
18
19 NEW SECTION. Section 6. Transition. A person appointed to the board of county printing shall
20 serve until [the effective date of this act].
21 - END -
