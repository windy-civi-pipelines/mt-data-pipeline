19 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Provisional license -- limitations -- validity -- temporary license. (1)
20 The department may issue a provisional license to an applicant whom the department has initially determined
21 to be eligible for licensure, except for one or more of the following pending conditions:
22 (a) passage of the required licensure examination;
23 (b) completion of supervised work or educational experience as a license holder;
24 (c) facility or equipment inspection;
25 (d) verification of licensure in good standing from other licensing jurisdictions and applicable
26 national licensing databases; or
27 (e) verification of absence of relevant criminal charges or other action.

19 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Nonroutine application review by screening panel or full board. (1)
20 (a) A screening panel shall review all nonroutine applications on behalf of a board in accordance with the
21 provisions of 37-1-307.
22 (b) A board consisting of not more than five members may determine, by rule, to have nonroutine
23 applications reviewed by the full board instead of the screening panel.
24 (2) A screening panel or full board may grant, deny, or restrict a license and set conditions on a
25 restricted license. The screening panel or full board shall provide notice to the licensee or license applicant as
26 provided in 37-1-309. If the license is not granted, the notice must set forth proposed restrictions on the license
27 or that the license is denied.
