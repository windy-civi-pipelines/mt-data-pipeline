8 SECTION 3. SECTION 15-30-2318, MCA, IS AMENDED TO READ:
9 "15-30-2318. Earned income tax credit. (1) Except as provided in subsection (3), a resident
10 taxpayer is allowed as a credit against the tax imposed by 15-30-2103 a percentage of the credit allowed for the
11 federal earned income credit for which the individual taxpayer is eligible for the tax year under section 32 of the
12 Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 32.
13 (2) The amount of the credit allowed under subsection (1) is 10% 20% of the amount of the credit
14 determined for the tax year under section 32 of the Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 32.
15 (3) The credit is not allowed on earned income that is treated as a dividend received by a member
16 of an agricultural organization provided for in section 501(d) of the Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 501(d).
17 For the purpose of this subsection, the amount of the state tax credit provided for in subsection (2) is reduced
18 by the reduction percentage.
19 (4) The taxpayer is entitled to a refund equal to the amount by which the credit exceeds the
20 taxpayer's tax liability or, if the taxpayer has no tax liability under this chapter, a refund equal to the amount of
21 the credit. The credit may be claimed by filing a Montana income tax return.
22 (5) For the purpose of this section, the following definitions apply:
23 (a) "Earned income" means earned income, as defined in section 32 of the Internal Revenue
24 Code, 26 U.S.C. 32, that was used to determine the amount of the federal earned income tax credit under
25 subsection (2).
26 (b) "Reduction percentage" means a percentage that is calculated by dividing the earned income
27 that is disallowed under subsection (3) by the total amount of earned income."
