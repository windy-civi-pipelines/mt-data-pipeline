9 Section 3. Section 15-30-2318, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "15-30-2318. Earned income tax credit. (1) Except as provided in subsection (3), a resident
11 taxpayer is allowed as a credit against the tax imposed by 15-30-2103 a percentage of the credit allowed for the
12 federal earned income credit for which the individual taxpayer is eligible for the tax year under section 32 of the
13 Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 32.
14 (2) The amount of the credit allowed under subsection (1) is 10% 20% of the amount of the credit
15 determined for the tax year under section 32 of the Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 32.
16 (3) The credit is not allowed on earned income that is treated as a dividend received by a member
17 of an agricultural organization provided for in section 501(d) of the Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 501(d).
18 For the purpose of this subsection, the amount of the state tax credit provided for in subsection (2) is reduced
19 by the reduction percentage.
20 (4) The taxpayer is entitled to a refund equal to the amount by which the credit exceeds the
21 taxpayer's tax liability or, if the taxpayer has no tax liability under this chapter, a refund equal to the amount of
22 the credit. The credit may be claimed by filing a Montana income tax return.
23 (5) For the purpose of this section, the following definitions apply:
24 (a) "Earned income" means earned income, as defined in section 32 of the Internal Revenue
25 Code, 26 U.S.C. 32, that was used to determine the amount of the federal earned income tax credit under
26 subsection (2).
27 (b) "Reduction percentage" means a percentage that is calculated by dividing the earned income
28 that is disallowed under subsection (3) by the total amount of earned income."
