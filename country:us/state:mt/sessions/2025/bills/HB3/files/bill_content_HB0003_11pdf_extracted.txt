Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 3.1
2 INTRODUCED BY L. JONES, D. BEDEY
3 BY REQUEST OF THE OFFICE OF BUDGET AND PROGRAM PLANNING
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT APPROPRIATING MONEY TO VARIOUS STATE AGENCIES FOR
6 THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE 30, 2025; CONTINUING AN APPROPRIATION FOR THE BIENNIUM
7 BEGINNING JULY 1, 2025; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Time limits. Except as provided in [section 2(2)], the appropriations
12 contained in [section 2] are intended to provide necessary and ordinary expenditures for the fiscal year ending
13 June 30, 2025. The unspent balance of any appropriation must revert to the appropriate fund.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Appropriations -- authorization to expend money. (1) The following
16 money is appropriated, subject to the terms and conditions of [section 1]:
17 Agency and Program Amount Fund
18 Department of Revenue
19 Property Assessment Division $1,700,000 General Fund
20 Department of Public Health and Human Services Division
21 Health Facilities Division $22,200,000 General Fund
22 Department of Justice
23 Montana Highway Patrol $4,067,348 General Fund
24 Office of State Public Defender
25 Conflict Division $12,500,000 General Fund
26 Department of Corrections
27 Public Safety Division $11,550,000 General Fund
28 Rehabilitation & Programs Division $380,000 General Fund
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 3
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 3.1
1 Office of Public Instruction
2 State Level Activities $26,655 General Fund
3 State Auditor's Office
4 Insurance Division $3,870,205 State Special
5 Department of Fish, Wildlife, & Parks
6 Wildlife Division $950,000 State Special
7 Administration Division $500,000 State Special
8 (2) The appropriation for the department of revenue in subsection (1) continues into the biennium
9 beginning July 1, 2025.
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
12 - END -
