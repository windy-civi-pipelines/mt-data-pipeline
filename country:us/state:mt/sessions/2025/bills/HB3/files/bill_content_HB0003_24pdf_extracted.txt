- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0003.2
2 INTRODUCED BY L. JONES, D. BEDEY
3 BY REQUEST OF THE OFFICE OF BUDGET AND PROGRAM PLANNING
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT APPROPRIATING MONEY TO VARIOUS STATE AGENCIES FOR
6 THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE 30, 2025; CONTINUING AN APPROPRIATION FOR THE BIENNIUM
7 BEGINNING JULY 1, 2025;AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Time limits. Except as provided in [section 2(2)], the THE appropriations
12 contained in [section 2] are intended to provide necessary and ordinary expenditures for the fiscal year ending
13 June 30, 2025. The unspent balance of any appropriation must revert to the appropriate fund.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Appropriations -- authorization to expend money. (1) The following
16 money is appropriated, subject to the terms and conditions of [section 1]:
17 Agency and Program Amount Fund
18 Department of Revenue
19 Property Assessment Division $1,700,000 General Fund
20 Department of Public Health and Human Services Division
21 Health Facilities Division $22,200,000 $27,200,000 General Fund
22 Department of Justice
23 Montana Highway Patrol $4,067,348 General Fund
24 LEGAL SERVICES DIVISION $973,201 GENERAL FUND
25 Office of State Public Defender
26 Conflict Division $12,500,000 General Fund
27 Department of Corrections
28 Public Safety Division $11,550,000 $13,800,000 General Fund

- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0003.2
1 Rehabilitation & Programs Division $380,000 General Fund
2 Office of Public Instruction
3 State Level Activities DISTRIBUTION TO PUBLIC SCHOOLS $26,655
4 General Fund
5 State Auditor's Office
6 Insurance Division $3,870,205 State Special
7 Department of Fish, Wildlife, & Parks
8 Wildlife Division $950,000 State Special
9 Administration Division $500,000 State Special
10 (2) The appropriation for the department of revenue in subsection (1) continues into the biennium
11 beginning July 1, 2025.
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
14 - END -
