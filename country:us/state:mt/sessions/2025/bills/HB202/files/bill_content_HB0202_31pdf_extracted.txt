6 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO THE UNLAWFUL USE OF
7 AIRCRAFT FOR HUNTING; INCLUDING GAME BIRDS IN THE PROTECTED SPECIES; EXTENDING THE
8 WAITING PERIOD AFTER USE OF AN AIRCRAFT; BANNING THE POSSESSION OF UNMANNED AERIAL
9 VEHICLES WHILE HUNTING; INCREASING FINES AND PENALTIES; AND AMENDING SECTION 87-6-208,
10 MCA; AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES AND A TERMINATION DATE.”
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
13
14 Section 1. Section 87-6-208, MCA, is amended to read:
15 "87-6-208. Unlawful use of aircraft. (1) Except as provided in 87-3-126, a person may not:
16 (a) kill, take, or shoot at HUNT any game bird, game animal, or fur-bearing animal from an aircraft;
17 (b) use an aircraft: HUNT ANY GAME BIRD, GAME ANIMAL, OR FUR-BEARING ANIMAL WITHIN 24 HOURS
18 AFTER UTILIZING A PRIVATE AN AIRCRAFT TO LOCATE OR SPOT ANY GAME BIRD, GAME ANIMAL, OR FUR-BEARING ANIMAL;
19 (i) to locate any game animal for the purpose of hunting that animal during the same calendar day
20 within 48 hours after using the aircraft; or
21 (ii)(C) UTILIZE AN AIRCRAFT for the purpose of concentrating, pursuing, driving, rallying, or stirring up
22 any game bird, migratory bird, game animal, or fur-bearing animal; or
23 (c)(D) if in or using UTILIZING an aircraft, spot or locate any GAME BIRD, game animal, or fur-bearing
24 animal and communicate the location of the GAME BIRD, game animal, or fur-bearing animal to any person:
25 (i) on the ground by means of any air-to-ground communication signal or other device as an aid to
26 hunting or pursuing wildlife; or
27 (ii) within the same calendar day 48 hours 24 HOURS after using UTILIZING the aircraft; or
28 (d)(E) hunt or attempt to hunt any game bird, game animal, or fur-bearing animal while in possession

6 (D) WHEN SUPPORTING AN AGRICULTURAL OPERATION.
7 (3) The following penalties apply for a violation of this section:
8 (a) Unless otherwise provided in this subsection (3), a person convicted of a violation of this
9 section shall be fined not less than $300 $3,000 or more than $1,000 $10,000 or be imprisoned in the county
10 detention center for not more than 6 months, or both. In addition, the person, upon conviction or forfeiture of
11 bond or bail, may be subject to forfeiture of any current hunting, fishing, or trapping license issued by this state
12 and the privilege to hunt, fish, or trap in this state or to use state lands, as defined in 77-1-101, for recreational
13 purposes for a period of time set by the court for 36 months from the date of conviction or forfeiture unless the
14 court imposes a longer period.
15 (b) If a person is convicted or forfeits bond or bail after being charged with unlawful use of an
16 aircraft to kill or take a deer, elk, antelope, mountain lion, mountain sheep, moose, wild buffalo, caribou,
17 mountain goat, black bear, or grizzly bear, the person shall be fined not less than $500 $5,000 or more than
18 $2,000 $20,000 or be imprisoned in the county detention center for not more than 6 months, or both. In
19 addition, the person shall forfeit any current hunting, fishing, recreational use, or trapping license issued by this
20 state and the privilege to hunt, fish, or trap in this state for 30 36 months from the date of conviction or forfeiture

22 (c) If a person is convicted or forfeits bond or bail after being charged with unlawful use of an
23 aircraft to kill or take a fur-bearing animal, the person shall be fined not less than $100 $1,000 or more than

26 state and the privilege to hunt, fish, or trap in this state for 24 36 months from the date of conviction or forfeiture

15 hunting or pursuing wildlife; or
16 (ii) within the same calendar day after using utilizing the aircraft; or
17 (e) hunt or attempt to hunt any game bird, game animal, or fur-bearing animal while in possession

20 (a) during emergency situations;

23 (d) when supporting an agricultural operation.
