Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 SB 370.1
2 INTRODUCED BY M. YAKAWICH
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING THAT INDIVIDUALS SUMMONED FOR JURY DUTY
5 MUST RECEIVE $60 A DAY AND AN ADDITIONAL $60 A DAY IF SELECTED FOR JURY DUTY;
6 PROVIDING A FUNDING ALLOCATION; AND AMENDING SECTION 3-15-201, MCA.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 3-15-201, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "3-15-201. Fees in courts of record. (1) A grand or trial jury panel member must receive $12 $60 per
12 a day for attendance before any court of record and a mileage allowance, as provided in 2-18-503, for traveling
13 each way between the member's residence and the court. Those jurors selected from the panel for a case must
14 receive an additional $13 $60 a day while serving.
15 (2) A juror who is excused from attendance upon on the juror's own motion on the first day of
16 appearance in obedience to a notice or who has been summoned as a special juror and not sworn in the trial of
17 the case forfeits per diem and mileage."
18
19 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Funding allocation. There is funding allocated to the department of
20 justice to reimburse counties for the funding required by 3-15-201 in criminal cases. A county seeking
21 reimbursement shall provide an invoice to the department within 30 days of the final day of a criminal trial or
22 settlement of a criminal case if the settlement occurs after the jury is required to report to the courthouse or the
23 jury is empaneled.
24 - END -
