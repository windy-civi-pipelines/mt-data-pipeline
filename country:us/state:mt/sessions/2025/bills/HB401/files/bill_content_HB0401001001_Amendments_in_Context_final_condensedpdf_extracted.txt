20 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Cell-cultured edible product -- prohibition -- rulemaking authority --
21 definition. (1) It is unlawful for a person to manufacture for sale, sell, hold or offer for sale, or distribute cell-
22 cultured edible product in this state.
23 (2) The department may adopt rules to implement this section.
24 (3) As used in [section 2] and this section, the following definitions apply:
25 (a) "Cell-cultured edible product" has the same meaning as provided in 50-31-103.
26 (b) "Retail food establishment" has the same meaning as provided in 50-50-102.
