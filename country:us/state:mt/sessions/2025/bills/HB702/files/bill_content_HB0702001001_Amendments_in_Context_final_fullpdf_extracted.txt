13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Operating unauthorized, unmanned aerial vehicle over correctional
14 institution. (1) A person commits the offense of operating an unauthorized, unmanned aerial vehicle in
15 proximity to a correctional institution if the person purposely or knowingly operates or assists in the operation of
16 an unmanned aerial vehicle over a correctional institution without the authorization of the correctional institution.
17 (2) A person convicted of the offense of operating an unauthorized, unmanned aerial vehicle in
18 proximity to a correctional institution shall be imprisoned in a state prison for a term of not less than 1 year or
19 more than 5 years.
