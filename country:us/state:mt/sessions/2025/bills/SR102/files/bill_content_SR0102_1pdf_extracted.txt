Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 102.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 102
2 INTRODUCED BY W. GALT
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE COAL BOARD MADE BY THE GOVERNOR AND
6 SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED FEBRUARY 19, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
9 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
10 As members of the Coal Board, in accordance with section 2-15-1821, MCA:
11 Hal Fuglevand, Billings, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
12 Catherine Laughner, Big Sky, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
13 Scott Rosenthal, Butte, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
14 Jon Wells, Hardin, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 3, 2029.
15
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
17 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
18 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
19 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
20 MCA.
21 - END -
