Section 1:

69th Legislature 2025 SB 38.1
2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ
3 BY REQUEST OF THE SENATE SELECT COMMITTEE ON JUDICIAL OVERSIGHT AND REFORM
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS TO PROHIBIT ATTORNEY FEE AWARDS
6 AGAINST A GOVERNMENT ENTITY RELATING TO LEGISLATIVE VETO OVERRIDES; PROHIBITING
7 ATTORNEY FEES AGAINST A GOVERNMENT ENTITY WHEN THE RESULT OF A LEGISLATIVE VETO
8 OVERRIDE POLL DOES NOT RESULT IN AN OVERRIDE OF A VETO; AMENDING SECTION 25-10-711,
9 MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 25-10-711, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "25-10-711. Award of costs against governmental government entity when suit or defense is
15 frivolous or pursued in bad faith -- award of costs related to legislative veto override poll. (1) In any civil
16 action brought by or against the state, a political subdivision, or an agency of the state or a political subdivision,
17 the opposing party, whether plaintiff or defendant, is entitled to the costs enumerated in 25-10-201 and
18 reasonable attorney fees as determined by the court if:
19 (a) the opposing party prevails against the state, political subdivision, or agency; and
20 (b) the court finds that the claim or defense of the state, political subdivision, or agency that
21 brought or defended the action was frivolous or pursued in bad faith.
22 (2) In an action in which a party seeks a writ of mandamus to compel a legislative veto override
23 poll and the result of the poll does not override the veto, the opposing party is not considered to have prevailed.
24 This subsection applies to an award of costs against a government entity even if a court finds that the defense
25 was frivolous or pursued in bad faith.
26 (2)(3) Costs may be granted pursuant to subsection (1) notwithstanding any other provision of the law
27 to the contrary."
28

69th Legislature 2025 SB 38.1
1 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
2
3 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Applicability. [This act] applies to actions relating to a legislative veto
4 override poll commenced on or after [the effective date of this act].
5 - END -
