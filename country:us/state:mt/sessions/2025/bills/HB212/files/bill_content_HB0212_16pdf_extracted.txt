7 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT LIMITING THE LIABILITY OF AGRICULTURAL VEHICLES
8 OPERATED ON HIGHWAYS; PROVIDING DEFINITIONS; AMENDING SECTIONS 61-1-101, 61-3-317, 61-5-
9 112, AND 61-8-102, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN APPLICABILITY DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Limited liability -- agricultural vehicles on highway. (1) The owner,
14 lessee, or operator of an agricultural vehicle operated on a highway is not liable in tort action for damages
15 suffered as a result of an act or omission of the owner, lessee, or operator in excess of $750,000 for each claim
16 and $1.5 million for each occurrence.
17 (2) An insurer is not liable for excess damages unless the insurer agrees by written endorsement
18 to provide coverage to the owner, lessee, or operator of an agricultural vehicle involved in amounts in excess of
19 a limitation stated in this section, in which case the insurer may not claim the benefits of the limitation
20 specifically waived.
21 (3) As used in this section, "agricultural vehicle" means:
22 (a) a farm tractor as defined in 61-9-102;
23 (b) an implement of husbandry as defined in 61-1-101;
24 (c) a self-propelled farm equipment unit;
25 (d) a combination of farm tractor and towed unit of farm equipment or implement of husbandry; or
26 (e) a vehicle used for hauling hay, straw, or both.
