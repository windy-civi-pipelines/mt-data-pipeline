69th Legislature 2025 LC 3229
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING CRIMINAL PROCEDURE LAWS;
5 REQUIRING A SEARCH WARRANT TO BE EXHIBITED TO A KNOWN LAWFUL OCCUPANT OF A
6 PROPERTY TO BE SEARCHED; AMENDING SECTION 46-5-227, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE
7 EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 46-5-227, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "46-5-227. Service and return of search warrant. Service of a search warrant is made by exhibiting
13 the original warrant or a duplicate original warrant at the place to be searched, or to the person to be searched,
14 or to a known lawful occupant of a property to be searched. The officer taking property under the warrant shall
15 give to the person from whom or from whose premises the property is taken a copy of the search warrant and a
16 receipt for the property taken or shall leave the copy and receipt at the place from which the property was
17 taken. Failure to leave a copy and receipt may not render the property seized inadmissible at trial."
18
19 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
20 - END -
