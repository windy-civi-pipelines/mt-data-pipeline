11 CANDIDATES FOR LOCAL AND STATE OFFICE TO FILE FOR OFFICE PRIOR TO THE PRIMARY
12 ELECTION; PROHIBITING UNSUCCESSFUL PRIMARY CANDIDATES FROM SEEKING NOMINATION IN

20 Section 1. Section 13-1-403, MCA, is amended to read:
21 "13-1-403. Election deadlines for candidate filing, write-in candidacy, and withdrawal -- election
22 cancellation -- election by acclamation. (1) Consistent with the candidate filing deadline in 13-10-201(7) for
23 primary elections and except as provided in subsection (2) for a write-in candidate, the candidate filing deadline
24 for election to a local government office is no sooner than 145 105 days and no later than 85 90 days before the
25 election.
26 (2) (a) A declaration of intent to be a write-in candidate must be filed with the election administrator
27 by 5 p.m. on the 65th 90th day before the date of the election.
