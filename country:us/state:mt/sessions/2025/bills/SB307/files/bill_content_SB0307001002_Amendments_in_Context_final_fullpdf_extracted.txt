4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE DISTRIBUTION OF MARIJUANA TAX REVENUE;
5 PROVIDING THAT A PORTION OF MARIJUANA TAX REVENUE BE USED FOR PREVENTION SERVICES,

7 GRANTS; CREATING A MARIJUANA TAX REVENUE ACCOUNTABILITY COUNCIL; PROVIDING FOR A

9 EXPANDING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; DISTRIBUTING MARIJUANA TAX REVENUE TO COUNTIES
10 IN PROPORTION TO MARIJUANA SALES AND IN PROPORTION TO POPULATION WITHIN A COUNTY;

17 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Marijuana prevention account -- rules. (1) There is a marijuana
18 prevention account in the state special revenue fund established in 17-2-102. The account consists of money
19 transferred to the account pursuant to 16-12-111.
20 (2) Money in the account must be used by the department of public health and human services to
21 fund the marijuana tax revenue accountability council provided for in [section 2] and to provide grants for
22 primary substance misuse prevention and youth suicide prevention in consultation with the council.
23 (3) The programs must be designed to:
24 (a) improve access to, utilization of, and engagement and retention in primary substance misuse
25 prevention services; and
26 (b) expand the availability of community-based services that reflect best practices or are evidence-
27 based.

16 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Marijuana impact report. The marijuana tax revenue accountability
