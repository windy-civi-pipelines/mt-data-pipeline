17 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Marijuana prevention account -- rules .. (1) There is a marijuana
18 prevention account in the state special revenue fund established in 17-2-102. The account consists of money
19 transferred to the account pursuant to 16-12-111.

21 (2) Money in the account must be used by the department of public health and human services to
22 fund the marijuana tax revenue accountability council provided for in [section 2] and to provide grants for

25 (a) improve access to, utilization of, and engagement and retention in primary substance misuse
26 prevention services; and

28 based.
