10 NEW SECTION. Section 11. Delivery training program -- rulemaking. (1) The department shall
11 certify all delivery training programs that include the following:
12 (a) methods to identify and prevent sales to underage or intoxicated persons;
13 (b) strategies used by underage persons to obtain alcohol;
14 (c) acceptable forms of identification and procedures for checking identification;
15 (d) actions that can be taken to prevent an alcohol-related incident from occurring; and
16 (e) other requirements adopted by the department supported by statute or rule relating to the
17 delivery of alcoholic beverages.
18 (2) On receipt of a proposed delivery training program from the holder of a third-party alcohol
19 delivery license, the department has 30 business days to approve, deny, or request modifications to the
20 proposed training program.
21 (3) The department may adopt rules to implement this section.
22
23 NEW SECTION. Section 12. Codification instruction. (1) [Section 1] is intended to be codified as
24 an integral part of Title 16, chapter 4, and the provisions of Title 16, chapter 4, apply to [section 1].
