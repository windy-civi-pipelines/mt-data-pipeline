Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 91.1
2 INTRODUCED BY M. THANE
3 BY REQUEST OF THE DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING TAXPAYERS WITH THE OPTION TO RECEIVE
6 ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE; PROVIDING RULEMAKING
7 AUTHORITY; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Electronic taxpayer communications -- rulemaking. (1) Except as
12 otherwise provided by law, the department may send correspondence, letters, notices, and other
13 communications electronically to a taxpayer when the taxpayer elects, on a form provided by the department, to
14 receive electronic communications from the department.
15 (2) The department shall adopt rules necessary to administer this section.
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
18 integral part of Title 15, chapter 1, part 2, and the provisions of Title 15, chapter 1, part 2, apply to [section 1].
19
20 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
21 - END -
