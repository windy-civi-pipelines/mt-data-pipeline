3 SPRUNGER, K. SEEKINS-CROWE, J. LYNCH
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE; ADDING MEMBERS
6 TO THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE; AUTHORIZING THE COMMISSIONER OF THE DEPARTMENT OF
7 LABOR AND INDUSTRY TO APPOINT MEMBERS TO THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE; COORDINATING
8 THE MEETING OF THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE WITH THE BOARD OF REGENTS, THE BOARD OF
9 PUBLIC EDUCATION, AND THE MONTANA STATE WORKFORCE INNOVATION BOARD; REQUIRING THE
10 STATE PLAN COMMITTEE TO COORDINATE WITH THE PERKINS STATE PLAN IN THE WORKFORCE
11 INNOVATION AND OPPORTUNITY ACT COMBINED STATE PLAN; AND AMENDING SECTION 20-7-330,
12 MCA.”
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
15
16 Section 1. Section 20-7-330, MCA, is amended to read:
17 "20-7-330. Creation of state plan committee -- meetings -- report. (1) (a) THE COMMISSIONER OF
18 HIGHER EDUCATION AND THE SUPERINTENDENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION SHALL FORM A STATE PLAN COMMITTEE AS
19 NECESSARY TO UPDATE THE STATE PLAN FOR CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION AS REQUIRED BY 20 U.S.C. 2301.
20 (B) The superintendent of public instruction and the commissioner OF HIGHER EDUCATION shall each
21 appoint three people from their respective advisory boards to serve on a committee to review and update the 5-
22 year state plan for career and technical education as required by 20 U.S.C. 2323. Two members appointed
23 from each advisory board must be educators, and the remaining member appointed from each advisory board
24 must be a representative of a business or community interest TO SERVE ON THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE AS
25 FOLLOWS:
26 (I) TWO EDUCATORS; AND
27 (II) ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF A BUSINESS OR COMMUNITY INTEREST.
28 (C) THE SUPERINTENDENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION SHALL APPOINT THREE PEOPLE TO SERVE ON THE

1 STATE PLAN COMMITTEE AS FOLLOWS:
2
3 (I) TWO EDUCATORS; AND
4 (II) ONE REPRESENTATIVE OF A BUSINESS OR COMMUNITY INTEREST.
5
6 (b) (D) The commissioner of labor and industry shall appoint three TWO people to serve on the state
7 plan committee as follows:
8 (i) one representative THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR OR THE BOARD CHAIR of the Montana state
9 workforce innovation board provided for in 53-2-1203; AND
10 (ii) one representative of the business community; and
11 (iii) (II) one representative of the labor community.
12 (2) (A) At least four times a year, In conjunction with the quarterly Montana state workforce
13 innovation board meetings, the board of regents shall meet with the superintendent of public instruction, AT
14 LEAST THREE TIMES A YEAR, THE COMMISSIONER OF HIGHER EDUCATION AND THE SUPERINTENDENT OF PUBLIC
15 INSTRUCTION SHALL MEET WITH the commissioner of labor and industry, teachers, students, labor organizations,
16 businesses, and institutions or agencies involved in vocational and technical education. THE MEETINGS MUST
17 TAKE PLACE DURING A REGULAR MEETING OF EACH OF THE FOLLOWING ENTITIES:
18 (I) THE BOARD OF REGENTS;
19
20 (II) THE BOARD OF PUBLIC EDUCATION; AND
21 (III) THE STATE WORKFORCE INNOVATION BOARD.
22 (B) DURING THE MEETINGS REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (2)(A), THE STATE PLAN COMMITTEE SHALL, AT
23 A MINIMUM to:
24 (a) (I) discuss the state plan;
25 (b) (II) identify any issues or concerns with the administration IN MONTANA of the Carl D. Perkins
26 Career and Technical Education Improvement Act of 2006 STRENGTHENING CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION
27 FOR THE 21ST CENTURY ACT OF 2018 in Montana;
28 (c) (III) identify the needs of technical students, employers, and career programs in Montana and
- 2 - Authorized Print Version – HB 823
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0823.2
1 determine the best way to meet those needs; and
2 (d) (IV) if necessary, make changes in the administration and operation IN MONTANA of the Carl D.
3 Perkins Career and Technical Education Improvement Act of 2006 STRENGTHENING CAREER AND TECHNICAL
4 EDUCATION FOR THE 21ST CENTURY ACT OF 2018 in Montana.
5 (3) If the state plan committee submits a new or revised 5-year 4-YEAR state plan in accordance
6 with subsection (2)(d) (2)(B)(IV), the plan must be written to coordinate with or to be included in the federal
7 Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act combined state plan as provided in 53-2-1205.
8 (3)(4) The board of regents shall report the results of the meetings required in subsection (2) to the
9 legislature in accordance with the provisions of 5-11-210."
10
11 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. DIRECTIONS TO CODE COMMISSIONER. WHENEVER A REFERENCE TO "5-YEAR
12 STATE PLAN FOR CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION REPORTS" APPEARS IN TITLE 5 OR TITLE 20 AND IN ALL LAW
13 ENACTED BY THE 69TH LEGISLATURE, THE CODE COMMISSIONER IS DIRECTED TO CHANGE IT TO A REFERENCE TO "4-
14 YEAR STATE PLAN FOR CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION REPORTS".
15 - END -
