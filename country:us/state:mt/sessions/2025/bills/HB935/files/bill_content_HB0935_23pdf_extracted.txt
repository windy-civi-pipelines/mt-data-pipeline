- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0935.2
2 INTRODUCED BY M. THIEL, B. CARTER, S. MANESS, R. MINER, G. OBLANDER, G. PARRY, J.
3 SCHILLINGER
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR DEPARTMENT SUSPENSION OR CLOSURE
6 OF NEW GROUND WATER APPROPRIATIONS IN THE FOX HILLS-HELL CREEK AQUIFER; PROVIDING
7 FOR A FEASIBILITY STUDY OF GROUND WATER AVAILABILITY; PROVIDING REPORTING
8 REQUIREMENTS; APPROPRIATING FUNDS; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND A
9 TERMINATION DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Fox Hills-Hell Creek aquifer -- temporary suspension of action on
14 permits -- groundwater study. (1) In order to balance the need for continued development of Montana's water
15 and for protection of existing water rights in the Fox Hills-Hell Creek aquifer, the department may SHALL
16 suspend action on a class of applications or may close an aquifer source and refuse to accept a class of
17 applications, or both, for a permit to appropriate ground water from that source pursuant to this part.
18 (2) Pursuant to subsection (1), the department may suspend or close permit applications to
19 appropriate ground water from the Fox Hills-Hell Creek aquifer within department administrative boundaries
20 39E, 39FJ, 39G, 39H, 40D, 40E, 40P, 40Q, 40R, 40S, 42A, 42C, 42J, 42K, 42KJ, 42L, and 42M if the proposed
21 appropriation is 3,000 gallons a minute or more EXCEEDS THE PERMIT EXCEPTIONS PROVIDED IN 85-2-306.
22 (3) The bureau of mines and geology shall complete a feasibility study on ground water availability
23 in the department administrative boundaries listed in subsection (2). THE STUDY MAY INCLUDE:
24 (A) THE COMPILATION AND UPDATE OF NEW GEOLOGIC AND HYDROGEOLOGIC DATA;
25 (B) A LIMITED FIELD COLLECTION OF WATER LEVELS AND PRESSURE FOR WELLS WITH HISTORICAL DATA;
26 (C) A PRELIMINARY WATER BUDGET FOR THE FOX HILLS-HELL CREEK AQUIFER; AND
27 (D) A PRELIMINARY MODEL TO EVALUATE THE EFFECTS OF PUMPING ON WATER LEVELS AND PRESSURE.
28 (4) THE BUREAU SHALL REPORT THE PROGRESS AND RESULTS OF ITS STUDY TO THE WATER POLICY
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 935
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0935.2
1 INTERIM COMMITTEE ESTABLISHED IN 5-5-231, IN ACCORDANCE WITH 5-11-210.
2
3 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Transfer. By June 30, 2025, the state treasurer shall transfer $5,000
4 $50,000 FROM THE GENERAL FUND to the ground water assessment account in 85-2-905.
5
6 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Appropriation. There is appropriated $5,000 $50,000 from the general
7 fund to the bureau of mines and geology for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, for a feasibility study
8 pursuant to subsection (3) of [section 1].
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
11 integral part of Title 85, chapter 2, part 3, and the provisions of Title 85, chapter 2, part 3, apply to [section 1].
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 6. Termination. [This act] terminates June 30, 2027.
16 - END -
