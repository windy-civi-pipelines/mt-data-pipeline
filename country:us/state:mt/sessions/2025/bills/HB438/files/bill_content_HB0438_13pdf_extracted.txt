18 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Board of physical, rehabilitative, and developmental health care
19 professionals. (1) In accordance with 37-1-123, there is a board of physical, rehabilitative, and developmental
20 health care professionals.
21 (2) The board consists of 12 members:
22 (a) two athletic trainers;
23 (b) two occupational therapists;
24 (c) four physical therapists;
25 (d) two speech-language pathologists;
26 (e) one audiologist; and
27 (f) one public member.
28 (3) The board is allocated to the department as prescribed in 2-15-121.

69th Legislature 2025 HB 438.1
1
2 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Purpose. The legislature declares it to be a policy of this state that in
3 order to help ensure the availability of services and protect the health, safety, and welfare of the citizens of this
4 state, it is a matter of public interest and concern that the health professions regulated by the board warrant and
5 receive the confidence of the public and that only qualified persons be permitted to practice in each respective
6 field from within or outside of the state and engage with clients located within the state.
7
8 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context requires
9 otherwise, the following definitions apply:
10 (1) "Board" means the board of physical, rehabilitative, and developmental health care
11 professionals provided for in [section 1].
12 (2) "Department" means the department of labor and industry provided for in Title 2, chapter 15,
13 part 17.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Duties of board -- rulemaking authority. (1) The board shall:
16 (a) administer, coordinate, and enforce the provisions of Title 37, chapters 11, 15, 24, and 36, and
17 [sections 2 through 4];
18 (b) for each profession licensed under the chapters listed in subsection (1)(a), establish criteria for
19 licensure;
20 (c) for each chapter listed under subsection (1)(a), adopt fees in accordance with 37-1-134 for the
21 following:
22 (i) applications for licensure;
23 (ii) examinations;
24 (iii) initial license issuance; and
25 (iv) license renewal; and
26 (d) authorize the department to issue and renew licenses to qualified applicants.
27 (2) The board may adopt rules necessary to implement the provisions of chapters 11, 15, 24, and
28 36, and [sections 2 through 4].
