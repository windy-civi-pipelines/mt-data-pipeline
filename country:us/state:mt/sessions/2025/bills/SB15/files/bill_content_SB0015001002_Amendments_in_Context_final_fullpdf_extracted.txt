69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Julianne Burkhardt, SB0015.001.002
1 SENATE BILL NO. 15
2 INTRODUCED BY T. MCGILLVRAY
3 BY REQUEST OF THE SENATE SELECT COMMITTEE ON JUDICIAL OVERSIGHT AND REFORM
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR ADDITIONAL GROUNDS FOR THE
6 IMPEACHMENT OF OFFICERS; AND AMENDING SECTION 5-5-401, MCA.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 5-5-401, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "5-5-401. Officers liable to impeachment. The governor, executive officers, heads of state
12 departments, and judicial officers are liable to impeachment for felonies, and misdemeanors, and
13 misdemeanors or malfeasance in corruption, incompetence, negligence in the performance of one's duties,
14 willful neglect of duty, oppressive use of one's office, or misconduct in one's office."
15 - END -
