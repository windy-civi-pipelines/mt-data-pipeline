69th Legislature 2025 LC 2320
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE MEMBERSHIP REQUIREMENTS OF A COUNTY
5 PLANNING BOARD; AND AMENDING SECTION 76-1-211, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 76-1-211, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "76-1-211. Membership of county planning board. (1) County planning boards consist of not less
11 than five members appointed by the board of county commissioners. At least one member of a county planning
12 board existing on or formed after July 1, 1973, must may be a member of the governing board of a conservation
13 district as provided for in chapter 15, an associate member of a conservation district designated by the
14 governing board of a conservation district, or a member of a state cooperative grazing district if officers of either
15 of the districts or the designated associate member of a conservation district reside resides in the county.
16 (2) If a city or town subsequently becomes represented on the county planning board pursuant to
17 76-1-111, additional members of the planning board representing the cities or towns must be appointed by the
18 respective city councils."
19 - END -
