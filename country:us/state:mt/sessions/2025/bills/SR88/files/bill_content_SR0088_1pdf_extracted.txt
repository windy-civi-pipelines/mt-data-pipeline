Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 88.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 88
2 INTRODUCED BY B. GILLESPIE
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE BOARD OF MILK CONTROL MADE BY THE GOVERNOR
6 AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED MARCH 21, 2025, TO THE SENATE.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
9 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
10 As members of the Board of Milk Control, in accordance with section 2-15-3105, MCA:
11 Brian Beerman, Fairfield, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
12 Ken Bryan, Roundup, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
13 Staci Ketchum, Miles City, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
14 W. Scott Mitchell, Billings, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
15 Travis Stroh, Glendive, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
16
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
18 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
19 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
20 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
21 MCA.
22 - END -
