Section 1:

69th Legislature 2025 SB 207.1
2 INTRODUCED BY D. EMRICH, G. LAMMERS, S. VINTON, J. TREBAS, B. BEARD
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROHIBITING THE BANNING OF USERS FROM DIGITAL
5 PUBLIC SQUARES; PROVIDING DEFINITIONS; PROVIDING EXCEPTIONS; AND PROVIDING
6 PENALTIES.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Digital public squares -- prohibition against banning access --
11 definitions. (1) A business or entity providing a digital public square may not limit or abridge an individual
12 user's right to assemble or exercise freedom of speech in a digital public square through digital prohibition, as
13 defined in this section, unless in violation of state or federal laws.
14 (2) A business or entity providing a digital public square may not censor user-created content
15 through any means, including prohibiting other users from viewing user-created content.
16 (3) A business or entity may not create community standards, contracts, or terms and conditions
17 that violate subsection (2) or (3).
18 (4) A business or entity in violation of subsection (2), (3), or (4) may be liable for actual and
19 punitive damages in an action brought by a user of a digital public square.
20 (5) A business or entity is not in violation of subsection (2) if it offers advertisements to users of a
21 digital public square.
22 (6) A digital public square may be terminated by the business or entity providing the digital public
23 square.
24 (7) For the purposes of this section, the following definitions apply:
25 (a) "Deplatforming" means removing a user of a digital public square without the request of the
26 user.
27 (b) "Digital prohibition" means banning, deplatforming, or censoring a user of a digital public
28 square through any means, including but not limited to the use of shadow banning, algorithms, monitoring,

2 (c) "Digital public square" means an online public platform provided free of charge to some or all

5 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
