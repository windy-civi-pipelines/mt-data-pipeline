9
10 Section 1. Section 13-27-302, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "13-27-302. Certification of signatures. (1) An affidavit, in substantially the following form, must be
12 attached to each sheet or section submitted to the county official:
13 I, (name of person who is the signature gatherer), swear that I gathered the signatures on the petition
14 to which this affidavit is attached on the stated dates, that I believe the signatures on the petition are genuine,
15 are the signatures of the persons whose names they purport to be, and are the signatures of Montana electors

1 the county election administrator within 4 weeks of the first collected signature covered by the affidavit BY 84
2 DAYS BEFORE THE FINAL DATE PROVIDED IN 13-27-104 FOR FILING THE PETITION.
3 (ii) For an affidavit provided for in subsection (1) that is attached to a signature sheet with a
4 collected signature dated between 49 and 97 days prior to the final date provided in 13-27-104 for filing the
5 petition, the signature gatherer or an agent of the group gathering signatures shall submit the affidavit and the
6 signature sheet or the section to which the affidavit is attached to the county election administrator by 35 days
7 before the final date provided in 13-27-104 for filing the petition.
8 (b) ONCE THE AFFIDAVIT PROVIDED FOR IN SUBSECTION (1) IS SUBMITTED, THE ELECTION ADMINISTRATOR
9 SHALL DATE AND TIME STAMP THE AFFIDAVIT, REGISTER IT IN A LOG, AND PROVIDE A RECEIPT OF SUBMISSION TO THE
10 SIGNATURE GATHERER OR AGENT OF THE GROUP GATHERING SIGNATURES.
11 (b)(c) If the affidavit and the signature sheet or the section to which the affidavit is attached are
12 SUBJECT TO THE a DEADLINE IN SUBSECTION (2)(A) AND ARE not submitted within 4 weeks of the earliest signature
13 covered by the affidavit THE TIMELINE REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (2)(A), the county election administrator shall
14 reject the filing and may not process it further."
15
16 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
17 - END -
