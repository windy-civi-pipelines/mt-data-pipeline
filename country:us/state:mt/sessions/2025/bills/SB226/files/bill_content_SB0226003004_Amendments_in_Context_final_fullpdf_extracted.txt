1 SENATE BILL NO. 226
2 INTRODUCED BY M. CUFFE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING CERTIFICATION OF PETITION SIGNATURES TO
5 ADD A FILING TIMELINE; AMENDING SECTION 13-27-302, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE
6 EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 Section 1. Section 13-27-302, MCA, is amended to read:
11 "13-27-302. Certification of signatures. (1) An affidavit, in substantially the following form, must be
12 attached to each sheet or section submitted to the county official:
13 I, (name of person who is the signature gatherer), swear that I gathered the signatures on the petition
14 to which this affidavit is attached on the stated dates, that I believe the signatures on the petition are genuine,
15 are the signatures of the persons whose names they purport to be, and are the signatures of Montana electors
16 who are registered at the address or have the telephone number following the person's signature, and that the
17 signers knew the contents of the petition before signing the
18 petition.______________________________________(Date on which the first signature was
19 gathered)______________________________________(Signature of petition signature
20 gatherer)______________________________________(Address of petition signature gatherer)
21 Subscribed and sworn to before me this ___ day of _________, 20__
22 Seal______________________________________(Person authorized to take
23 oaths)______________________________________(Title or notarial information)
24 (2) (a) (i) The For an affidavit provided for in subsection (1) that is attached to a signature sheet
25 with a collected signature dated 126 days or more prior to the final date provided in 13-27-104 for filing the
26 petition, and the county where the signature sheet is to be submitted has a total number of 20,000 or more
27 registered electors at the time of the most recent general election, the signature gatherer or agent of the group

1 gathering signatures shall submit the affidavit and the signature sheet or section to which the affidavit is
2 attached to the county election administrator by 112 days before the final date provided for in 13-27-104 for
3 filing the petition.
4 (ii) FOR AN AFFIDAVIT PROVIDED FOR IN SUBSECTION (1) THAT IS ATTACHED TO A SIGNATURE SHEET WITH
5 A COLLECTED SIGNATURE DATED 98 DAYS OR MORE PRIOR TO THE FINAL DATE PROVIDED IN 13-27-104 FOR FILING THE
6 PETITION, THE signature gatherer or an agent of the group gathering signatures shall submit the affidavit
7 provided for in subsection (1) and the signature sheet or the section to which the affidavit is attached to the
8 county election administrator within 4 weeks of the first collected signature covered by the affidavit BY 84 DAYS
9 BEFORE THE FINAL DATE PROVIDED IN 13-27-104 FOR FILING THE PETITION.
10 (b) ONCE THE AFFIDAVIT PROVIDED FOR IN SUBSECTION (1) IS SUBMITTED, THE ELECTION ADMINISTRATOR
11 SHALL DATE AND TIME STAMP THE AFFIDAVIT, REGISTER IT IN A LOG, AND PROVIDE A RECEIPT OF SUBMISSION TO THE
12 SIGNATURE GATHERER OR AGENT OF THE GROUP GATHERING SIGNATURES.
13 (b)(c) If the affidavit and the signature sheet or the section to which the affidavit is attached are
14 SUBJECT TO THE DEADLINE IN SUBSECTION (2)(A)(i) or (2)(a)(ii) AND ARE not submitted within 4 weeks of the earliest
15 signature covered by the affidavit THE TIMELINE REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION (2)(A)(i) or (2)(a)(ii), the county
16 election administrator shall reject the filing and may not process it further."
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
19 - END -
