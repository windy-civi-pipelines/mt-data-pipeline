Section 1:
Labor and Economic Affairs
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 Drafter: Matthew Weaver, HB0726.001.001
1 HOUSE BILL NO. 726
2 INTRODUCED BY N. NICOL
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING SURETY BAIL BOND INSURANCE;
5 REVISING QUALIFICATIONS FOR SURETY BAIL BOND INSURANCE; REVISING SURETY BAIL BOND
6 INSURANCE QUALIFICATIONS AND CONTINUING EDUCATION; PROVIDING FOR DISCLOSURE OF
7 FEES; AND AMENDING SECTIONS 33-17-1601 AND 33-17-1602, MCA.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 33-17-1601, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "33-17-1601. Special qualifications for surety bail bond insurance license. (1) Before approving
13 an application for a surety bail bond insurance license, the commissioner shall verify that the individual:
14 (a) is a natural person at least 21 18 years of age;
15 (b) is a citizen of the United States or is lawfully entitled to remain and work in the United States;
16 (c) is a resident of the state and maintains a principal fixed place of business or is a full-time agent
17 within the state;
18 (c)(d) has obtained a high school diploma, a general equivalency diploma or equivalent document, or
19 an equivalent education as determined by the commissioner;
20 (d)(e) has complied with the requirements of 33-17-211; and
21 (e)(f) has successfully completed the training required in 33-17-1602.
22 (2) An individual may not receive, renew, or hold a surety bail bond license if the individual:
23 (a) has been convicted of a felony in this state or of any offense committed in another state that
24 would be a felony if committed in this state; or
25 (b) has been convicted of an offense involving dishonesty, a breach of trust, violence, threatened
26 violence, or the unlawful use, sale, or possession of a controlled substance."
27
