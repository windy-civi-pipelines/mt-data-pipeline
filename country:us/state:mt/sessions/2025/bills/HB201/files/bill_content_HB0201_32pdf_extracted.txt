9 SPRUNGER, E. TILLEMAN, M. VINTON, K. WALSH, Z. WIRTH, K. ZOLNIKOV
10
11 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REQUIRING PAID SIGNATURE GATHERERS TO DISCLOSE
12 THEIR STATUS AND TO PROVIDE THEIR STATE OF LEGAL RESIDENCE; AND PROVIDING AN
13 IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Paid signature gatherers -- disclosure requirements. (1) A paid
18 signature gatherer, as defined in 13-27-112, shall VERBALLY disclose their FIRST NAME, THE STATE IN WHICH THEY
19 LEGALLY RESIDE, AND THEIR status as a paid signature gatherer verbally and by wearing a badge stating this
20 information to anyone the paid signature gatherer approach es about signing a petition TO ANYONE THEY
21 APPROACH ABOUT SIGNING A PETITION. THE PAID SIGNATURE GATHERER SHALL ALSO WEAR A BADGE STATING THIS
22 INFORMATION, INCLUDING THEIR FULL FIRST NAME AND LAST INITIAL.
23 (2) The disclosure provided for in subsection (1) must include the state the paid signature gatherer
24 legally resides in.
25
26 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
27 integral part of Title 13, chapter 27, and the provisions of Title 13, chapter 27, apply to [section 1].
28
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 201
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0201.3
1 NEW SECTION. SECTION 3. SEVERABILITY. IF A PART OF [THIS ACT] IS INVALID, ALL VALID PARTS THAT ARE
2 SEVERABLE FROM THE INVALID PART REMAIN IN EFFECT. IF A PART OF [THIS ACT] IS INVALID IN ONE OR MORE OF ITS
3 APPLICATIONS, THE PART REMAINS IN EFFECT IN ALL VALID APPLICATIONS THAT ARE SEVERABLE FROM THE INVALID
4 APPLICATIONS.
5
6 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
7 - END -
