- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0228.3
2 INTRODUCED BY B. MERCER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING REQUIREMENTS FOR THE REPORTING OF ACTUAL
5 COSTS FOR LEGISLATION WITH PROJECTED FISCAL IMPACTS; MODIFYING THE REPORTING PERIOD
6 TO EVERY SIX MONTHS; REVISING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; REPEALING A TERMINATION DATE;
7 AMENDING SECTION 5-4-208, MCA; REPEALING SECTION 6, CHAPTER 350, LAWS OF 2021; AND
8 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.”
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 Section 1. Section 5-4-208, MCA, is amended to read:
13 "5-4-208. (Temporary) Report Biannual report REPORT of actual costs for legislation with
14 projected fiscal impact. (1) Except for the general appropriations act and any bill exempted by the legislature,
15 for each bill with an estimated expenditure amount of over $1 million $500,000 from the general fund in any 1 of
16 the 4 fiscal years identified in the bill's fiscal note, or as required in the bill, that is passed and approved for
17 which a fiscal note is prepared and presented to the legislature, the office of budget and program planning shall
18 prepare and present an annual a AN ANNUAL report to the legislative finance committee by October 1 of each
19 year every February and October BY OCTOBER 1 OF EACH YEAR.
20 (2) (a) The February report The report must include the following information:
21 (a)(i)(A)the name of the bill;
22 (b)(ii)(B)the actual expenditures incurred based on the bill as enacted for the past entire fiscal year
23 during the first 2 quarters of the preceding fiscal year, FOR THE PAST ENTIRE FISCAL YEAR and a description of how
24 the funds were expended; and
25 (c)(iii)(C) a comparison to the projected costs of the bill as projected in the fiscal note for that
26 same fiscal year period of time FISCAL YEAR.
27 (b) The October report must include the following information:
28 (i) the name of the bill;
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HB 228
- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0228.3
1 (ii) the actual expenditures incurred based on the bill as enacted during the last 2 quarters of the
2 preceding fiscal year, and a description of how the funds were expended; and
3 (iii) a comparison to the projected costs of the bill as projected in the fiscal note for that same
4 period of time.
5 (2)(3) The report to the legislature must include bills that qualify under subsection (1) for which a
6 fiscal note was prepared which have been in effect for at least 1 fiscal year. The bill must be included in the
7 report for each year 6-month period YEAR a cost was projected in a corresponding fiscal note.
8 (3)(4) The report must be provided in an electronic format.
9 (4)(5) The report must also be submitted to the legislature as provided in 5-11-210. (Terminates
10 December 31, 2025--sec. 6, Ch. 350, L. 2021.)"
11
12 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Repealer. Section 6, Chapter 350, Laws of 2021, is repealed.
13
14 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Effective date. [This act] is effective June 30, 2025.
15 - END -
