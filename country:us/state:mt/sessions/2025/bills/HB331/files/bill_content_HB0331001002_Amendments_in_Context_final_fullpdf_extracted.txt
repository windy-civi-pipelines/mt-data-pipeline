11 Section 1. Section 16-12-102, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "16-12-102. Definitions. As used in this chapter, the following definitions apply:
13 (1) "Adult-use dispensary" means a licensed premises from which a person licensed by the
14 department may:
15 (a) obtain marijuana or marijuana products from a licensed cultivator, manufacturer, dispensary, or
16 other licensee approved under this chapter; and
17 (b) sell marijuana or marijuana products to registered cardholders, adults that are 21 years of age
18 or older, or both.
19 (2) (a) "Advertise" or "advertising" means the publication, dissemination, solicitation, or circulation
20 of visual, oral, or written communication to directly induce a person to purchase or consume marijuana or
21 marijuana products.
22 (b) The term does not include branding on packaging and labeling of marijuana and marijuana
23 products for sale to registered cardholders or consumers.
24 (2)(3) "Affiliate" means a person that directly, or indirectly through one or more intermediaries,
25 controls or is controlled by, or is under common control with, another person.
26 (3)(4) "Beneficial owner of", "beneficial ownership of", or "beneficially owns an" is determined in
27 accordance with section 13(d) of the federal Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, as amended.

17 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Limitations on advertising. (1) A licensee may not promote their
18 business or market their brand or products.
19 (2) A licensee may use the terms "marijuana" and "cannabis" in its signage and on its website.
20 (3) A licensee's outdoor signage may not use colloquial terms for marijuana or marijuana products
21 and may not use an image or visual representation of usable marijuana, marijuana-infused products, marijuana
22 concentrates, marijuana paraphernalia, or an image that indicates the presence of a product, including but not
23 limited to smoke or edibles.
24 (4) A licensee's outdoor signage must comply with any applicable local jurisdiction sign ordinances
25 and regulations.

27 individuals visiting the website are 21 years of age or older, including the requirement to establish an account to
