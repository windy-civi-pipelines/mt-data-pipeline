2 INTRODUCED BY J. LYNCH, B. EDWARDS, J. ISALY, M. LEE, P. STRAND, J. SECKINGER, B. CLOSE, S.
3 FYANT, J. SOOKTIS, M. CUNNINGHAM, E. BUTTREY, E. MATTHEWS, T. RUNNING WOLF, F. SMITH, D.

5 MARLER, S. DEMAROIS
6
7 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE DATES TO APPLY FOR A SPEECH-LANGUAGE
8 PATHOLOGIST OR AUDIOLOGIST LICENSE; AMENDING SECTION 37-15-303, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN
9 IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 37-15-303, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "37-15-303. Qualifications. (1) To be eligible for licensing by the board as a speech-language
15 pathologist or audiologist, the applicant:
16 (a) must meet the current academic, supervised clinical practicum, and supervised professional
17 experience requirements as defined by board rule; and
18 (b) shall pass an examination approved by the board.
19 (2) (a) To be eligible for licensing by the board as a speech-language pathology assistant or an
20 audiology assistant, the applicant:
21 (i) must have completed the academic requirements as defined by board rule; or
22 (ii) shall provide evidence to the board that the applicant served as an unlicensed speech-
23 language pathology assistant or an unlicensed audiology assistant sufficient to meet the requirements in
24 subsection (2)(a)(i) for a period prior to January 1, 2024 July 15, 2025.
25 (b) The board shall provide by rule the type of evidence and conditions to meet equivalency for
26 current academic experience.
27 (c) Licensure for speech-language pathology assistants qualifying under subsection (2)(a)(ii) must
28 be retroactive to the date in which the board rules establishing equivalency were satisfied.

1 (3) The standards defined by the board must be equal to or greater than the standards generally
2 accepted as the national norm."

4 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
