69th Legislature 2025 HB0266.2
2 INTRODUCED BY L. MUSZKIEWICZ, J. ISALY, P. STRAND, P. ELVERUM, M. ROMANO, M. THANE
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE ANNUAL INFLATION-RELATED ADJUSTMENTS
5 TO K-12 BASE AID TO IMPROVE ALIGNMENT WITH THE DEFINITION OF "BASIC SYSTEM OF FREE
6 QUALITY PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS" PROVIDED IN SECTION 20-9-309, MCA;
7 ENSURING THAT THE GENERAL FUND FUNDING FORMULA IS SELF-EXECUTING AND INCLUDES A
8 MECHANISM FOR ACCURATE ANNUAL INFLATIONARY ADJUSTMENTS; PROVIDING THAT THE
9 PRESENT LAW BASE MAY BE INCREASED BY A CALCULATED AMOUNT BY MOTION OF TWO
10 SPECIFIC INTERIM COMMITTEES; ESTABLISHING REPORTING REQUIREMENTS; AMENDING SECTION
11 20-9-326, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.”
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
14
15 Section 1. Section 20-9-326, MCA, is amended to read:
16 "20-9-326. Annual inflation-related adjustments to basic entitlements and per-ANB entitlements
17 -- reporting. (1) In preparing and submitting an agency budget pursuant to 17-7-111 and 17-7-112, the
18 superintendent of public instruction shall determine the inflation factor for the basic and per-ANB entitlements,
19 the data-for-achievement payment, the per-ANB amount used to calculate the total special education allocation
20 in 20-9-306, and the general fund payments in 20-9-327 through 20-9-330 in each fiscal year of the ensuing
21 biennium. The inflation factor is calculated as follows:
22 (a) for the first year of the biennium, divide the consumer price index for July 1 of the prior calendar
23 year by the consumer price index for July 1 of the calendar year 3 years prior to the prior calendar year and
24 raise the resulting ratio to the power of one-third; and
25 (b) for the second year of the biennium, divide the consumer price index for July 1 of the current
26 calendar year by the consumer price index for July 1 of the calendar year 3 years prior to the current calendar
27 year and raise the resulting ratio to the power of one-third.
28 (2) (a) The present law base for the entitlements referenced in subsection (1) of this section,

- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0266.2
1 calculated under Title 17, chapter 7, part 1, must consist of any enrollment increases or decreases plus the
2 inflation factor calculated pursuant to this section, not to exceed 3% in each year, applied to both years of the
3 biennium plus, in the second year ofthe applicable bienniums AND CONTINGENT ON MOTION OF THE TWO INTERIM
4 COMMITTEES LISTED IN SUBSECTION (3) OF THIS SECTION, the inflation reconciliation factor AMOUNT calculated
5 under subsection (3) of this section THAT MAY BE INCLUDED IN THE PRESENT LAW BASE.
6 (b) This subsection (2) does not limit the superintendent or the governor from recommending, or
7 limit the legislature from adopting, inflationary adjustments other than those calculated in this section.
8 (3) Beginning in fiscal year 2026 and then every fourth fiscal year thereafter, the superintendent of
9 public instruction shall provide a report no later than September 1 to the education interim budget committee
10 and the education interim committee in accordance with 5-11-210 regarding the educational relevance in the
11 implementation of this section. The report must include:
12 (a) the increase in funding over the prior year on a per-ANB basis for the BASE aid elements
13 represented in 20-9-306(2)(a) and (2)(c) through (2)(h) budgeted to be provided to school districts in the current
14 fiscal year and provided to school districts in the preceding 5 fiscal years, which must be reported in both dollar
15 and percentage amounts, with the six percentages rounded to the nearest one hundredth;
16 (b) the percentage change from July of the preceding year to July of the applicable year in the
17 consumer price index for the current year and the preceding 5 years, with the six percentages rounded to the
18 nearest one hundredth; and
19 (c) a cumulative numerical comparison of the difference between the percentages reported in
20 subsections (3)(a) and (3)(b), rounded to the nearest one hundredth. If the sum of the percentages reported in
21 subsection (3)(a) is less than the sum of the percentages reported in subsection (3)(b), the difference is the
22 inflation reconciliation factor that must MAY be incorporated in the next calculation of present law base for the
23 entitlements measured in subsection (3)(a) for the second year of the biennium to which the present law applies
24 IF INCLUSION OF THE AMOUNT IN THE PRESENT LAW BASE IS ADOPTED BY MOTION OF BOTH INTERIM COMMITTEES
25 PRESENTED WITH THE CALCULATION THROUGH THE PROCESS OUTLINED IN SUBSECTION (2).
26 (3)(4) For the purposes of this section, "consumer price index" means the consumer price index, U.S.
27 city average, all urban consumers, for all items, using the 1982-84 base of 100, as published by the bureau of
28 labor statistics of the U.S. department of labor."
