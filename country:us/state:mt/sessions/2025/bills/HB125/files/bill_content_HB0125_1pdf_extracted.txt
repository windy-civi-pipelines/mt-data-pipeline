Section 1:
****
69th Legislature 2025 HB 125.1
2 INTRODUCED BY D. BEDEY, M. BERTOGLIO, W. MCKAMEY, J. DARLING
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING ELECTION JUDGE QUALIFICATIONS FOR SCHOOL
5 DISTRICT ELECTIONS; ALLOWING QUALIFIED REGISTERED ELECTORS IN THE COUNTY WHERE THE
6 ELECTION IS BEING HELD TO SERVE AS ELECTION JUDGES; AMENDING SECTION 20-20-109, MCA;
7 AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 20-20-109, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "20-20-109. Election judges -- qualifications -- training. (1) Election (a) Except as provided in
13 subsection (1)(b), election judges must be qualified registered electors of the school district in which they serve.
14 (b) If a county election administrator agrees to conduct an election pursuant to 20-20-417, election
15 judges must be qualified registered electors in the county in which they serve.
16 (2) An election judge may not be:
17 (a) the candidate;
18 (b) an ascendant, descendant, brother, or sister of a candidate; or
19 (c) the spouse of the candidate or of any of the individuals listed in subsection (2)(b).
20 (3) School election judges must meet the training and certification requirements of 13-4-203."
21
22 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Effective date. [This act] is effective on passage and approval.
23 - END -
