69th Legislature 2025 LC 1641
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
(Primary Sponsor)
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE PROSECUTION OF STATE CRIMES
5 AGAINST PERSONS THAT ARE SUBJECT TO FEDERAL IMMIGRATION DETENTION.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Criminal prosecution of persons subject to federal immigration
10 detention. (1) The state may initiate a criminal prosecution against a person who is subject or may be subject
11 to federal detention for federal immigration violations for a violation of criminal law committed in this state.
12 (2) If a criminal prosecution has been instituted against a person under the laws of this state and is
13 still pending, the state may surrender the person to the federal government on demand for federal immigration
14 violations or hold the person until the person has been tried and discharged or convicted and punished in this
15 state.
16
17 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
18 integral part of Title 46, chapter 1, part 1, and the provisions of Title 46, chapter 1, part 1, apply to [section 1].
19 - END -
