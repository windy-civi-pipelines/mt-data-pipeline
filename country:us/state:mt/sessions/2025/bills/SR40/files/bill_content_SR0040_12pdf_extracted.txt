Section 1:
69th Legislature SR 40.1
1 SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 40
2 INTRODUCED BY R. TEMPEL
3
5 CONSENTING TO THE APPOINTMENTS TO THE TRANSPORTATION COMMISSION MADE BY THE
6 GOVERNOR AND SUBMITTED BY WRITTEN COMMUNICATION DATED JANUARY 16, 2025, TO THE
7 SENATE.
8
9 WHEREAS, the Governor of the State of Montana has made the appointments, below designated, that
10 have been submitted to the Senate by the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-302, MCA:
11 As members of the Transportation Commission, in accordance with section 2-15-2502, MCA:
12 Scott Aspenlieder, Billings, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
13 Bob Cloninger, Helena, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
14 Loran Frazier, Great Falls, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
15 Shane Sanders, Bozeman, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 2, 2029.
16 Noel Sansaver, Wolf Point, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
17 Kody Swartz, Missoula, Montana, appointed to serve a term ending January 1, 2027.
18
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
20 That the Senate of the Regular Session of the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana does hereby
21 concur in, confirm, and consent to the above appointments and that the Secretary of the Senate immediately
22 deliver a copy of this resolution to the Secretary of State and to the Governor pursuant to section 5-5-303,
23 MCA.
24 - END -
