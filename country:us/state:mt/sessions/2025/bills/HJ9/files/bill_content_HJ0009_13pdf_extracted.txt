1 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. 9
3 ALBUS, K. LOVE, G. OVERSTREET, T. SHARP, C. SCHOMER, C. COCHRAN, M. THIEL, E. TILLEMAN, D.
4 BEDEY, M. BERTOGLIO, A. REGIER, C. SPRUNGER, G. LAMMERS, S. FITZPATRICK, B. LER, R. MINER,
5 J. ETCHART, B. BARKER, L. BREWSTER, J. FITZPATRICK, G. KMETZ, B. MITCHELL, G. NIKOLAKAKOS,
6 Z. WIRTH, K. BOGNER, V. MOORE, L. BENNETT, E. STAFMAN
7
8 A JOINT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
9 MONTANA SUPPORTING ISRAEL'S RIGHT TO EXIST; CONDEMNING ALL FORMS OF ANTISEMITISM;
10 AND CONDEMNING TERRORIST ATTACKS ON THE PEOPLE OF ISRAEL.
11
12 WHEREAS, throughout history and across the reign of multiple kingdoms, the Jewish people were
13 persecuted and expelled from the Land of Israel and forced to live as minority diaspora communities in other
14 lands; and
15 WHEREAS, Jewish diaspora communities were historically violently persecuted in, and in some cases
16 expelled from, other countries throughout the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Asia due to their religion; and
17 WHEREAS, the Nazis attempted to annihilate the entire Jewish population of Europe during the
18 Holocaust, murdering 6 million Jewish people during this time; and
19 WHEREAS, this genocide provided new urgency to reestablish a Jewish homeland for the Jewish
20 people following the Holocaust where they would not be a vulnerable minority, where they could freely practice
21 their faith, and where something like the Holocaust could never happen again; and
22 WHEREAS, the modern State of Israel was established on May 14, 1948; and
23 WHEREAS, even after the establishment of the State of Israel, other countries and terrorist entities
24 continued to attack Israel, reject its right to exist, and call for its destruction.
25
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
27 THE STATE OF MONTANA:
28 (1) That the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana condemns all forms of antisemitism.
- 1 - Authorized Print Version – HJ 9
69th Legislature 2025 HJ 9.1
1 (2) That the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana condemns all terrorist attacks on the people
2 of Israel and the State of Israel, including all terrorist attacks from Hamas, Hezbollah, and the nation of Iran.
3 (3) That the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana reaffirms the State of Israel's right to exist.
4 (4) That the 69th Legislature of the State of Montana rejects all calls for Israel's destruction and
5 elimination.
6 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Secretary of State send a copy of this resolution to President
7 Donald J. Trump and the Montana Congressional Delegation.
8 - END -
