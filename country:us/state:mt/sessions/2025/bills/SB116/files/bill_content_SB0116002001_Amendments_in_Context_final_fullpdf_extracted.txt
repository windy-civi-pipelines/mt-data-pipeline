10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
11
12 Section 1. Section 22-3-1002, MCA, is amended to read:
13 "22-3-1002. Montana heritage preservation and development commission. (1) There is a
14 Montana heritage preservation and development commission. The commission is attached to the department of
15 commerce for administrative purposes only, pursuant to 2-15-121. The commission and the department shall
16 negotiate a specific indirect administrative rate annually. The rate determined by the commission and the
17 department must be reported to the economic affairs interim committee in accordance with 5-11-210.
18 (2) (a) The commission consists of 149 14 members. The members shall must broadly represent
19 the state. Nine Four TWO Nine members must be appointed by the governor, one member TWO MEMBERS one
20 member must be appointed by the president of the senate, and one member TWO MEMBERS one member must
21 be appointed by the speaker of the house.

23 which they are responsible within 6 2 months of a vacancy having occurred in those positions, the members
24 must be appointed by the governor.
25 (c) The director of the Montana historical society, the director of the department of fish, wildlife,
26 and parks, and the director of the department of commerce shall serve as members.
