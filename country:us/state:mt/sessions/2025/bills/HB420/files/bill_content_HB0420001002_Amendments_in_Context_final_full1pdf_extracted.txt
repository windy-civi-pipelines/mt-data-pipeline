4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING TAX LIEN AND TAX DEED LAWS;
5 REVISING THE TIME FOR REDEMPTION BY AN INTERESTED PARTY; PROVIDING THAT A TAX DEED
6 MAY NOT BE ISSUED UNTIL A DELINQUENCY EXCEEDS CERTAIN EQUITY THRESHOLDS; PROVIDING
7 FOR ADDITIONAL NOTICES TO INTERESTED PARTIES; REQUIRING ONLINE BIDDING IN REAL TIME;
8 PROVIDING THAT ALL PROPERTY IS SUBJECT TO A TAX DEED AUCTION; CLARIFYING TIMELINES
9 FOR NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS; PROVIDING FOR THE AUTOMATIC QUIETING OF TITLE FOR
10 PROPERTY SOLD PURSUANT TO A TAX DEED AUCTION; PROVIDING FOR DISTRIBUTION OF
11 SURPLUS FUNDS AS UNCLAIMED PROPERTY; REVISING PROPERTY TYPES THAT ARE SUBJECT TO
12 INCREASED PROTECTIONS; AMENDING SECTIONS 15-16-101, 15-17-121, 15-17-122, 15-18-111, 15-18-
13 112, 15-18-211, 15-18-212, 15-18-213, 15-18-214, 15-18-215, 15-18-216, 15-18-217, 15-18-219, 15-18-220,
14 AND 15-18-221, AND 15-18-225, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE AND AN
15 APPLICABILITY DATE.”
