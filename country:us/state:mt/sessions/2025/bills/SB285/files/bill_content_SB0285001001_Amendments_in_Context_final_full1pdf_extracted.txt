4 NEW SECTION. Section 2. The Department of Public Health and Human Services shall amend ARM
5 37.96.101 to read:
6 "37.96.101 GENERAL DEFINITIONS (1) "Applicant" means an individual or legal entity who has made
7 a formal request for a child care license or registration.
8 (2) "Capacity" means the maximum number of children a facility is authorized by the department to
9 have in care at any given time.
10 (3) "Child" is defined has the meaning provided in 52-2-703, MCA, and means:
11 (a) a person under 13 years old; or
12 (b) a person with special needs, as defined by the department, who is under 18 years old or is 18 years
13 old and a full-time student expected to complete an educational program by 19 years old.
14 (4) "Child care" has the same meaning as "day care," as defined in 52-2-703, MCA, and means care for
15 children provided by an adult, other than a parent of the children or other person living with the children as a
16 parent, on a regular basis for daily periods of less than 24 hours, whether that care is for daytime or nighttime
17 hours. The term does not include:
18 (a) school-age care, unless the provider opts to be licensed or registered;
19 (b) informal short term, drop-in care for children provided on an unscheduled or on-demand basis while
20 parents attend discrete activities such as meetings, appointments, or leisure activities, unless the provider opts
21 to be licensed or registered;
22 (c) recreational, creative, educational, or religious programs and camps operating at specific and
23 limited times of the year;
24 (d) programs established chiefly for educational purposes that limit their services to children who are
25 three years old or older, unless the provider opts to be licensed or registered;
26 (e) relative care, unless they opt to be registered; or
27 (f) Family, Friend, and Neighbor care, unless they opt to be registered.
