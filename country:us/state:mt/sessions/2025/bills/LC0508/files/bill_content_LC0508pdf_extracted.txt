69th Legislature 2025 LC0508.1
2 INTRODUCED BY _________________________________________________
3 BY REQUEST OF THE DEPARTMENT OF FISH, WILDLIFE, AND PARKS
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS RELATED TO CLASS D-5 LICENSES FOR
6 TRAINING BIRD HUNTING DOGS; REQUIRING AN APPLICANT FOR A CLASS D-5 LICENSE TO REPORT
7 THE NUMBER OF DOGS THAT WILL BE TRAINED; ALLOWING THE FISH AND WILDLIFE COMMISSION
8 TO REGULATE THE NUMBER OF CLASS D-5 LICENSES; PROVIDING RULEMAKING AUTHORITY; AND
9 AMENDING SECTION 87-3-602, MCA.”
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
12
13 Section 1. Section 87-3-602, MCA, is amended to read:
14 "87-3-602. Training bird hunting dogs -- Class D-5 license -- rulemaking. (1) (a) Training of bird
15 hunting dogs using game birds not raised in captivity is allowed with the purchase of a Class D-5 license and
16 pursuant to the requirements of this part.
17 (b) A Class D-5 license is required for training bird hunting dogs using game birds not raised in
18 captivity. A person who is at least 18 years of age or older or who will turn 18 years of age during the season
19 for which the license is issued may purchase a license for a fee of $5 for residents and $10 for nonresidents.
20 The department may issue a free resident or nonresident Class D-5 license to a youth who is 12 to 18 years of
21 age.
22 (c) An applicant for a Class D-5 license shall report on the application the number of dogs to be
23 trained. The number of dogs must be printed on the license issued by the department.
24 (2) A Class D-5 license is not required for a person training bird hunting dogs with a method that
25 kills game birds raised in captivity. A person shall tag or mark the captive game bird prior to release. Captive
26 game birds must be obtained from a game bird farm licensed under 87-4-903 or from a source of game birds
27 approved by the department.
28 (3) A person who takes an untagged or unmarked game bird while training a bird hunting dog
