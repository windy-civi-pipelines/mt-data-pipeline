17 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Board of facility health care professionals. (1) In accordance with 37-
18 1-123, there is a board of facility health care professionals.
19 (2) The board consists of SEVEN members:
20 (a) two clinical laboratory scientists;
21 (b) two radiologic technologists;
22 (c) two respiratory therapists; and
23 (d) one public member.
24 (3) The board is allocated to the department as prescribed in 2-15-121.
