(Primary Sponsor)
3 BY REQUEST OF THE STATE ADMINISTRATION AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS INTERIM COMMITTEE
4
5 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING THE RETIREMENT CRITERIA FOR THE HIGHWAY
6 PATROL OFFICERS' RETIREMENT SYSTEM AND THE SHERIFFS' RETIREMENT SYSTEM; AMENDING
7 SECTIONS 19-6-501 AND 19-7-501, MCA; AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE DATES.”
8
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
10
11 Section 1. Section 19-6-501, MCA, is amended to read:
12 "19-6-501. Eligibility for service retirement benefit. (1) (a) Subject to a member's right to a refund
13 of the member's accumulated contributions under Title 19, chapter 2, part 6, a member hired on or before June
14 30, 2023, is eligible to receive a nonforfeitable service retirement benefit under 19-6-502 after completing 20
15 years or more of membership service and terminating service.
16 (b) Subject to a member's right to a refund of the member's accumulated contributions under Title
17 19, chapter 2, part 6, a member hired on or after July 1, 2023, is eligible to receive a nonforfeitable service
18 retirement benefit under 19-6-502 after completing 20 years or more of membership service, reaching the age
19 of 50, and terminating service.
20 (2) For the purposes of compliance with section 411 of the Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 411,
21 a vested member is treated as having who has either reached age 50 or completed 20 years of membership
22 service, whichever occurs later, has attained normal retirement age and has a nonforfeitable right to the
23 member's service retirement."
24
25 Section 2. Section 19-7-501, MCA, is amended to read:
26 "19-7-501. Eligibility for service retirement benefit. (1) (a) Subject to a member's right to a refund
27 of the member's accumulated contributions under Title 19, chapter 2, part 6, a member hired on or before June
28 30, 2023, who has completed at least 20 years of membership service is eligible for a nonforfeitable service
