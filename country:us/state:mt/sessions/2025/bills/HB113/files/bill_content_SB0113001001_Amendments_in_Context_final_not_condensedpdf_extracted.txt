1 SENATE BILL NO. 113
2 INTRODUCED BY M. YAKAWICH
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING MOTOR VEHICLE TOWING AND STORAGE LIEN
5 EXEMPTIONS; SPECIFYING THE TYPES OF FOOD AND CONSUMER GOODS, THE TYPES OF
6 PERSONAL DOCUMENTS, AND THE TYPE OF RECORDS THAT ARE EXEMPT; AND AMENDING
7 SECTION 71-3-1201, MCA.”
8
9 WHEREAS, Montana allows for towing and storage liens to allow tow truck operators to render their
10 services effectively; and
11 WHEREAS, Montana law exempts certain personal property from towing and storage liens, typically
12 food and perishable goods belonging to the driver of the disabled vehicle, but there is a lack of clarity in the
13 current statute; and
14 WHEREAS, this act will clarify that the towing and storage lien exemptions only apply to items that are
15 consumer products and goods that aren't for resale, allowing Montana tow truck operators to effectively conduct
16 business in this state.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
19
20 Section 1. Section 71-3-1201, MCA, is amended to read:
21 "71-3-1201. Liens for service -- towing and storage lien -- extension of lien to certain personal
22 property contained in motor vehicle that is subject to lien -- nonpossessory special liens. (1) If there is
23 an express or implied contract for collecting, processing, packaging, or storing embryos or semen from
24 livestock, a reproductive technology business to whom embryos or semen is entrusted and who still has
25 possession has a lien upon the embryos or semen for the amount due for collecting, processing, packaging, or
26 storing the embryos or semen and may retain possession of the embryos or semen until the sum due is paid.
27 (2) (a) A person who, while lawfully in possession of an article of personal property, product, or

4 property involved unless, within 30 days from the time of the completion of the service, repair, or improvement
5 of the property, the person desiring to assert a special lien upon the property gives notice in writing to the
6 secured party or other lienholder stating the intention to assert a special lien on the property under the terms of

9 personal service or by mailing by registered or certified mail a copy of the notice to the secured party or other

11 notice in the post office."
