- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 HB0759.2
2 INTRODUCED BY R. GREGG, S. FITZPATRICK
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT GENERALLY REVISING CAMPAIGN FINANCE LAWS;
5 PROVIDING LIMITATIONS THAT ONLY A LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY AND PARTNERSHIP THAT IS
6 TAXED AS A SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP OR A PARTNERSHIP, OR A PARTNERSHIP TAXED AS A
7 PARTNERSHIP, MAY MAKE CONTRIBUTIONS TO A CANDIDATE AND THAT THE CONTRIBUTIONS
8 MUST BE REPORTED UNDER THE NAME OF THE MEMBER OR PARTNER MAKING THE
9 CONTRIBUTION; AND PROHIBITING AN INDIVIDUAL CONTRIBUTION SEPARATE FROM THE
10 CONTRIBUTION MADE BY THE MEMBER OR PARTNER OF THE LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY OR
11 PARTNERSHIP CLARIFYING CONTRIBUTION LIMITS.”
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Limitations on limited liability company and partnership
16 contributions to candidates -- reporting requirements. (1) A candidate may only accept a contribution from
17 a limited liability company or partnership if the limited liability company or partnership is classified and taxed as
18 a SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP OR A partnership, OR THE PARTNERSHIP IS TAXED AS A PARTNERSHIP, for federal tax
19 purposes. A candidate may not accept a contribution from a limited liability company or partnership that is taxed
20 as a C. corporation or an S. corporation for federal tax purposes.
21 (2) A contribution from a limited liability company or partnership taxed as a SOLE PROPRIETORSHIP
22 OR partnership, OR A PARTNERSHIP TAXED AS A PARTNERSHIP, for federal tax purposes must be reported under the
23 name of the member or partner making the contribution subject to the disclosure requirements under 13-37-
24 229.
25 (3) The member or partner reported as making a contribution to a candidate as a limited liability
26 company or partnership may not make a separate contribution as an individual and is subject to the INDIVIDUAL
27 contribution limitations under 13-37-219 13-37-216.
28
