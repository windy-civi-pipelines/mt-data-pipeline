8 NEW SECTION. Section 2. Coordination of fund sources for grants to political subdivisions
9 and local governments. A grant recipient listed under [section 1(4) and (7)] may not receive funds from both
10 the reclamation and development grants program and the renewable resource grant and loan program for the
11 same project during the same biennium.
12
13 NEW SECTION. Section 3. Condition of grants. The disbursement of funds under [section 1] is
14 subject to the following conditions that must be met by the grant recipient:
15 (1) The grant recipient must have a scope of work, schedule, and budget for the project that is
16 approved by the department of natural resources and conservation. Any changes in scope of work or budget
17 after legislative approval may not alter project goals and objectives. Changes in activities that would reduce the
18 public or natural resource benefits as presented in department of natural resources and conservation reports
19 and applicant testimony to the 69th legislature may result in a proportional reduction in the grant amount.
20 (2) The grant recipient must document that other matching funds required for completion of the
21 project are firmly committed.
22 (3) The grant recipient must have a project management plan that is approved by the department
23 of natural resources and conservation.
24 (4) The grant recipient shall comply with the auditing and reporting requirements provided for in 2-
25 7-503 and establish a financial accounting system that the department can reasonably ensure conforms to
26 generally accepted accounting principles. Tribal governments shall comply with the auditing and reporting
27 requirements provided for in 2 CFR 200.
