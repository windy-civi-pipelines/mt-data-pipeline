1 HOUSE BILL NO. 869
2 INTRODUCED BY M. ROMANO, J. KASSMIER, G. HUNTER, S. VINTON, M. YAKAWICH, V. RICCI, L.
3 SMITH, J. ETCHART, D. BAUM, B. CARTER, J. COHENOUR, M. CUNNINGHAM, N. DURAM, P. ELVERUM,
4 D. HAWK, S. KELLY, D. JOY, M. MARLER, E. MATTHEWS, R. MINER, B. MITCHELL, G. NIKOLAKAKOS, J.
5 REAVIS, T. RUNNING WOLF, P. STRAND, P. TUSS, J. WEBER
6
7 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT SUPPORTING THE PREVENTION OF CARDIAC ARREST IN
8 YOUTH ATHLETES; REQUIRING THE SUPERINTENDENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION TO DEVELOP
9 RESOURCES; REQUIRING COACHES TO UNDERGO TRAINING ON CARDIAC ARREST; REQUIRING
10 COACHES TO HOLD INFORMATIONAL MEETINGS ON CARDIAC ARREST; ALLOWING COACHES,
11 TRAINERS, AND OFFICIALS TO REMOVE A YOUTH ATHLETE FROM PARTICIPATION IF THE YOUTH
12 ATHLETE EXHIBITS WARNING SIGNS OF CARDIAC ARREST; REQUIRING A YOUTH ATHLETE
13 REMOVED FROM PARTICIPATION TO BE CLEARED BY A MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL PRIOR TO
14 RETURNING TO PLAY; CLARIFYING THAT THE PURCHASE OF AUTOMATIC EXTERNAL
15 DEFIBRILLATORS IS ALLOWED AS AN ENHANCEMENT TO SCHOOL AND STUDENT SAFETY AND
16 SECURITY AND IS THEREFORE AN ALLOWABLE EXPENDITURE FOR SCHOOL MAJOR MAINTENANCE
17 AID; CREATING A REIMBURSEMENT GRANT PROGRAM FOR THE COST OF AUTOMATIC EXTERNAL
18 DEFIBRILLATORS TO BE ADMINISTERED BY THE OFFICE OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION; PROVIDING A
19 DEFINITION; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION; AMENDING SECTIONS 20-9-236 AND 20-9-502, MCA;
20 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.”
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
23
24 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Cardiac arrest response for youth athletes -- purpose --
25 requirements of schools and superintendent of public instruction. (1) The purposes of this section are to:
26 (a) support, adopt, and implement a cardiac emergency response plan in schools that integrates
27 evidence-based core elements in accordance with the American heart association guidelines or other nationally
28 recognized, evidence-based standards;

3
4 NEW SECTION. Section 4. Appropriation. (1) There is appropriated $150,000 $240,000 from the
5 general fund to the office of public instruction for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, to develop the
6 information sheet described in [section 1] and support school districts in implementing the requirements of
7 [section 1].
8 (2) The office of public instruction shall administer a grant program to reimburse school districts for
9 the purchase and ongoing maintenance of automatic external defibrillators as part of the implementation of
10 [section 1]. The grant program shall provide a reimbursement of $1 for every $1 paid by a school district up to a
11 maximum state contribution of $700 per automatic external defibrillator.
12 (3) The office of public instruction may use up to 5% of the appropriation in this section for the
13 purpose of administering the reimbursement grant program.
14
15 NEW SECTION. Section 5. Codification instruction. [Section 1] is intended to be codified as an
16 integral part of Title 20, chapter 7, part 13, and the provisions of Title 20, chapter 7, part 13, apply to [section 1].
17
18 NEW SECTION. Section 6. Effective date. [This act] is effective July 1, 2025.
19 - END -
